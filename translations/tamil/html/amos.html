Am1.1 thekkoavaa oor meaypparukku'l iruntha aamoas, yoothaavin raajaavaagiya usiyaavin naadka'lilum, isravealin raajaavaagiya yoavaasudaiya kumaaranaagiya erobeyaamin naadka'lilum, boomi athirchchi u'ndaaga ira'ndu varushaththukku munnea, isravealaikku’riththuth tharisanangka'ndu sonna vaarththaiga'l.

Am1.2 karththar seeyoanilirunthu kerchchiththu, erusaleamilirunthu saththamiduvaar; athinaal meaypparin thaabarangga'l thukkangko'ndaadum; karmealin kodumudiyum kaaynthupoagum.

Am1.3 karththar sollugi’rathu ennaven’raal: thamaskuvinudaiya moon’ru paathagangga'linimiththamum, naalu paathagangga'linimiththamum, naan athin aakkinaiyaith thiruppamaattean; avarga'l keeleayaaththai iruppuk karuviga'linaal poaradiththaarga'lea.

Am1.4 aasakealin veettilea theekko'luththuvean; athu benaathaathin aramanaiga'laip padchikkum.

Am1.5 naan thamaskuvin thaazhppaa'lai udaiththu, kudiga'lai aavean ennum pa'l'laththaakkilum, senggoal seluththugi’ravanaip betheatheanilum iraathapadikkuch sanggaarampa'n'nuvean; appozhuthu seeriyaavin janangga'l keerukkuch si’raippattuppoavaarga'l en’ru karththar sollugi’raar.

Am1.6 karththar sollugi’rathu ennaven’raal: kaathsaavinudaiya moon’ru paathagangga'linimiththamum, naalu paathagangga'linimiththamum, naan athin aakkinaiyaith thiruppamaattean; avarga'l si’raippattavarga'lai eathoamiyaridaththil oppuvikkumpadi muzhuthum si’raiyaakkinaarga'lea.

Am1.7 kaathsaavin mathilukku'l theekko'luththuvean; athu athinudaiya aramanaiga'laip padchikkum.

Am1.8 naan kudiga'lai asthoaththilum, senggoal seluththugi’ravanai askaloanilum iraathapadikkuch sanggaarampa'n'ni, pelistharil meethiyaanavarga'l azhiyumpadikku en kaiyai ekroanukku viroathamaagath thiruppuvean en’ru karththaraagiya aa'ndavar sollugi’raar.

Am1.9 mealum: theeruvinudaiya moon’ru paathagangga'linimiththamum, naalu paathagangga'linimiththamum, naan athin aakkinaiyaith thiruppamaattean; avarga'l sagoathararin udanpadikkaiyai ninaiyaamal, si’raippattavarga'lai muzhuthum eathoamiyar kaiyil oppiththaarga'lea.

Am1.10 theeruvin mathilukku'l theekko'luththuvean; athu athin aramanaiga'laip padchikkum en’ru karththar sollugi’raar.

Am1.11 mealum: eathoamudaiya moon’ru paathagangga'linimiththamum, naalu paathagangga'linimiththamum, naan avan aakkinaiyaith thiruppamaattean; avan than sagoatharanaip pattayaththoadea thodarnthu, than manathai irakkamat’rathaakki, than koabaththinaalea en’raikkum avanaip pee’rippoattu, than moorkkaththai niththiyakaalamaaga vaiththirukki’raanea.

Am1.12 theamaanilea theekko'luththuvean; athu boas’raavin aramanaiga'laip padchikkum en’ru karththar sollugi’raar.

Am1.13 karththar sollugi’rathu ennaven’raal: ammoan puththirarin moon’ru paathagangga'linimiththamum, naalu paathagangga'linimiththamum, naan avarga'l aakkinaiyaith thiruppamaattean; avarga'l thangga'l ellaiga'lai visthaaramaakkumpadikkuk keeleayaath theasaththin karppasthireega'laik kee’rippoattaarga'lea.

Am1.14 rabbaavin mathilukku'l theekko'luththuvean; athu yuththanaa'lin muzhakkamaagavum, perungkaat’rin pusalaagavum athin aramanaiga'laip padchikkum.

Am1.15 avarga'ludaiya raajaavum, avanudaiya athibathiga'lum si’raippattuppoavaarga'l en’ru karththar sollugi’raar.

Am2.1 karththar sollugi’rathu ennaven’raal: moavaabin moon’ru paathagangga'linimiththamum, naalu paathagangga'linimiththamum, naan avan aakkinaiyaith thiruppamaattean; avan eathoamudaiya raajaavin elumbuga'lai nee’raagach suttuppoattaanea.

Am2.2 moavaabtheasaththil theekko'luththuvean; athu keeriyoaththin aramanaiga'laip padchikkum; moavaabiyar ama'liyoadum aarpparippoadum ekkaa'la saththaththoadum saavaarga'l.

Am2.3 niyaayaathibathiyai avarga'l naduvil iraathapadikku naan sanggaarampa'n'ni, avanoadeakooda avarga'ludaiya pirabukka'laiyellaam kon’rupoaduvean en’ru karththar sollugi’raar.

Am2.4 mealum: yoothaavin moon’ru paathagangga'linimiththamum, naalu paathagangga'linimiththamum, naan avarga'l aakkinaiyaith thiruppamaattean; avarga'l karththarudaiya veathaththai ve’ruththu, avarudaiya katta'laiga'laik kaikko'l'laamal, thangga'l pithaakka'l pinpat’rina poyga'linaal moasampoanaarga'lea.

Am2.5 yoothaavilea naan theekko'luththuvean; athu erusaleamin aramanaiga'laip padchikkum en’ru karththar sollugi’raar.

Am2.6 mealum: isravealin moon’ru paathagangga'linimiththamum, naalu paathagangga'linimiththamum, naan avarga'ludaiya aakkinaiyaith thiruppamaattean; avarga'l neethimaanaip pa'naththukkum, e'liyavanai oru joadu paatharadchaikkum vit’ruppoattaarga'lea.

Am2.7 avarga'l thariththirarudaiya thalaiyinmeal ma'n'naivaari i’raiththu, si’rumaiyaanavarga'lin vazhiyaip purattugi’raarga'l; en parisuththa naamaththaik kulaichchalaakkumpadikku maganum thagappanum oru pe'n'nidaththil piraveasikki’raarga'l.

Am2.8 avarga'l sagala peedangga'larugilum adaimaanamaay vaanggina vasthirangga'linmeal paduththukko'ndu, the'ndam pidikkappattavarga'ludaiya mathubaanaththaith thangga'l theavarga'lin koavililea kudikki’raarga'l en’ru karththar sollugi’raar.

Am2.9 naanoa: keathurumarangga'laippoal uyaramum, karvaali marangga'laippoal vairamumaayiruntha emoariyanai avarga'lukku munbaaga azhiththean; uyara iruntha avanudaiya kaniyaiyum, thaazha iruntha avanudaiya vearga'laiyum azhiththuppoattu,

Am2.10 neengga'l emoariyanudaiya theasaththaik kattikko'l'lumpadikku ungga'lai naan egipthutheasaththilirunthu varappa'n'ni, ungga'lai naa’rpathu varushamaaga vanaantharaththilea vazhinadaththi,

Am2.11 ungga'l kumaararil silaraith theerkkatharisiga'laagavum, ungga'l vaalibaril silarai nasareayaraagavum ezhumbappa'n'ninean; israveal puththirarea, ippadi naan seyyavillaiyaa en’ru karththar keadki’raar.

Am2.12 neengga'loa nasareayarukkuth thiraadcharasam kudikkak koduththu, theerkkatharisiga'lai noakki: neengga'l theerkkatharisanagnsollavea'ndaam en’ru ka’rpiththeerga'l.

Am2.13 ithoa, koathumaikkattuga'l ni’raibaaramaaga eat’rappatta va'ndiyil iruththugi’rathupoala, naan ungga'lai neengga'l irukki’ra sthalaththil iruththuvean.

Am2.14 appozhuthu veagamaanavan oadiyum pugalidamillai; balavaan than balaththinaal balappaduvathumillai; baraakkiramasaali than piraa'nanaith thappuvippathumillai.

Am2.15 villaip pidikki’ravan ni’rpathumillai; veagamaanavan than kaalga'laal thappippoavathumillai; kuthiraiyinmeal ea’rugi’ravan than piraa'nanai iradchippathumillai.

Am2.16 balasaaliga'lukku'l'lea thairiyavaan annaa'lilea nirvaa'niyaay oadippoavaan en’ru karththar sollugi’raar.

Am3.1 israveal puththirarea, karththaraagiya naan egipthutheasaththilirunthu varappa'n'nina muzhukkudumbamaagiya ungga'lukku viroathamaaych solliya intha vasanaththaik kea'lungga'l.

Am3.2 boomiyin ellaa vamsangga'lukku'l'lum ungga'laimaaththiram a’rinthuko'ndean; aagaiyaal ungga'ludaiya ellaa akkiramangga'linimiththamum ungga'laith tha'ndippean.

Am3.3 ira'ndupear orumanappattirunthaalozhiya orumiththu nadanthupoavaarga'loa?

Am3.4 thanakku irai agappadaamalirukkak kaattilea singgam kerchchikkumoa? iraipidiyaamal irukkumpoathu baalasinggam than kebiyilirunthu saththamidumoa?

Am3.5 kuruvikkuth tharaiyilea surukkup poadappadaathirunthaal, athu ka'n'niyil agappadumoa? on’rum padaathirukkaiyil, ka'n'ni tharaiyilirunthu edukkappadumoa?

Am3.6 ooril ekkaa'lam oothinaal, janangga'l kalanggaathiruppaarga'loa? karththarudaiya seyal illaamal ooril theenggu u'ndaagumoa?

Am3.7 karththaraagiya aa'ndavar theerkkatharisiga'laagiya thammudaiya oozhiyakkaararukkuth thamathu iragasiyaththai ve'lippaduththaamal oru kaariyaththaiyum seyyaar.

Am3.8 singgam kerchchikki’rathu, yaar bayappadaathiruppaan? karththaraagiya aa'ndavar peasugi’raar, yaar theerkkatharisanam sollaathiruppaan?

Am3.9 neengga'l samaariyaavin parvathangga'lil vanthu koodi, athin naduvil nadakki’ra periya kalagangga'laiyum atha’rku'l seyyappadugi’ra idukka'nga'laiyum paarungga'l en’ru asthoaththin aramanaiga'lmealum, egipthutheasaththin aramanaiga'lmealum koo’rungga'l.

Am3.10 avarga'l semmaiyaanathaich seyya a’riyaamal, thangga'l aramanaiga'lil kodumaiyaiyum ko'l'laiyaiyum kuviththukko'l'lugi’raarga'l en’ru karththar sollugi’raar.

Am3.11 aagaiyaal saththuru vanthu, theasaththaich soozhnthuko'ndu, un belaththai unnilirunthu at’ruppoagappa'n'nuvaan; appozhuthu un aramanaiga'l ko'l'laiyidappadum en’ru karththaraagiya aa'ndavar sollugi’raar.

Am3.12 mealum: oru meayppan ira'ndu kaalga'laiyaavathu oru kaathin thu'ndaiyaavathu singgaththin vaayilirunthu pidunggith thappuvikkumaappoala, samaariyaavil kudiyirukki’ra israveal puththirar oru padukkaiyin moolaiyilirunthum, oru magnchaththinmealirunthum thappuvikkappaduvaarga'l en’ru karththar sollugi’raar.

Am3.13 neengga'l keattu yaakkoabu vamsaththaarukku'l'lea saadchiyaaga a’rivikkavea'ndiyathu: seanaiga'lin theavanaayirukki’ra karththaraagiya aa'ndavar sollugi’rathu ennaven’raal,

Am3.14 naan isravealudaiya paathagangga'linimiththam avanai visaarikkum naa'lilea naan beththealin balipeedangga'lai visaarippean; balipeedaththin kombuga'l vettu'ndu tharaiyilea vizhum.

Am3.15 maarikaalaththu veettaiyum koadaikaalaththu veettaiyum azhippean; appozhuthu yaanaiththanthaththaal seyyappatta veeduga'l azhiyum; periya veeduga'lukkum mudivu varum en’ru karththar sollugi’raar.

Am4.1 samaariyaavin malaiga'lilu'l'la baasaanin maaduga'lea, neengga'l intha vaarththaiga'laik kea'lungga'l; thariththirarai odukki, e'liyavarga'lai no’rukki, avarga'ludaiya ejamaanga'lai noakki: naangga'l kudikkumpadik ko'nduvaarungga'l en’ru sollugi’reerga'l.

Am4.2 ithoa, karththaraagiya aa'ndavar ungga'laith thu’raduga'laalum, ungga'l pinsanthathiyai meenpidikki’ra thoo'ndilga'laalum izhuththukko'ndupoagum naadka'l varumen’ru avar thammudaiya parisuththaththaikko'ndu aa'naiyittaar.

Am4.3 appozhuthu neengga'l ovvoruvanum aramanaikkuch sumanthuko'ndupoavathai e’rinthuvittu, thanakku ethiraana thi’rappuga'lin vazhiyaayp pu’rappattuppoaveerga'l en’ru karththar sollugi’raar.

Am4.4 beththealukkup poayth thuroagam pa'n'nungga'l, kilgaalukkum poayth thuroagaththaip perugappa'n'ni, kaalaithoa’rum ungga'l baliga'laiyum, moon’raam varushaththilea ungga'l thasamapaagangga'laiyum seluththi,

Am4.5 pu'liththamaavu'l'la sthoaththirabaliyoadea thoobangkaatti, u’rchaaga baliga'laik koo’rith theriyappaduththungga'l; israveal puththirarea, ippadich seyvathea ungga'lukkup piriyam en’ru karththaraagiya aa'ndavar sollugi’raar.

Am4.6 aagaiyaal naan ungga'l patta'nangga'lil ellaam ungga'l palluga'lukku oayvaiyum, ungga'l sthalangga'lil ellaam appakku’raivaiyum katta'laiyittean; aagilum neengga'l ennidaththil thirumbaama’rpoaneerga'l en’ru karththar sollugi’raar.

Am4.7 ithuvumallaamal, a’ruppukkaalam vara innum moon’rumaatham irukkumpoathea mazhaiyai naan thaduththean, oru patta'naththinmeal mazhaipeyyavum oru patta'naththinmeal mazhai peyyaamalirukkavum pa'n'ninean; oru vayalinmeal mazhaipeythathu, mazhaipeyyaatha mat’ra vayal kaaynthu poayit’ru.

Am4.8 ira'ndu moon’ru patta'nangga'lin manushar tha'n'neer kudikka orea patta'naththukkup poay alainthum thaagantheerththukko'l'lavillai; aagilum neengga'l ennidaththil thirumbaama’rpoaneerga'l en’ru karththar sollugi’raar.

Am4.9 karukkaayinaalum vishappaniyinaalum ungga'lai tha'ndiththean; ungga'l soalaiga'lilum thiraadchaththoattangga'lilum aththimarangga'lilum olivamarangga'lilum miguthiyaanathaip pachchaippuzhu ariththuppoattathu; aagilum ennidaththil thirumbaama’rpoaneerga'l en’ru karththar sollugi’raar.

Am4.10 egipthilea u'ndaanatha’rku oththa ko'l'lainoayai ungga'lukku'l anuppinean; ungga'l vaalibaraip pattayaththaalea kon’rean; ungga'l kuthiraiga'lai azhiththuppoattean; ungga'l paa'layangga'lin naat’raththai ungga'l naasiga'lilum ea’rappa'n'ninean; aagilum neengga'l ennidaththil thirumbaama’rpoaneerga'l en’ru karththar sollugi’raar.

Am4.11 soathoamaiyum komoaraavaiyum theavan kavizhththuppoattathu poala, ungga'laik kavizhththuppoattean; neengga'l akkiniyinin’ru pa’rikkappatta ko'l'liyaippoala iruntheerga'l; aagilum neengga'l ennidaththil thirumbaama’rpoaneerga'l en’ru karththar sollugi’raar.

Am4.12 aagaiyaal isravealea, inthappiragaaramaaga unakkuch seyvean; isravealea, naan ippadi unakkuch seyyappoagi’rapadiyinaal un theavanaich santhikkumpadi aayaththappadu.

Am4.13 avar parvathangga'lai uruvaakkinavarum, kaat’raich sirushdiththavarum, manushanudaiya ninaivuga'l innathen’ru avanukku ve'lippaduththugi’ravarum, vidiya’rkaalaththai anthagaaramaakkugi’ravarum, boomiyinudaiya uyarntha sthaanangga'linmeal ulaavugi’ravarumaayirukki’raar; seanaiga'lin theavanaagiya karththar enbathu avarudaiya naamam.

Am5.1 israveal vamsaththaarea, ungga'laikku’riththu naan pulambich sollum intha vasanaththaik kea'lungga'l.

Am5.2 israveal ennum kannigai vizhunthaa'l, ava'l ini orupoathum ezhunthirukkamaattaa'l; than theasaththil vizhunthukidakki’raa'l, ava'lai eduppaarillai.

Am5.3 nagaraththilirunthu pu’rappatta aayirampearil noo’rupearum, noo’rupearil paththuppearum israveal vamsaththaarukku meenthiruppaarga'l en’ru karththaraagiya theavan sollugi’raar.

Am5.4 karththar israveal vamsaththaarukkuch sollugi’rathu ennaven’raal: ennaith theadungga'l, appozhuthu pizhaippeerga'l.

Am5.5 beththealaith theadaatheayungga'l, kilgaalilum searaatheayungga'l, beyersebaavukkum poagaatheayungga'l; eanen’raal kilgaal si’raiyiruppaagavum, beththeal paazhaana sthalamaagavum poagum.

Am5.6 karththaraith theadungga'l, appozhuthu pizhaippeerga'l; illaavittaal beththealil irukki’ra oruvaraalum avikkappadaatha akkini yoaseappin veettil pat’ri, athaip padchikkum.

Am5.7 niyaayaththai ettiyaaga maat’ri, neethiyaith tharaiyilea vizhappa'n'nugi’ravarga'lea, avaraith theadungga'l.

Am5.8 avar a’rumeenaiyum mirugaseerishaththaiyum u'ndaakkinavar; avar mara'na iru'lai vidiya’rkaalamaaga maat’ri, pagalai iraaththiriyaaga anthagaarappaduththugi’ravar; avar samuththiraththin tha'n'neerga'lai varavazhaiththu, avaiga'laip boomiyin visaalaththinmeal oot’rugi’ravar; karththar enbathu avarudaiya naamam.

Am5.9 ara'naana sthalaththinmeal paazhkkadippu varaththakkathaaga, avar ko'l'lai koduththavanaip balaththavanukku viroathamaay laguvadaiyappa'n'nugi’ravar.

Am5.10 olimugavaasalilea kadinthuko'l'lugi’ravanai avarga'l pagaiththu, yathaarththamaayp peasugi’ravanai ve’rukki’raarga'l.

Am5.11 neengga'l thariththiranai mithiththu, avan kaiyilea thaaniyaththaich sumaisumaiyaay vaanggugi’rapadiyinaal, neengga'l po'lintha ka’rka'laal veeduga'laik kattineerga'l, aanaalum avaiga'lil neengga'l kudiyiruppathillai; inbamaana thiraadchaththoattangga'lai naattineerga'l, aanaalum avaiga'lin irasaththai neengga'l kudippathillai.

Am5.12 ungga'l mee’ruthalga'l miguthiyen’rum, ungga'l paavangga'l balaththathen’rum a’rivean; neethimaanai odukki, parithaanam vaanggi, olimugavaasalil eazhaiga'lin niyaayaththaip purattugi’reerga'l.

Am5.13 aagaiyaal buththimaan anthak kaalaththilea maunamaayirukkavea'ndum; anthak kaalam theemaiyaana kaalam.

Am5.14 neengga'l pizhaikkumpadikkuth theemaiyai alla, nanmaiyaith theadungga'l; appozhuthu neengga'l sollugi’rapadiyea seanaiga'lin theavanaagiya karththar ungga'loadea iruppaar.

Am5.15 neengga'l theemaiyai ve’ruththu, nanmaiyai virumbi, olimugavaasalil niyaayaththai nilaippaduththungga'l; oruvea'lai seanaiga'lin theavanaagiya karththar yoaseappilea meethiyaanavarga'lukku irangguvaar.

Am5.16 aathalaal aa'ndavarum seanaiga'lin theavanumaagiya karththar sollugi’rathu ennaven’raal: ellaaththerukka'lilum pulambal u'ndaagum; ellaa veethiga'lilum aiyoa! aiyoa! en’ru oalamiduvaarga'l; payiridugi’ravarga'laith thukkangko'ndaadugi’ratha’rkum, oppaaripaada a’rinthavarga'laip pulambugi’ratha’rkum varavazhaippaarga'l.

Am5.17 ellaath thiraadchaththoattangga'lilum pulambal u'ndaayirukkum, naan un naduvea kadanthupoavean en’ru karththar sollugi’raar.

Am5.18 karththarudaiya naa'lai virumbugi’ravarga'lukku aiyoa! athinaal ungga'lukku enna u'ndu? karththarudaiya naa'l ve'lichchamaayiraamal anthagaaramaayirukkum.

Am5.19 singgaththukkuth thappinavanukkuk karadi ethirppattathupoalavum, allathu veettukku'l'lea vanthu suvarinmeal than kaiyai vaiththapoathu paambu avanaik kadiththathupoalavum irukkum.

Am5.20 karththarudaiya naa'l ve'lichchamaayiraamal, iru'lum piragaasamat’ra anthagaaramumaayirukkumallavoa?

Am5.21 ungga'l pa'ndigaiga'laip pagaiththu ve’rukki’rean; ungga'l aasarippu naadka'lil enakkup piriyamillai.

Am5.22 ungga'l thaganabaliga'laiyum poajanabaliga'laiyum enakkup padaiththaalum naan anggeegarikkamaattean; kozhumaiyaana ungga'l mirugangga'lin sthoaththirabaliga'laiyum naan noakkip paarkkamaattean.

Am5.23 un paattuga'lin iraichchalai ennaivittu agat’ru; un vee'naiga'lin oasaiyai naan keadkamaattean.

Am5.24 niyaayam tha'n'neeraippoalavum, neethi vat’raatha nathiyaippoalavum pura'ndu varakkadavathu.

Am5.25 israveal vamsaththaarea, neengga'l vanaantharaththilea iruntha naa’rpathu varushamvaraiyil baliga'laiyum kaa'nikkaiga'laiyum enakkuch seluththineerga'loa?

Am5.26 neengga'l ungga'lukku u'ndaakkina moa'loakudaiya koodaaraththaiyum, ungga'l theavarga'lin nadchaththiraraasiyaagiya ungga'l soroobangga'lin sapparaththaiyum sumanthuko'nduvantheerga'lea.

Am5.27 aagaiyaal ungga'laith thamaskuvukku appaalea kudiyoattuvean en’ru seanaiga'ludaiya theavan ennum naamamu'l'la karththar sollugi’raar.

Am6.1 seeyoanilea nirvisaaramaayirukki’ravarga'lum samaariyaavin parvathaththai nambikko'ndirukki’ravarga'lum, jaathiga'lin thalaimaiyennappattu, israveal vamsaththaar theadivarugi’ravarga'lumaagiya ungga'lukku aiyoa!

Am6.2 neengga'l kalneamattum sen’ru, avvidaththilirunthu aamaath ennum periya patta'naththukkup poay, pelistharudaiya kaathpatta'naththukku i’ranggi, avaiga'l intha raajyangga'laippaarkkilum nallavaiga'loa en’rum, avaiga'lin ellaiga'l ungga'l ellaiga'laippaarkkilum visthaaramaanavaiga'loa en’rum paarungga'l.

Am6.3 theenggunaa'l thooramen’re'n'nik kodumaiyin aasanam kittivarumpadi seythu,

Am6.4 thanthak kattilga'lil paduththukko'ndu, thangga'l magnchangga'linmeal savukkiyamaaych sayaniththu, manthaiyilu'l'la aattukkuttiga'laiyum, maattuththozhuvaththilu'l'la kan’rukkuttiga'laiyum thin’ru,

Am6.5 thamburai vaasiththup paadi, thaaveethaippoal keethavaaththiyangga'laith thangga'lukku u'ndupa'n'ni,

Am6.6 periya paaththirangga'lil mathubaanaththaik kudiththu, si’rantha parima'lathailangga'laip poosikko'l'lugi’raarga'l; aanaalum yoaseappukku nearitta aabaththukkuk kavalaippadaama’rpoagi’raarga'l.

Am6.7 aagaiyaal avarga'l si’raiyiruppukkup poagi’ravarga'lin munna'niyilea poavaarga'l; ippadiyea selvamaaych sayaniththavarga'lin virunthuko'ndaadal nin’rupoagum.

Am6.8 naan yaakkoabudaiya meanmaiyai ve’ruththu, avanudaiya aramanaiga'laip pagaikki’rean; naan nagaraththaiyum athin ni’raivaiyum oppukkoduththuviduvean en’ru karththaraagiya aa'ndavar thammudaiya jeevanaikko'ndu aa'naiyittaar enbathaich seanaiga'lin theavanaagiya karththar sollugi’raar.

Am6.9 oru veettilea paththuppear meethiyaayirunthaalum avarga'l seththuppoavaarga'l.

Am6.10 avarga'ludaiya inaththaanaavathu, pireathaththaith thagikki’ravanaavathu elumbuga'lai veettilirunthu ve'liyea ko'ndupoagumpadikku, avaiga'lai eduththu, veettin udpu’raththilea irukki’ravanai noakki: unnidaththil innum yaaraayinum u'ndoa en’ru keadpaan, avan illaiyenbaan; appozhuthu ivan: nee maunamaayiru; karththarudaiya naamaththaich sollalaagaathu enbaan.

Am6.11 ithoa, karththar katta'laiyittirukki’raar; periya veettaith thi’rappuga'l u'ndaagavum, si’riya veettai vedippuga'l u'ndaagavum adippaar.

Am6.12 kanmalaiyinmeal kuthiraiga'l oadumoa? anggea oruvan maaduga'laal uzhuvaanoa? niyaayaththai nagnchaagavum, neethiyin kaniyai ettiyaagavum maat’rineerga'l.

Am6.13 naangga'l engga'l balaththinaalea engga'lukkuk kombuga'lai u'ndaakkikko'l'lavillaiyoa en’ru solli, vee'nkaariyaththil magizhugi’raarga'l.

Am6.14 israveal vamsaththaarea, ithoa, naan oru jaathiyai ungga'lukku viroathamaaga ezhuppuvean; avarga'l aamaaththukku'l piraveasikki’ra vazhithodanggich samanaana naattin aa’rumattaaga ungga'lai odukkuvaarga'l en’ru seanaiga'lin theavanaagiya karththar sollugi’raar.

Am7.1 karththaraagiya aa'ndavar enakkuk kaa'nbiththathaavathu: ithoa, raajaavinudaiya pulla’ruppukkuppinbu ira'ndaam kanthaayaththup pul mu'laikkath thodanggugaiyil avar vettukki'liga'lai u'ndaakkinaar.

Am7.2 avaiga'l theasaththin pullaiththin’ru theernthapoathu, naan: karththaraagiya aa'ndavarea, manniththaru'lum; yaakkoabu thirumba yaaraalea ezhunthiruppaan? avan si’ruththuppoanaan en’rean.

Am7.3 karththar atha’rku manasthaabappattu, appadi aavathillai en’raar.

Am7.4 karththaraagiya aa'ndavar enakkuk kaa'nbiththathaavathu: ithoa, akkiniyaalea niyaayam visaarippean en’ru karththaraagiya aa'ndavar ea’rpattaar; athu mahaa aazhiyaip padchiththathu, athil oru panggaip padchiththuth theernthathu.

Am7.5 appozhuthu naan: karththaraagiya aa'ndavarea, ni’ruththumea; yaakkoabu thirumba yaaraalea ezhunthiruppaan? avan si’ruththuppoanaan en’rean.

Am7.6 karththar atha’rku manasthaabappattu, appadi aavathillai en’raar.

Am7.7 pinbu avar enakkuk kaa'nbiththathaavathu: ithoa, thookkunool piramaa'naththinaal kattappatta oru mathilinmeal nin’raar; avar kaiyil thookkunool irunthathu.

Am7.8 karththar ennai noakki: aamoasea, nee ennaththaik kaa'ngi’raay en’raar; thookkunoolaik kaa'ngi’rean en’rean; appozhuthu aa'ndavar: ithoa, isravealennum en janaththin naduvea thookkunoolai viduvean; ini avarga'lai mannikkamaattean.

Am7.9 eesaakkin meadaiga'l paazhum, isravealin parisuththa sthalangga'l avaantharamumaakkappadum; naan erobeyaam veettaarukku viroathamaayp pattayaththoadea ezhumbivaruvean en’raar.

Am7.10 appozhuthu beththealil aasaariyanaana amathsiyaa isravealin raajaavaagiya erobeyaamukku aa'l anuppi: aamoas israveal vamsaththaarin naduvea umakku viroathamaagak kattuppaadu pa'n'nugi’raan; theasam avan vaarththaiga'laiyellaam sagikkamaattaathu.

Am7.11 erobeyaam pattayaththinaal saavaan en’rum, israveal than theasaththilirunthu si’raipidiththuk ko'ndupoagappaduvaan en’rum aamoas sollugi’raan en’ru sollachsonnaan.

Am7.12 amathsiyaa aamoasai noakki: tharisanam paarkki’ravanea, poa; nee yoothaatheasaththukku oadippoa, anggea appam thin’ru, anggea theerkkatharisanam sollu.

Am7.13 beththealilea inith theerkkatharisanam sollaathea; athu raajaavin parisuththa sthalamum raajyaththin aramanaiyumaayirukki’rathu en’raan.

Am7.14 aamoas amathsiyaavukkup pirathiyuththaramaaga: naan theerkkatharisiyumalla, theerkkatharisiyin puththiranumalla; naan manthai meaykki’ravanum, kaattaththippazhangga'laip po’rukkugi’ravanumaayirunthean.

Am7.15 aanaal manthaiyin pinnaalea poagi’rapoathu ennaik karththar azhaiththu, nee poay en janamaagiya isravealukku viroathamaagath theerkkatharisanam sollu en’ru karththar uraiththaar.

Am7.16 ippoathum nee karththarudaiya vaarththaiyaik kea'l; isravealukku viroathamaayth theerkkatharisanam sollaathea, eesaakkin vamsaththaarukku viroathamaaga on’raiyum sollaathea en’ru sollugi’raayea.

Am7.17 ithinimiththam: un pe'njaathi nagaraththil veasiyaavaa'l; un kumaararum un kumaaraththiga'lum pattayaththaal vizhuvaarga'l; un vayal a'lavunoolaal panggittukko'l'lappadum; neeyoavenil asuththamaana theasaththilea seththuppoavaay; isravealum than theasaththilirunthu si’raipidikkappattuk ko'ndupoagappaduvaan en’ru karththar sollugi’raar en’raan.

Am8.1 pinbu karththaraagiya aa'ndavar enakkuk kaa'nbiththathaavathu: ithoa, pazhuththa pazhangga'lu'l'la oru koodai irunthathu.

Am8.2 avar: aamoasea, nee ennaththaik kaa'ngi’raay en’ru keattaar? pazhuththa pazhangga'lu'l'la oru koodaiyaik kaa'ngi’rean en’rean. appozhuthu karththar ennai noakki: en janamaagiya isravealukku mudivukaalam vanthathu; ini avarga'lai mannikkamaattean.

Am8.3 annaa'lilea theavaalayappaattuga'l ala’ruthalaaga maa’rum; ellaa idaththilum thira'laana pireathangga'l pulambalillaamal e’rinthuvidappadum en’ru karththaraagiya aa'ndavar sollugi’raar.

Am8.4 theasaththil si’rumaippattavarga'lai ozhiyappa'n'na, e'liyavarga'lai vizhunggi:

Am8.5 naangga'l marakkaalaik ku’raiththu, seakkal ni’raiyai athigamaakki, ka'l'laththaraasinaal vagnchiththu, thariththiraraip pa'naththukkum, e'liyavarga'lai oru joadu paatharadchaikkum ko'l'lumpadikkum, thaaniyaththin patharai vi’rkumpadikkum,

Am8.6 naangga'l thavasam vi’rkaththakkathaaga maathappi’rappum, naangga'l thaaniyaththin pa'ndasaalaiga'laith thi’rakkaththakkathaaga oayvunaa'lum eppoathu mudiyum en’ru sollugi’ravarga'lea, ithaik kea'lungga'l.

Am8.7 avarga'l seygaiga'laiyellaam naan orupoathum ma’rappathillaiyen’ru karththar yaakkoabudaiya magimaiyinpearil aa'naiyittaar.

Am8.8 ithinimiththam theasam athiravum, athin kudiga'l ellaam thukkikkavum, enggum nathiga'laayp pura'ndoadavum, egipthin aat’ruve'l'laththaippoal adiththu, peruve'l'lamaagavum vea'ndaamoa?

Am8.9 annaa'lilea naan maththiyaanaththilea sooriyanai asthamikkappa'n'ni, pattappagalilea theasaththai anthagaarappaduththi,

Am8.10 ungga'l pa'ndigaiga'laith thukkippaagavum, ungga'l paattuga'laiyellaam pulambalaagavum maa’rappa'n'ni, sagala araiga'lilum irattaiyum, sagala thalaiga'lilum mottaiyaiyum varuviththu, avarga'ludaiya thukkippai orea pi'l'laikkaagath thukkikki’ra thukkippukkuch samaanamaakki, avarga'ludaiya mudivaik kasappaana naa'laakkuvean en’ru karththaraagiya aa'ndavar sollugi’raar.

Am8.11 ithoa, naan theasaththinmeal pagnchaththai anuppum naadka'l varum; aagaarak ku’raivinaal u'ndaagiya pagnchamalla, jalakku’raivinaal u'ndaagiya thaagamumalla, karththarudaiya vasanam keadkak kidaiyaatha pagnchaththai anuppuvean en’ru karththaraagiya aa'ndavar sollugi’raar.

Am8.12 appozhuthu avarga'l karththarudaiya vasanaththaith theada oru samuththiranthodanggi ma’ru samuththiramattum, vadathisaithodanggik keezhththisaimattum alainthu thirinthum athaik ka'ndadaiyaama’r poavaarga'l.

Am8.13 annaa'lilea saunthariyamu'l'la kannigaiga'lum vaalibarum thaagaththinaal soarnthupoavaarga'l.

Am8.14 thaa'nea! un theavanudaiya jeevanaikko'ndum, beyersebaa maarkkaththin theavanudaiya jeevanaikko'ndum en’ru solli, samaariyaavin thoashaththinmeal aa'naiyidugi’ravarga'l vizhuvaarga'l; ini orupoathum ezhunthirukkamaattaarga'l en’raar.

Am9.1 aa'ndavaraip balipeedaththinmeal ni’rkakka'ndean; avar: nee vaasal nilaiga'l asaiyumpadi poathigaiyai adiththu, avaiga'lai avarga'l ellaarudaiya thalaiyinmealum vizha udaiththuppoadu; avarga'lukkup pinnaagavarum meethiyaanavarga'lai naan pattayaththinaal kon’rupoaduvean; avarga'lil oadugi’ravan oruvanum thappuvathumillai, avarga'lil thappugi’ravan oruvanum iradchikkappaduvathumillai.

Am9.2 avarga'l paathaa'lapariyantham thoa'ndip pathunggikko'ndaalum, en kai avvidaththilirunthu avarga'laip pidiththukko'nduvarum; avarga'l vaanapariyantham ea’rinaalum, avvidaththilumirunthu avarga'lai i’ranggappa'n'nuvean;

Am9.3 avarga'l karmealin kodumudiyilea o'liththukko'ndaalum, anggea avarga'laith theadippidippean; avarga'l samuththiraththin aazhaththilea poay en ka'nga'lukku ma’rainthuko'ndaalum, anggea avarga'laik kadikkap paambuga'lukkuk katta'laiyiduvean.

Am9.4 avarga'l thangga'l saththuruvukku munbaagach si’raippattuppoanaalum, anggea avarga'laik kon’rupoadap pattayaththukku naan katta'laiyittu, en ka'nga'lai avarga'lmeal nanmaikkalla, theemaikken’rea vaippean.

Am9.5 seanaiga'lin karththaraagiya aa'ndavar theasaththaith thoda, athu urugippoam; appozhuthu athin kudiga'lellaarum pulambuvaarga'l; enggum nathiyaayp pura'ndoadi, egipthinudaiya aat’ru ve'l'laththaippoal ve'l'lamaagum.

Am9.6 avar vaanaththil thamathu meala’raiga'laik katti, boomiyil thamathu keezha’raiga'lai asthibaarappaduththi, samuththiraththin tha'n'neerga'lai varavazhaiththu, avaiga'laip boomiyinudaiya visaalaththinmeal oot’rugi’ravar; karththar enbathu avarudaiya naamam.

Am9.7 israveal puththirarea, neengga'l enakku eththiyoappiyarin puththiraraippoal irukki’reerga'l allavoa en’ru karththar sollugi’raar; naan isravealai egipthutheasaththilirunthum, pelistharaik kapthoarilirunthum, seeriyaraik keerilirunthum ko'nduvaravillaiyoa?

Am9.8 ithoa, karththaraagiya aa'ndavarin ka'nga'l paavamu'l'la raajyaththukku viroathamaaga vaikkappattirukki’rathu; athaip boomiyinmeal iraathapadikku azhiththuppoaduvean; aagilum yaakkoabin vamsaththai muzhuvathum azhikkamaattean en’ru karththar sollugi’raar.

Am9.9 ithoa, jalladaiyinaal saliththarikki’rathupoal israveal vamsaththaarai ellaa jaathiga'lukku'l'lum saliththarikkumpadikku naan katta'laiyiduvean; aanaalum oru koathumaima'niyum tharaiyilea vizhuvathillai.

Am9.10 theenggu engga'lai a'nuguvathumillai, engga'lukku neariduvathumillaiyen’ru en janaththil sollugi’ra paaviga'lellaarum pattayaththinaal saavaarga'l.

Am9.11 eathoamil meethiyaanavarga'laiyum, en naamam vi'langgiya sagala jaathiga'laiyum vasamaakkikko'l'lumpadikku,

Am9.12 annaa'lilea vizhunthupoana thaaveethin koodaaraththai naan thirumba eduppiththu, athin thi’rappuga'lai adaiththu, athil pazhuthaayppoanathaich seerppaduththi, poorvanaadka'lil irunthathupoala athai sthaabippean en’ru ithaich seygi’ra karththar sollugi’raar.

Am9.13 ithoa, uzhugi’ravan a’rukki’ravanaiyum, thiraadchappazhangga'lai aalaiyaadugi’ravan vithaikki’ravanaiyum thodarnthupidiththu, parvathangga'l thiraadcharasamaay vadigi’rathum, meaduga'lellaam karaigi’rathumaana naadka'l varum en’ru karththar sollugi’raar.

Am9.14 en janamaagiya isravealin si’raiyiruppaith thiruppuvean; avarga'l paazhaana nagarangga'laik katti, avaiga'lil kudiyirunthu, thiraadchaththoattangga'lai naatti, avaiga'ludaiya pazharasaththaik kudiththu, thoattangga'lai u'ndaakki, avaiga'lin kaniga'laip pusippaarga'l.

Am9.15 avarga'lai avarga'l theasaththilea naattuvean; naan avarga'lukkuk koduththa theasaththilirunthu avarga'l inip pidunggappaduvathillaiyen’ru un theavanaagiya karththar sollugi’raar en’raar.
