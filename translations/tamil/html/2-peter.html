2Pe1.1 nammudaiya theavanum iradchagarumaayirukki’ra iyeasuki’risthuvinudaiya neethiyaal engga'laippoala arumaiyaana visuvaasaththaip pet’ravarga'lukku, iyeasuki’risthuvin oozhiyakkaaranum appoasthalanumaagiya seemoan peathuru ezhuthugi’rathaavathu:

2Pe1.2 theavanaiyum nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuvaiyum a’rigi’ra a’rivinaal ungga'lukkuk kirubaiyum samaathaanamum perugakkadavathu.

2Pe1.3 thammudaiya magimaiyinaalum kaaru'niyaththinaalum nammai azhaiththavarai a’rigi’ra a’rivinaalea jeevanukkum theavabakthikkum vea'ndiya yaavat’raiyum, avarudaiya thivviya vallamaiyaanathu namakkuth thantharu'linathuman’ri,

2Pe1.4 ichchaiyinaal ulagaththilu'ndaana keattukkuth thappi, thivviya subaavaththukkup panggu'l'lavarga'laagumporuttu, mahaa meanmaiyum arumaiyumaana vaakkuththaththangga'lum avaiga'linaalea namakku a'likkappattirukki’rathu.

2Pe1.5 ippadiyirukka, neengga'l athiga jaakkirathaiyu'l'lavarga'laay ungga'l visuvaasaththoadea thairiyaththaiyum, thairiyaththoadea gnaanaththaiyum,

2Pe1.6 gnaanaththoadea ichchaiyadakkaththaiyum, ichchaiyadakkaththoadea po’rumaiyaiyum, po’rumaiyoadea theavabakthiyaiyum,

2Pe1.7 theavabakthiyoadea sagoathara sineagaththaiyum, sagoathara sineagaththoadea anbaiyum kootti vazhanggungga'l.

2Pe1.8 ivaiga'l ungga'lukku u'ndaayirunthu peruginaal, ungga'lai nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki’risthuvai a’rigi’ra a’rivilea vee'narum kaniyat’ravarga'lumaayirukkavottaathu.

2Pe1.9 ivaiga'l illaathavan evanoa, avan munseytha paavangga'la’rath thaan suththigarikkappattathai ma’ranthu ka'nsorugippoana kurudanaayirukki’raan.

2Pe1.10 aagaiyaal, sagoathararea, ungga'l azhaippaiyum therinthuko'l'luthalaiyum u’ruthiyaakkumpadi jaakkirathaiyaayirungga'l; ivaiga'laich seythaal neengga'l orukkaalum ida’rivizhuvathillai.

2Pe1.11 ivvithamaay, nammudaiya karththarum iradchagarumaagiya iyeasuki’risthuvinudaiya niththiya raajyaththukkudpadum piraveasam ungga'lukkup paripoora'namaay a'likkappadum.

2Pe1.12 ithinimiththam, ivaiga'lai neengga'l a’rinthum, neengga'l ippozhuthu a’rinthirukki’ra saththiyaththil u’ruthippattirunthum, ungga'lukku ivaiga'lai eppozhuthum ninaippootta naan asathiyaayirean.

2Pe1.13 nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki’risthu enakku a’riviththapadi naan en koodaaraththaivittuppoavathu seekkiraththil nearidumen’ru a’rinthu,

2Pe1.14 inthak koodaaraththil naan irukkuma'lavum ungga'lai ninaippootti ezhuppividuvathu niyaayamen’ru e'n'nugi’rean.

2Pe1.15 mealum, naan sen’rupoanapinbu ivaiga'lai neengga'l eppozhuthum ninaiththukko'l'la eathuvu'ndaayirukkumpadi pirayaththanampa'n'nuvean.

2Pe1.16 naangga'l thanthiramaana kattukkathaiga'laip pinpat’rinavarga'laaga alla, avarudaiya magaththuvaththaik ka'n'naarak ka'ndavarga'laagavea nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki’risthuvin vallamaiyaiyum varugaiyaiyum ungga'lukku a’riviththoam.

2Pe1.17 ivar ennudaiya neasakumaaran, ivaridaththil piriyamaayirukki’rean en’ru sollugi’ra saththam unnathamaana magimaiyilirunthu avarukku u'ndaagi, pithaavaagiya theavanaal avar kanaththaiyum magimaiyaiyum pet’rapoathu,

2Pe1.18 avaroadeakooda naangga'l parisuththa paruvathaththilirukkaiyil, vaanaththilirunthu pi’rantha anthach saththaththaikkeattoam.

2Pe1.19 athiga u’ruthiyaana theerkkatharisanavasanamum namakku u'ndu; pozhuthu vidinthu vidive'l'li ungga'l iruthayangga'lil uthikkuma'lavum iru'lu'l'la sthalaththil piragaasikki’ra vi'lakkaippoan’ra avvasanaththaik kavaniththiruppathu nalamaayirukkum.

2Pe1.20 veathaththilu'l'la enthath theerkkatharisanamum suyathoat’ramaana poru'laiyudaiyathaayiraathen’ru neengga'l munthi a’riyavea'ndiyathu.

2Pe1.21 theerkkatharisanamaanathu orukaalaththilum manusharudaiya siththaththinaalea u'ndaagavillai; theavanudaiya parisuththa manusharga'l parisuththa aaviyinaalea eavappattup peasinaarga'l.

2Pe2.1 ka'l'laththeerkkatharisiga'lum janangga'lukku'l'lea irunthaarga'l, appadiyea ungga'lukku'l'lum ka'l'lappoathagarga'l iruppaarga'l; avarga'l keattukkeathuvaana veathappurattuga'laith thanthiramaay nuzhaiyappa'n'ni, thangga'laik kirayaththukkukko'nda aa'ndavarai ma’ruthaliththu, thangga'lukkuth theeviramaana azhivai varuviththukko'l'luvaarga'l.

2Pe2.2 avarga'ludaiya ketta nadakkaiga'lai aneagar pinpat’ruvaarga'l; avarga'lnimiththam saththiyamaarkkam thooshikkappadum.

2Pe2.3 poru'laasaiyudaiyavarga'laay, thanthiramaana vaarththaiga'laal ungga'laith thangga'lukku aathaayamaaga vasappaduththikko'l'luvaarga'l; poorvakaalamuthal avarga'lukku vithikkappatta aakkinai ayarnthiraathu, avarga'ludaiya azhivu u’ranggaathu.

2Pe2.4 paavagnseytha thootharga'laith theavan thappavidaamal, anthagaarach sanggiliga'linaalea katti naragaththilea tha'l'li niyaayaththeerppukku vaikkappattavarga'laaga oppukkoduththu;

2Pe2.5 poorva ulagaththaiyum thappavidaamal, neethiyaip pirasanggiththavanaagiya noavaa muthalaana ettuppearaik kaappaat’ri, avabakthiyu'l'lavarga'l ni’raintha ulagaththinmeal jalappira'layaththai varappa'n'ni;

2Pe2.6 soathoam komoaraa ennum patta'nangga'laiyum saambalaakkik kavizhththuppoattu, aakkinaikku'l'laagath theerththu, pi’rkaalaththilea avabakthiyaay nadappavarga'lukku avaiga'laith thirushdaanthamaaga vaiththu;

2Pe2.7 akkiramakkaararukku'l vaasamaayirukkaiyil avarga'ludaiya kaamavigaara nadakkaiyaal varuththappattu;

2Pe2.8 naa'lthoa’rum avarga'ludaiya akkiramakkiriyaiga'laik ka'ndu keattu neethiyu'l'la thannudaiya iruthayaththil vaathikkappatta neethimaanaagiya loaththai avar iradchiththirukka;

2Pe2.9 karththar theavabakthiyu'l'lavarga'laich soathanaiyinin’ru iradchikkavum, akkiramakkaararai aakkinaikku'l'laanavarga'laaga niyaayaththeerppu naa'lukku vaikkavum a’rinthirukki’raar.

2Pe2.10 viseashamaaga asuththa ichchaiyoadea maamsaththi’rkeat’rapadi nadanthu, karththaththuvaththai asattaipa'n'nugi’ravarga'lai appadich seyvaar. ivarga'l thu'nigarakkaarar, aganggaariga'l, magaththuvangga'laith thooshikka agnchaathavarga'l.

2Pe2.11 athiga belanaiyum vallamaiyaiyumudaiya theavathootharga'l muthalaayk karththarukku munbaaga avarga'laith thoosha'namaayk kut’rappaduththamaattaarga'lea.

2Pe2.12 ivarga'loa pidipattazhikkappaduvatha’rku u'ndaana buththiyat’ra mirugajeevanga'laippoalath thangga'lukkuth theriyaathavaiga'laith thooshiththu, thangga'l keattilea kettazhinthu, aneethaththin palanai adaivaarga'l.

2Pe2.13 ivarga'l orunaa'l vaazhvai inbamen’re'n'ni, ka’raiga'lum ilachchaiga'lumaayirunthu; ungga'loadea virunthu'ngaiyil thangga'l vagnchanaiga'lil ullaasamaay vaazhgi’ravarga'l;

2Pe2.14 vibasaaramayakkaththaal ni’rainthavaiga'lum, paavaththai vittoayaathavaiga'lumaayirukki’ra ka'nga'laiyudaiyavarga'l; u’ruthiyillaatha aaththumaakka'laith thanthiramaayp pidiththu, poru'laasaiga'lil pazhagina iruthayaththaiyudaiya saabaththin pi'l'laiga'l.

2Pe2.15 semmaiyaana vazhiyaivittuth thappinadanthu, beayoarin kumaaranaagiya bileayaamin vazhiyaip pinpat’rippoanavarga'l; avan aneethaththin kooliyai virumbi,

2Pe2.16 thannudaiya akkiramaththinimiththam kadinthuko'l'lappattaan; peasaatha mirugam manusharpeachchaip peasith theerkkatharisiyinudaiya mathikeattaith thaduththathu.

2Pe2.17 ivarga'l tha'n'neerillaatha ki'na’ruga'lum, suzhalkaat’rinaal adiyu'ndoadugi’ra meagangga'lumaayirukki’raarga'l; en’ren’raikkumu'l'la kaariru'lea ivarga'lukku vaikkappattirukki’rathu.

2Pe2.18 vagnchagamaay nadakki’ravarga'lidaththilirunthu arithaayth thappinavarga'lidaththil ivarga'l aganthaiyaana vee'nvaarththaiga'laip peasi, maamsa ichchaiga'linaalum kaamavigaarangga'linaalum avarga'laith thanthiramaayp pidikki’raarga'l.

2Pe2.19 thaangga'lea keattukku adimaiga'laayirunthum, avarga'lukkuch suyaatheenaththai vaakkuththaththampa'n'nugi’raarga'l; ethinaal oruvan jeyikkappattirukki’raanoa atha’rku avan adimaippattirukki’raanea.

2Pe2.20 karththarum iradchagarumaayirukki’ra iyeasuki’risthuvai a’rigi’ra a’rivinaalea ulagaththin asuththangga'lukkuth thappinavarga'l ma’rupadiyum avaiga'lil sikkikko'ndu jeyikkappattaal, avarga'ludaiya pinnilaimai munnilaimaiyilum keadu'l'lathaayirukkum.

2Pe2.21 avarga'l neethiyin maarkkaththai a’rinthapinbu thangga'lukku oppuvikkappatta parisuththa ka’rpanaiyai vittu vilaguvathaippaarkkilum athai a’riyaathirunthaarga'laanaal avarga'lukku nalamaayirukkum.

2Pe2.22 naay thaan kakkinathaith thinnavum, kazhuvappatta pan’ri seat’rilea pura'lavum thirumbinathu en’ru sollappatta meyyaana pazhamozhiyinpadiyea avarga'lukkuch sambaviththathu.

2Pe3.1 piriyamaanavarga'lea, intha ira'ndaam nirubaththai ippozhuthu ungga'lukku ezhuthugi’rean.

2Pe3.2 parisuththa theerkkatharisiga'laal munsollappatta vaarththaiga'laiyum, iradchagaraayirukki’ra karththarudaiya appoasthalaraagiya engga'ludaiya katta'laiga'laiyum neengga'l ninaivukoorumpadi intha nirubangga'linaal ungga'l u'nmaiyaana manathai ninaippootti ezhuppugi’rean.

2Pe3.3 muthalaavathu neengga'l a’riyavea'ndiyathu ennavenil: kadaisinaadka'lil pariyaasakkaarar vanthu, thangga'l suya ichchaiga'linpadiyea nadanthu,

2Pe3.4 avar varuvaar en’ru sollugi’ra vaakkuththaththam enggea? pithaakka'l niththiraiyadainthapinbu sagalamum sirushdippinthoat’ramuthal irunthavithamaayirukki’rathea en’ru solluvaarga'l.

2Pe3.5 poorvakaalaththil theavanudaiya vaarththaiyinaalea vaanangga'lum, jalaththinin’ru thoan’ri jalaththinaalea nilaiko'ndirukki’ra boomiyum u'ndaayinavenbathaiyum,

2Pe3.6 appozhuthu iruntha ulagam jalappira'layaththinaalea azhinthathenbathaiyum manathaara a’riyaamalirukki’raarga'l.

2Pe3.7 ippozhuthu irukki’ra vaanangga'lum boomiyum antha vaarththaiyinaaleayea akkinikku iraiyaaga vaikkappattu, theavabakthiyillaathavarga'l niyaayantheerkkappattu azhinthupoagum naa'lvaraikkum kaakkappattirukki’rathu.

2Pe3.8 piriyamaanavarga'lea, karththarukku orunaa'l aayiram varushampoalavum, aayiram varusham orunaa'lpoalavum irukki’rathengi’ra intha oru kaariyaththai neengga'l a’riyaathirukkavea'ndaam.

2Pe3.9 thaamathikki’raar en’ru silar e'n'nugi’rapadi, karththar thamathu vaakkuththaththaththaikku’riththuth thaamathamaayiraamal; oruvarum kettuppoagaamal ellaarum mananthirumbavea'ndumen’ru virumbi, nammeal neediya po’rumaiyu'l'lavaraayirukki’raar.

2Pe3.10 karththarudaiya naa'l iravilea thirudan varugi’ravithamaay varum; appozhuthu vaanangga'l madamada en’ru agan’rupoam, poothangga'l venthu urugippoam, boomiyum athilu'l'la kiriyaiga'lum erinthu azhinthupoam.

2Pe3.11 ippadi ivaiga'lellaam azhinthu poagi’rathaayirukki’rapadiyaal neengga'l eppadippatta parisuththa nadakkaiyum theavabakthiyum u'l'lavarga'laayirukka vea'ndum!

2Pe3.12 theavanudaiya naa'l seekkiramaay varumpadikku miguntha aavaloadea kaaththirungga'l; antha naa'lil vaanangga'l venthu azhinthu, poothangga'l erinthu urugippoam.

2Pe3.13 avarudaiya vaakkuththaththaththinpadiyea neethi vaasamaayirukkum puthiya vaanangga'lum puthiya boomiyum u'ndaagumen’ru kaaththirukki’roam.

2Pe3.14 aagaiyaal, piriyamaanavarga'lea, ivaiga'l varak kaaththirukki’ra neengga'l ka’raiyat’ravarga'lum pizhaiyillaathavarga'lumaaych samaathaanaththoadea avar sannithiyil kaa'nappadumpadi jaakkirathaiyaayirungga'l.

2Pe3.15 mealum nammudaiya karththarin neediya po’rumaiyai iradchippen’ru e'n'nungga'l; namakkup piriyamaana sagoatharanaagiya pavulum thanakku aru'lappatta gnaanaththinaalea ippadiyea ungga'lukku ezhuthiyirukki’raan;

2Pe3.16 ellaa nirubangga'lilum ivaiga'laikku’riththup peasiyirukki’raan; avan sonnavaiga'lil sila kaariyangga'l a’rigi’ratha’rku arithaayirukki’rathu; kallaathavarga'lum u’ruthiyillaathavarga'lum mat’ra veathavaakkiyangga'laip purattugi’rathupoalath thangga'lukkuk keaduvaraththakkathaaga ivaiga'laiyum purattugi’raarga'l.

2Pe3.17 aathalaal piriyamaanavarga'lea, ivaiga'lai munnamea neengga'l a’rinthirukki’rapadiyaal, akkiramakkaararudaiya vagnchagaththilea neengga'l izhuppu'ndu ungga'l u’ruthiyilirunthu vilagi vizhunthupoagaathapadikku echcharikkaiyaayirunthu,

2Pe3.18 nammudaiya karththarum iradchagarumaagiya iyeasuki’risthuvin kirubaiyilum avarai a’rigi’ra a’rivilum va'larungga'l. avarukku ippozhuthum en’ren’raikkum magimaiyu'ndaavathaaga. aamen.
