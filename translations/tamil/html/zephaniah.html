Zep1.1 aamoanin puththiranaagiya yoasiyaa ennum yoothaa raajaavin naadka'lilea, eskiyaavin kumaaranaagiya aamariyaavukkuk kumaaranaana kethaliyaa enbavanudaiya maganaagiya kooshin kumaaran seppaniyaavukku u'ndaana karththarudaiya vasanam.

Zep1.2 theasaththil u'ndaanathai ellaam mut'rilum vaarikko'l'luvean en'ru karththar sollugi'raar.

Zep1.3 manusharaiyum mirugajeevanga'laiyum vaarikko'l'luvean; naan aagaayaththup pa'ravaiga'laiyum, samuththiraththu machchangga'laiyum, ida'rugi'ratha'rkeathuvaanavaiga'laiyum thunmaarkkaroadeakooda vaarikko'ndu, theasaththil u'ndaana manusharaich sanggaarampa'n'nuvean en'ru karththar sollugi'raar.

Zep1.4 naan yoothaavinmealum, erusaleamilu'l'la ellaak kudiga'linmealum en kaiyai neetti, baagaalil meethiyaayirukki'rathaiyum, aasaariyarga'loadeakoodak kemmareem enbavarga'lin pearaiyum,

Zep1.5 veeduga'linmeal vaanaseanaiyaip pa'nigi'ravarga'laiyum, karththarpearil aa'naiyittu, malkaaminpearilum aa'naiyittup pa'nigi'ravarga'laiyum,

Zep1.6 karththarai vittup pinvaanggugi'ravarga'laiyum, karththaraith theadaamalum, avaraikku'riththu visaariyaamalumirukki'ravarga'laiyum, ivvidaththil iraathapadikkuch sanggaarampa'n'nuvean.

Zep1.7 karththaraagiya aa'ndavarukku munbaaga maunamaayirungga'l; karththarudaiya naa'l sameebiththirukki'rathu; karththar oru yaagaththai aayaththampa'n'ni, atha'rku virunthaa'liga'laiyum azhaiththirukki'raar.

Zep1.8 karththarudaiya yaagaththin naa'lilea naan athibathiga'laiyum raajakumaararaiyum ma'rutheasaththu vasthiram tharikki'ra yaavaraiyum tha'ndippean.

Zep1.9 vaasa'rpadiyaith thaa'ndi, kodumaiyinaalum vagnchagaththinaalum thangga'l ejamaanga'lin veeduga'lai nirappugi'ra yaavaraiyum annaa'lilea tha'ndippean.

Zep1.10 annaa'lilea meenvaasalilirunthu kookkuralin saththamum, nagaraththin ira'ndaam paagaththilirunthu ala'ruthalum, meaduga'lilirunthu mahaa sanggaaraththin iraichchalum u'ndaagumen'ru karththar sollugi'raar.

Zep1.11 maktheashin kudiga'lea ala'rungga'l; varththagarellaarum sanggaaramaanaarga'l; kaasukkaarar yaavarum vettu'ndupoanaarga'l.

Zep1.12 akkaalaththilea naan erusaleamai vi'lakkukko'luththich soathiththu, va'ndalpoalak kuzhambiyirukki'ravarga'lum, karththar nanmaiseyvathum illai theemaiseyvathum illaiyen'ru thangga'l iruthayaththil sollugi'ravarga'lumaana manusharaith tha'ndippean.

Zep1.13 avarga'ludaiya aasthi ko'l'laiyaagum; avarga'ludaiya veeduga'l paazhaayppoagum; avarga'l veeduga'laik kattiyum, avaiga'lil kudiyirukkamaattaarga'l; avarga'l thiraadchaththoattangga'lai naattiyum, avaiga'lin pazharasaththaik kudippathillai.

Zep1.14 karththarudaiya periyanaa'l sameebiththirukki'rathu; athu kittichsearnthu migavum theeviriththuvarugi'rathu; karththarudaiya naa'lengi'ra saththaththukkup baraakkiramasaali muthalaay anggea manangkasanthu ala'ruvaan.

Zep1.15 antha naa'l ukkiraththin naa'l; athu ikkattum idukkamumaana naa'l; athu azhivum paazhkkadippumaana naa'l; athu iru'lum anthagaaramumaana naa'l; athu mappum manthaaramumaana naa'l.

Zep1.16 athu ara'nippaana nagarangga'lukkum, uyaramaana koththa'langga'lukkum viroathamaaga ekkaa'lam oothugi'rathum aarpparikki'rathumaana naa'l.

Zep1.17 manushar karththarukku viroathamaayp paavagnseythapadiyaal, avarga'l kurudaraippoal nadakkumpadi naan avarga'lai varuththappaduththuvean; avarga'l iraththam puzhuthiyaippoal soriyappadum; avarga'l maamsam eruvaippoal kidakkum.

Zep1.18 karththarudaiya ukkiraththin naa'lilea avarga'l ve'l'liyum avarga'l ponnum avarga'laith thappuvikkamaattaathu; avarudaiya erichchalin akkiniyinaal theasamellaam azhiyum; theasaththin kudiga'laiyellaam sadithiyaay nirmoolampa'n'nuvaar.

Zep2.1 virumbappadaatha jaathiyea, katta'lai pi'rakkumunnum, patharaippoala naa'l pa'ranthupoagumunnum karththarudaiya ukkirakoabam ungga'lmeal i'ranggumunnum, karththarudaiya koabaththin naa'l ungga'lmeal varumunnum,

Zep2.2 neengga'l ungga'lai uyththu aaraaynthu soathiyungga'l.

Zep2.3 theasaththilu'l'la ellaach si'rumaiyaanavarga'lea, karththarudaiya niyaayaththai nadappikki'ravarga'lea, avaraith theadungga'l; neethiyaith theadungga'l, manaththaazhmaiyaith theadungga'l; appozhuthu oruvea'lai karththarudaiya koabaththinnaa'lilea ma'raikkappaduveerga'l.

Zep2.4 kaathsaa kudiyat'ru, askaloan paazhaagum; asthoaththaip pattappagalilea pa'rakkadippaarga'l; ekroan vearoadea pidunggappadum.

Zep2.5 samuththirakkarai kudiga'laagiya kireaththiyarukku aiyoa! pelistharin theasamaagiya kaanaanea, karththarudaiya vaarththai unakku viroathamaayirukki'rathu; ini unnil kudiyillaathapadikku unnai azhippean.

Zep2.6 samuththirakkarai theasam meayppar thanggum kudilga'lum aattuththoazhangga'lumaagum.

Zep2.7 anthath theasam yoothaa vamsaththaaril meethiyaanavarga'lin vasamaagum; avarga'l avvidangga'lil manthai meayppaarga'l; askaloanin veeduga'lilea saayanggaalaththilea paduththukko'lvaarga'l; avarga'ludaiya theavanaagiya karththar avarga'lai visaariththu, avarga'l si'raiyiruppaith thiruppuvaar.

Zep2.8 moavaab seytha ninthanaiyaiyum, ammoan puththirar en janaththai ninthiththu, avarga'l ellaiyaik kadanthu perumaipaaraattich sonna thoosha'nangga'laiyum keattean.

Zep2.9 aagaiyaal moavaab soathoamaippoalum, ammoan puththirarin theasam komoaraavaippoalumaagi, kaagncho'ri padarum idamum, uppuppa'l'lamum, niththiya paazhumaayirukkum; en janaththil meenthavarga'l avarga'laik ko'l'laiyittu, en jaathiyil meethiyaanavarga'l avarga'laich suthanthariththukko'lvaarga'l enbathai en jeevanaikko'ndu sollugi'rean en'ru isravealin theavanaagiya seanaiga'lin karththar uraikki'raar.

Zep2.10 avarga'l seanaiga'ludaiya karththarin janaththukku viroathamaayp perumaipaaraatti avarga'lai ninthiththapadiyinaal, ithu avarga'l aganggaaraththukkup bathilaaga avarga'lukkuk kidaikkum.

Zep2.11 karththar avarga'lmeal kediyaayiruppaar; boomiyilu'l'la theavarga'laiyellaam melinthupoagappa'n'nuvaar; appozhuthu theevuga'lilu'l'la sagala pu'rajaathiga'lum avaravar thangga'l thangga'l sthaanaththilirunthu avaraip pa'ninthuko'lvaarga'l.

Zep2.12 eththiyoappiyaraagiya neengga'lum en pattayaththinaal kolaiseyyappaduveerga'l.

Zep2.13 avar thamathu kaiyai vadatheasaththukku viroathamaay neetti, aseeriyaavai azhiththu, niniveayaip paazhum vanaantharaththukkoththa va'radchiyumaana sthalamaakkuvaar.

Zep2.14 athin naduvil manthaiga'lum jaathijaathiyaana sagala mirugangga'lum paduththukko'l'lum; athinudaiya sigarangga'linmeal naaraiyum koattaanum iraaththanggum; palaga'niga'lil koovugi'ra saththam pi'rakkum; vaasa'rpadiga'lil paazhkkadippu irukkum; keathurumarangga'lin machchaith thi'rappaakkippoaduvaar.

Zep2.15 naanthaan, ennaiththavira vea'roruvarum illai en'ru than iruthayaththil solli, nirvisaaramaay vaazhnthu ka'likoornthiruntha nagaram ithuvea; ithu paazhum mirugajeevanga'lin thaabaramumaayppoayvittathea! athin vazhiyaayppoagi'ravan evanum eesal poattuth than kaiyaik kottuvaan.

Zep3.1 idukka'n seythu, ooththaiyum azhukkumaayirukki'ra nagaraththukku aiyoa!

Zep3.2 athu saththaththukkuch sevikodukkavillai; athu kadinthuko'l'luthalai eat'rukko'l'lavillai; athu karththarai nambavillai; athu than theavanidaththil searavillai.

Zep3.3 atha'rku'l'lea irukki'ra athin athibathiga'l kerchchikki'ra singgangga'l; athin niyaayaathibathiga'l saayanggaalaththil pu'rappadugi'rathum vidiya'rkaalamattum oru elumbaiyum meethiyaaga vaikkaathathumaana oanaayga'l.

Zep3.4 athin theerkkatharisiga'l vee'nperumaiyum vagnchagamumu'l'lavarga'l; athin aasaariyarga'l parisuththa sthalaththaip parisuththakkulaichchalaakki, veathaththukku aniyaayagnseythaarga'l.

Zep3.5 atha'rku'l irukki'ra karththar neethiyu'l'lavar; avar aniyaayagnseyvathillai; avar ku'raivillaamal kaalaithoa'rum thammudaiya niyaayaththai vi'langgappa'n'nugi'raar; aniyaayakkaaranoa vedkam a'riyaan.

Zep3.6 jaathiga'laich sanggariththean; avarga'l thurugangga'l paazhaayina; avarga'ludaiya veethiga'lai oruvarum kadanthupoagaathapadikkup paazhaakkinean; avarga'ludaiya patta'nangga'l manushar illaathapadikkum kudiyillaathapadikkum avaantharaiyaayina.

Zep3.7 un vaasasthalam nirmoolamaagaathapadikku nee enakkup bayanthu, kadinthuko'l'luthalai eat'rukko'l en'rean; naan avarga'lai eppadith tha'ndiththaalum, avarga'l athikaalaiyil ezhunthu thangga'l kiriyaiga'laiyellaam keadaakkinaarga'l.

Zep3.8 aagaiyaal naan ko'l'laiyaada ezhumbum naa'lmattum enakkuk kaaththirungga'l en'ru karththar sollugi'raar; en sinamaagiya ukkira koabaththaiyellaam avarga'lmeal soriyumpadi jaathiga'laich searkkavum, raajyangga'laik koottavum naan theermaanampa'n'ninean; boomiyellaam en erichchalin akkiniyinaal azhiyum.

Zep3.9 appozhuthu janangga'lellaarum karththarudaiya naamaththaith thozhuthuko'ndu, orumanappattu avarukku aaraathanai seyyumpadikku, naan avarga'l baashaiyaich suththamaana baashaiyaaga maa'rappa'n'nuvean.

Zep3.10 eththiyoappiyaavin nathiga'lukku akkaraiyilirunthu ennidaththil vi'n'nappampa'n'nugi'ravarga'laagiya sitha'radikkappattavarga'lin kumaaraththiyaanava'l enakkuk kaa'nikkai ko'nduvaruvaa'l.

Zep3.11 enakku viroathamaayth thuroagampa'n'ni, nee seytha un ellaak kiriyaiga'linimiththamum annaa'lilea vedkappadaathiruppaay; appozhuthu naan un perumaiyaikku'riththuk ka'likoornthavarga'lai un naduvilirunthu vilakkividuvean; nee ini en parisuththa parvathaththil aganggaarangko'l'lamaattaay.

Zep3.12 un naduvil si'rumaiyum e'limaiyumaana janaththai meethiyaaga vaippean; avarga'l karththarudaiya naamaththinmeal nambikkaiyaayiruppaarga'l.

Zep3.13 isravealil meethiyaanavarga'l aniyaayagnseyvathillai; avarga'l poy peasuvathumillai; vagnchaganaavu avarga'l vaayil ka'ndupidikkappaduvathumillai; avarga'l thangga'laip bayappaduththuvaarillaamal pusiththup paduththukko'lvaarga'l.

Zep3.14 seeyoan kumaaraththiyea, kembeeriththuppaadu; isravealarea, aarppariyungga'l; erusaleam kumaaraththiyea, nee muzhu iruthayaththoadum magizhnthu ka'likooru.

Zep3.15 karththar un aakkinaiga'lai agat'ri, un saththurukka'lai vilakkinaar; isravealin raajaavaagiya karththar un naduvilea irukki'raar; inith theenggaik kaa'naathiruppaay.

Zep3.16 annaa'lilea erusaleamaip paarththu, bayappadaathea en'rum, seeyoanaip paarththu, un kaiga'laith tha'laravidaathea en'rum sollappadum.

Zep3.17 un theavanaagiya karththar un naduvil irukki'raar; avar vallamaiyu'l'lavar, avar iradchippaar; avar unpearil santhoashamaay magizhnthu, thammudaiya anbinimiththam amarnthiruppaar; avar unpearil kembeeramaayk ka'likooruvaar.

Zep3.18 un sabaiyin manusharaayirunthu, pa'ndigai aasarippillaamaiyaal u'ndaana ninthaiyinimiththam sagnchalappattavarga'lai naan eagamaayk koottikko'l'luvean.

Zep3.19 ithoa, akkaalaththilea unnaich si'rumaippaduththina yaavaraiyum tha'ndippean; no'ndiyaanavanai iradchiththu, tha'l'lu'ndavanaich searththukko'l'luvean; avarga'l vedkam anubaviththa sagala theasangga'lilum avarga'lukkup pugazhchchiyum keerththiyum u'ndaagach seyvean.

Zep3.20 akkaalaththilea ungga'laik koottikko'nduvaruvean; akkaalaththilea ungga'laich searththukko'l'luvean; ungga'l ka'nkaa'na naan ungga'l si'raiyiruppaith thiruppumpoathu, boomiyilu'l'la sagala janangga'lukku'l'lum naan ungga'laik keerththiyum pugazhchchiyumaaga vaippean en'ru karththar sollugi'raar.
