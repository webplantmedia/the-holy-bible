Na1.1 niniveayin baaram. elkoasaanaagiya naagoomin tharisanap pusthagam.

Na1.2 karththar erichchalu'l'lavarum neethiyaich sarikkattugi'ravarumaana theavan; karththar neethiyaich sarikkattugi'ravar, ukkirakoabamu'l'lavar; karththar thammudaiya saththurukka'lukkup pirathipalan a'likki'ravar, avar thammudaiya pagaignarukkaagak koabaththai vaiththuvaikki'ravar.

Na1.3 karththar neediya saanthamum, miguntha vallamaiyumu'l'lavar; avarga'lai aakkinaiyillaamal thappuvikkamaattaar; karththarudaiya vazhi suzhalkaat'rilum perungkaat'rilum irukki'rathu; meagangga'l avarudaiya paathaththoo'laayirukki'rathu.

Na1.4 avar samuththiraththai athatti, athai vat'rippoagappa'n'ni, sagala aa'ruga'laiyum va'radchiyaakkugi'raar; baasaanum karmealum soarnthu, leebanoanin sezhippu vaadippoagum.

Na1.5 avar samugaththil parvathangga'l athirnthu malaiga'l karainthupoagum; avar pirasannaththinaal boomiyum poochchakkaramum athil kudiyirukki'ra anaivaroadum edupattuppoam.

Na1.6 avarudaiya koabaththukku munbaaga ni'rpavan yaar? avarudaiya ukkirakoabaththilea tharippavan yaar? avarudaiya erichchal akkiniyaippoala i'raikkappadugi'rathu; avaraalea kanmalaiga'l pearkkappadum.

Na1.7 karththar nallavar, ikkattunaa'lilea ara'naana koattai; thammai nambugi'ravarga'lai a'rinthirukki'raar.

Na1.8 aanaalum niniveayin sthaanaththai, pura'nduvarugi'ra ve'l'laththinaal sarvasanggaarampa'n'nuvaar; iru'l avar saththurukka'laip pinthodarum.

Na1.9 neengga'l karththarukku viroathamaagach seyya ninaikki'rathenna? avar sarvasanggaarampa'n'nuvaar; idukkam ma'rupadiyum u'ndaagaathu.

Na1.10 avarga'l sannapinnalaayirukki'ra mudchediga'lukku oppaayirukkaiyilum, thangga'l mathubaanaththinaal ve'riko'ndirukkaiyilum, avarga'l muzhuthum kaaynthupoana seththaiyaippoala erinthupoavaarga'l.

Na1.11 karththarukku viroathamaagap pollaatha ninaivuko'ndirukki'ra thuraaloasanaikkaaran oruvan unnidaththilirunthu pu'rappattaan.

Na1.12 karththar sollugi'rathu ennaven'raal: avarga'l sampoora'namadainthu aneagaraayirunthaalum a'ruppu'ndupoavaarga'l; avan ozhinthupoavaan; unnai naan si'rumaippaduththinean, ini unnaich si'rumaippaduththaathiruppean.

Na1.13 ippoathum naan unmeal irukki'ra avan nugaththai mu'riththu, un kattuga'lai a'ruppean.

Na1.14 unnaikku'riththuk karththar katta'laikoduththirukki'raar; ini un pearukku viththuvithaikkappaduvathillai; un theavarga'lin koavilil irukki'ra vettappatta vikkiragaththaiyum, vaarkkappatta vikkiragaththaiyum naan nirmoolampa'n'nuvean; nee kanaveenanaanapadiyaal athai unakkup pireathakkuzhiyaakkuvean.

Na1.15 ithoa, samaathaanaththaik koo'rugi'ra suviseashaganudaiya kaalga'l malaiga'linmeal varugi'rathu; yoothaavea, un pa'ndigaiga'lai aasari; un poruththanaiga'laich seluththu; thushdan ini un vazhiyaayk kadanthuvaruvathillai, avan muzhuvathum sanggarikkappattaan.

Na2.1 sitha'radikki'ravan un mugaththukku munbaaga varugi'raan; ara'naik kaaththukko'l, vazhiyaik kaavalpa'n'nu, araiyaik kettiyaayk kattikko'l, un belanai migavum sthirappaduththu.

Na2.2 ve'rumaiyaakkugi'ravarga'l avarga'lai ve'rumaiyaakki, avarga'ludaiya thiraadchakkodiga'laik keduththuppoattaalum, karththar yaakkoabin magimaiyaith thirumbivarappa'n'nuvathu poal, isravealin magimaiyaiyum thirumbivarappa'n'nuvaar.

Na2.3 avanudaiya baraakkiramasaaliga'lin keadagam iraththamayamaagum; avanudaiya yuththaveerar iraththaambaran thariththukko'ndirukki'raarga'l; avan thannai aayaththampa'n'num naa'lilea irathangga'l juvaalikki'ra kadkangga'lai udaiyathaayirukkum; eettiga'l kulunggum.

Na2.4 irathangga'l therukka'lil kadakadaven'roadi, veethiga'lil idasaari valasaari varum; avaiga'l theevattiga'laippoala vi'langgi, minnalga'laippoala veagamaayp pa'rakkum.

Na2.5 avan than pirabalastharai ninaivukooruvaan; avarga'l thangga'l nadaiga'lil ida'ri, alanggaththukku virainthu oaduvaarga'l; ma'raividam aayaththappaduththappadum.

Na2.6 aa'ruga'lin mathaguga'l thi'rakkappadum, aramanai karainthupoam.

Na2.7 avan si'raippattuppoagath theermaanamaayit'ru; ava'ludaiya thaathimaarga'l thangga'l maarbilea adiththukko'ndu, pu'raakka'laippoalach saththamittuk koodappoavaarga'l.

Na2.8 ninivea poorvakaalamuthal tha'n'neerth thadaagampoal irunthathu; ippoathoa avarga'l oadippoagi'raarga'l; nillungga'l nillungga'l en'raalum, thirumbippaarkki'ravan illai.

Na2.9 ve'l'liyaik ko'l'laiyidungga'l, ponnaiyum ko'l'laiyidungga'l; sambaththukku mudivillai; ichchikkappadaththakka sagalavitha poru'lga'lum irukki'rathu.

Na2.10 ava'l ve'rumaiyum ve'liyum paazhumaavaa'l; manam karainthupoagi'rathu; muzhangkaalga'l tha'l'laadugi'rathu; ellaa iduppuga'lilum miguntha veathanai u'ndu; ellaarudaiya mugangga'lum karugippoagi'rathu.

Na2.11 singgangga'lin vaasasthalam enggea? baalasinggam iraithingi'ra idam enggea? kizhachsinggamaagiya singgamum, singgakkuttiga'lum bayappaduththuvaarillaamal sagncharikki'ra sthaanam enggea?

Na2.12 singgam than kuttiga'lukkuth theavaiyaanathaip pee'ri, than pe'n singgangga'lukku vea'ndiyathaith tho'ndaiyaip pidiththuk kon'ru, iraiga'linaal than kebiga'laiyum, pee'rippoattavaiga'linaal than thaabarangga'laiyum nirappit'ru.

Na2.13 ithoa, naan unakku viroathamaaga vanthu, irathangga'laip pugaiyezhumba eriththuppoaduvean; pattayam un baalasinggangga'laip padchikkum; nee iraikkaagap pidikkum veattaiyaith theasaththil at'ruppoagappa'n'nuvean; un sthaanaabathiga'lin saththam inik keadkappaduvathillai en'ru seanaiga'lin karththar sollugi'raar.

Na3.1 iraththappazhiga'lin nagaraththi'rku aiyoa! athu vagnchagaththinaalum kodumaiyinaalum ni'rainthirukki'rathu; ko'l'lai oayaamal nadakki'rathu.

Na3.2 savukkuga'lin oasaiyum, uru'laiga'lin athirchchiyum, kuthiraiga'lin paaychchalum, irathangga'l kadakadaven'ru oadugi'ra saththamum,

Na3.3 veerar kuthirai ea'rugi'rathum, pattayangga'l thulanggugi'rathum, eettiga'l minnugi'rathum, vettu'ndavarga'lin thira'lum, pireathangga'lin earaa'lamum anggea u'ndaayirukkum; pi'nangga'lukkuth thogaiyillai; avarga'l pi'nangga'lil ida'rivizhugi'raarga'l.

Na3.4 than veasiththanangga'linaal jaathiga'laiyum, than sooniyangga'linaal vamsangga'laiyum vi'rki'ra mahaa sooniyakkaariyum roobavathiyumaayirukki'ra veasiyinudaiya thira'laana veasiththanangga'linimiththam,

Na3.5 ithoa, naan unakku viroathamaaga vanthu un vasthiraththu oarangga'lai un mugamattum thookkiyeduththu, jaathiga'lukku un nirvaa'naththaiyum, raajyangga'lukku un maanaththaiyum theriyappa'n'ni,

Na3.6 unmeal theettaanavaiga'lai e'rinthu, unnaik kanaveenappaduththi, unnai veadikkaiyaakkippoaduvean en'ru seanaiga'lin karththar sollugi'raar.

Na3.7 appozhuthu unnaik kaa'ngi'ravanellaam ninivea paazhaayppoayit'ru, atha'rkaagap pulambugi'ravar yaar? aa'ruthal sollugi'ravarga'lai unakku enggea theaduveanen'ru solli, unnaivittoadip poavaan.

Na3.8 nathiga'l maththiyiliruntha noa ammoanaippaarkkilum nee sireashdamoa? athaich sut'rilum tha'n'neer irunthathu; samuththiram athin ara'num, samuththirakkaal athin mathilumaayirunthathu.

Na3.9 eththiyoappiyaavum egipthum e'n'ni'rantha seanaiyaal atha'rkup belanaaga irunthathu; pooththum loobeemum atha'rkuch sagaayamaayirunthathu.

Na3.10 aayinum ava'l kudivilakkappattuch si'raiyiruppilea ko'ndupoagappattaa'l; ava'l kuzhanthaiga'l ellaa veethiga'lin muganaiga'lilum moathiyadikkappattaarga'l; ava'ludaiya kanavaanga'lpearil seettup poattaarga'l; ava'ludaiya periyavarga'l ellaarum sanggiliga'laal kattappattaarga'l.

Na3.11 neeyum ve'riko'ndu o'liththukko'lvaay; neeyum un saththuruvukkuth thappa ara'naana koattaiyaith theaduvaay.

Na3.12 un ara'nga'lellaam muthal pazhukkum pazhangga'lu'l'la aththimarangga'laippoal irukkum; avaiga'l kulukkappattaal avaiga'lin pazham thingi'ravan vaayilea vizhum.

Na3.13 ithoa, un naduvil irukki'ra janangga'l peadiga'l; un theasaththin vaasalga'l un saththuruvukkumun thi'ravu'ndupoagum; akkini un thaazhppaa'lga'laip padchikkum.

Na3.14 mut'rigaikkuth tha'n'neer mo'ndu vai, un ara'nga'laip balappaduththu; seat'rilea poayk ka'lima'n'nai mithi, soo'laiyaik kettippaduththu.

Na3.15 anggea akkini unnaip padchikkum, pattayam unnaich sanggarikkum; athu pachchaikki'liga'laippoal unnaip padchiththuppoadum; unnaip pachchaikki'liga'laththanaiyaakkikko'l, unnai vettukki'liga'laththanaiyaakkikko'l.

Na3.16 un varththagarai vaanaththu nadchaththirangga'lilum athigamaakkinaay; inthap pachchaikki'liga'l paravip pa'ranthupoagum.

Na3.17 un magudavarththanar vettukki'liga'lukkum, un tha'lakarththar perungki'liga'lukkum samaanamaayirukki'raarga'l; avaiga'l ku'lirchchiyaana naa'lil vealiga'lil paa'laiyami'ranggi, sooriyan uthiththamaaththiraththil pa'ranthupoam; pinbu avaiga'l irukkum idam innathen'ru theriyaathu.

Na3.18 aseeriyaa raajaavea, un meaypparga'l u'rangguvaarga'l; un pirabalasthar paduththiruppaarga'l; un janangga'l parvathangga'linmeal sitha'riyirukki'raarga'l, avaiga'laik koottich searppavan illai.

Na3.19 un no'rungguthalukkup parigaaram illai; un kaayam kodiyathu; un seythiyaik keadpavar yaavarum unpearil kaikottuvaarga'l; un pollaappu ennearamum yaarpearileathaan paayaama'rpoayit'ru?
