1Pe1.1 iyeasuki’risthuvin appoasthalanaagiya peathuru, ponthu, kalaaththiyaa, kappaththoakkiyaa, aasiyaa, biththiniyaa theasangga'lilea sitha’riyirukki’ravarga'lil,

1Pe1.2 pithaavaagiya theavanudaiya munna’rivinpadiyea, aaviyaanavarin parisuththamaakkuthalinaalea, keezhppadithalukkum iyeasuki’risthuvinudaiya iraththanthe'likkappaduthalukkum therinthuko'l'lappatta paratheasiga'lukku ezhuthugi’rathaavathu: kirubaiyum samaathaanamum ungga'lukkup perugakkadavathu.

1Pe1.3 nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki’risthuvin pithaavaagiya theavanukku sthoaththiram u'ndaavathaaga;

1Pe1.4 avar, iyeasuki’risthu mariththoarilirunthu ezhunthathinaalea, azhiyaathathum maasat’rathum vaadaathathumaagiya suthantharaththi’rkeathuvaaga, jeevanu'l'la nambikkai u'ndaagumpadi, thamathu miguntha irakkaththinpadiyea nammai ma’rupadiyum jenippiththaar.

1Pe1.5 kadaisikkaalaththilea ve'lippada aayaththamaakkappattirukki’ra iradchippukku eathuvaaga visuvaasaththaikko'ndu theavanudaiya balaththinaalea kaakkappattirukki’ra ungga'lukku anthach suthantharam paraloagaththil vaikkappattirukki’rathu.

1Pe1.6 ithilea neengga'l migavum santhoashappadugi’reerga'l; en’raalum, thunbappadavea'ndiyathu avasiyamaanathaal, ippozhuthu kognchakkaalam palavithamaana soathanaiga'linaalea thukkappadugi’reerga'l.

1Pe1.7 azhinthupoagi’ra pon akkiniyinaalea soathikkappadum; athaippaarkkilum athiga vilaiyea’rappet’rathaayirukki’ra ungga'l visuvaasam soathikkappattu, iyeasuki’risthu ve'lippadumpoathu ungga'lukkup pugazhchchiyum kanamum magimaiyumu'ndaagak kaa'nappadum.

1Pe1.8 avarai neengga'l kaa'naamalirunthum avaridaththil anbukoorugi’reerga'l; ippozhuthu avaraith tharisiyaamalirunthum avaridaththil visuvaasam vaiththu, sollimudiyaathathum magimaiyaal ni’rainthathumaayirukki’ra santhoashamu'l'lavarga'laayk ka'likoornthu,

1Pe1.9 ungga'l visuvaasaththin palanaagiya aaththumaradchippai adaigi’reerga'l.

1Pe1.10 ungga'lukku u'ndaana kirubaiyaik ku’riththuth theerkkatharisanagnsonna theerkkatharisiga'l intha iradchippaikku’riththuk karuththaay aaraaynthu parisoathanaipa'n'ninaarga'l;

1Pe1.11 thangga'lilu'l'la ki’risthuvin aaviyaanavar ki’risthuvukku u'ndaagum paaduga'laiyum, avaiga'lukkup pinvarum magimaiga'laiyum munna’riviththapoathu, innakaalaththaik ku’riththaarenbathaiyum, anthak kaalaththin viseasham innathenbathaiyum aaraaynthaarga'l.

1Pe1.12 thangga'lnimiththamalla, namathunimiththamea ivaiga'laith theriviththaarga'len’ru avarga'lukku ve'liyaakkappattathu; paraloagaththilirunthu anuppappatta parisuththa aaviyinaalea ungga'lukkuch suviseashaththaip pirasanggiththavarga'laikko'ndu ivaiga'l ippozhuthu ungga'lukku a’rivikkappattu varugi’rathu; ivaiga'lai ut’ruppaarkkath theavathootharum aasaiyaayirukki’raarga'l.

1Pe1.13 aagaiyaal, neengga'l ungga'l manathin araiyaik kattikko'ndu, the'lintha buththiyu'l'lavarga'laayirunthu; iyeasuki’risthu ve'lippadumpoathu ungga'lukku a'likkappadung kirubaiyinmeal poora'na nambikkaiyu'l'lavarga'laayirungga'l.

1Pe1.14 neengga'l munnea ungga'l a’riyaamaiyinaalea ko'ndiruntha ichchaiga'linpadi ini nadavaamal, keezhppadigi’ra pi'l'laiga'laayirunthu,

1Pe1.15 ungga'lai azhaiththavar parisuththaraayirukki’rathupoala, neengga'lum ungga'l nadakkaiga'lellaavat’rileayum parisuththaraayirungga'l.

1Pe1.16 naan parisuththar, aagaiyaal neengga'lum parisuththaraayirungga'l en’ru ezhuthiyirukki’rathea.

1Pe1.17 an’riyum, padchapaathamillaamal avanavanudaiya kiriyaiga'linpadi niyaayantheerkki’ravarai neengga'l pithaavaagath thozhuthuko'nduvarugi’rapadiyaal, inggea paratheasiga'laaych sagncharikkuma'lavum bayaththudanea nadanthuko'l'lungga'l.

1Pe1.18 ungga'l munnoarga'laal paarambariyamaay neengga'l anusariththuvantha vee'naana nadaththaiyinin’ru azhivu'l'la vasthukka'laagiya ve'l'liyinaalum ponninaalum meedkappadaamal,

1Pe1.19 kut’ramillaatha maasat’ra aattukkuttiyaagiya ki’risthuvin vilaiyea’rappet’ra iraththaththinaalea meedkappatteerga'len’ru a’rinthirukki’reerga'lea.

1Pe1.20 avar ulagaththoat’raththi’rku munnea ku’rikkappattavaraayirunthu, thamathu moolamaayth theavanmeal visuvaasamaayirukki’ra ungga'lukkaaga inthak kadaisik kaalangga'lil ve'lippattaar.

1Pe1.21 ungga'l visuvaasamum nambikkaiyum theavanmealirukkumpadi, avarai mariththoarilirunthu ezhuppi, avarukku magimaiyaik koduththaar.

1Pe1.22 aagaiyaal neengga'l maayamat’ra sagoathara sineagamu'l'lavarga'laagumpadi, aaviyinaalea saththiyaththi’rkuk keezhppadinthu, ungga'l aaththumaakka'laich suththamaakkikko'ndavarga'laayirukki’rapadiyaal, suththa iruthayaththoadea oruvariloruvar ookkamaay anbukoorungga'l;

1Pe1.23 azhivu'l'la viththinaalea alla, en’ren’raikkum ni’rki’rathum jeevanu'l'lathumaana theavavasanamaagiya azhivillaatha viththinaalea ma’rupadiyum jenippikkappattirukki’reerga'lea.

1Pe1.24 maamsamellaam pullaippoalavum, manusharudaiya magimaiyellaam pullin poovaippoalavumirukki’rathu; pul ularnthathu, athin poovum uthirnthathu.

1Pe1.25 karththarudaiya vasanamoa en’ren’raikkum nilaiththirukkum; ungga'lukkuch suviseashamaay a’rivikkappattuvarugi’ra vasanam ithuvea.

1Pe2.1 ippadiyirukka, karththar thayaiyu'l'lavarenbathai neengga'l rusipaarththathu'ndaanaal,

1Pe2.2 sagala thurkku'naththaiyum, sagalavitha kabadaththaiyum, vagnchagangga'laiyum, po’raamaiga'laiyum, sagalavitha pu’rangkoo’ruthalaiyum ozhiththuvittu,

1Pe2.3 neengga'l va'larumpadi, puthithaayp pi’rantha kuzhanthaiga'laippoala, thiruvasanamaagiya ka'langgamillaatha gnaanappaalinmeal vaagnchaiyaayirungga'l.

1Pe2.4 manusharaal tha'l'lappattathaayinum, theavanaal therinthuko'l'lappattathum vilaiyea’rappet’rathumaayirukki’ra jeevanu'l'la kallaagiya avaridaththil searnthavarga'laagiya neengga'lum,

1Pe2.5 jeevanu'l'la ka’rka'laippoala aavikkeat’ra maa'ligaiyaagavum, iyeasuki’risthu moolamaayth theavanukkup piriyamaana aavikkeat’ra baliga'laich seluththumpadikkup parisuththa aasaariyakkoottamaagavum kattappattuvarugi’reerga'l.

1Pe2.6 anthappadiyea: ithoa, therinthuko'l'lappattathum vilaiyea’rappet’rathumaayirukki’ra moolaikkallai seeyoanil vaikki’rean; athinmeal visuvaasamaayirukki’ravan vedkappaduvathillai en’ru veathaththilea solliyirukki’rathu.

1Pe2.7 aagaiyaal visuvaasikki’ra ungga'lukku athu vilaiyea’rappet’rathu; keezhppadiyaamalirukki’ravarga'lukkoa veettaik kattugi’ravarga'laal tha'l'lappatta pirathaana moolaikkallaagiya anthak kal ida’rutha’rkeathuvaana kallum vizhutha’rkeathuvaana kanmalaiyumaayit’ru;

1Pe2.8 avarga'l thiruvasanaththi’rkuk keezhppadiyaathavarga'laayirunthu ida’rugi’raarga'l; atha’rken’rea niyamikkappattavarga'laayum irukki’raarga'l.

1Pe2.9 neengga'loa, ungga'lai anthagaaraththinin’ru thammudaiya aachchariyamaana o'liyinidaththi’rku varavazhaiththavarudaiya pu'n'niyangga'lai a’rivikkumpadikkuth therinthuko'l'lappatta santhathiyaayum, raajareegamaana aasaariyakkoottamaayum, parisuththa jaathiyaayum, avarukkuch sonthamaana janamaayum irukki’reerga'l.

1Pe2.10 munnea neengga'l theavanudaiya janangga'laayirukkavillai, ippozhuthoa avarudaiya janangga'laayirukki’reerga'l; munnea neengga'l irakkam pe’raathavarga'laayiruntheerga'l, ippozhuthoa irakkam pet’ravarga'laayirukki’reerga'l.

1Pe2.11 piriyamaanavarga'lea, anniyarga'lum paratheasiga'lumaayirukki’ra neengga'l aaththumaavukku viroathamaayp poarseygi’ra maamsa ichchaiga'lai vittu vilagi,

1Pe2.12 pu’rajaathiga'l ungga'lai akkiramakkaararen’ru viroathamaayp peasum vishayaththil, avarga'l ungga'l na’rkiriyaiga'laik ka'ndu, avat’rinimiththam santhippin naa'lilea theavanai magimaippaduththumpadi neengga'l avarga'lukku'l'lea nalnadakkaiyu'l'lavarga'laay nadanthuko'l'lungga'l en’ru ungga'lukkup buththisollugi’rean.

1Pe2.13 neengga'l manusharudaiya katta'laiga'l yaavat’ri’rkum karththarnimiththam keezhppadiyungga'l.

1Pe2.14 mealaana athigaaramu'l'la raajaavukkaanaalugnsari, theemaiseygi’ravarga'lukku aakkinaiyum nanmaiseygi’ravarga'lukkup pugazhchchiyum u'ndaagumpadi avanaal anuppappatta athigaariga'lukkaanaalugnsari, keezhppadiyungga'l.

1Pe2.15 neengga'l nanmaiseygi’rathinaalea buththiyeena manusharudaiya a’riyaamaiyai adakkuvathu theavanudaiya siththamaayirukki’rathu.

1Pe2.16 suyaatheenamu'l'lavarga'laayirunthum ungga'l suyaatheenaththaith thurkku'naththi’rku moodalaagak ko'ndiraamal, theavanukku adimaiga'laayirungga'l.

1Pe2.17 ellaaraiyum kanampa'n'nungga'l; sagoathararidaththil anbukoorungga'l; theavanukkup bayanthirungga'l; raajaavaik kanampa'n'nungga'l.

1Pe2.18 vealaikkaararea, athiga bayaththudanea ungga'l ejamaanga'lukkuk keezhppadinthirungga'l; nallavarga'lukkum saanthaku'namu'l'lavarga'lukkum maaththiram alla, murattukku'namu'l'lavarga'lukkum keezhppadinthirungga'l.

1Pe2.19 eanenil, theavanmeal pat’ruthalaayirukki’ra manachsaadchiyinimiththam oruvan aniyaayamaayp paadupattu ubaththiravangga'laip po’rumaiyaaych sagiththaal athuvea pireethiyaayirukkum.

1Pe2.20 neengga'l kut’ragnseythu adikkappadumpoathu po’rumaiyoadea sagiththaal, athinaal enna keerththiyu'ndu? neengga'l nanmaiseythu paadupadumpoathu po’rumaiyoadea sagiththaal athuvea theavanukkumunbaagap pireethiyaayirukkum.

1Pe2.21 itha’rkaaga neengga'l azhaikkappattumirukki’reerga'l; eanenil, ki’risthuvum ungga'lukkaagap paadupattu, neengga'l thammudaiya adichchuvaduga'laith thodarnthuvarumpadi ungga'lukku maathiriyaip pinvaiththuppoanaar.

1Pe2.22 avar paavagnseyyavillai, avarudaiya vaayilea vagnchanai kaa'nappadavumillai;

1Pe2.23 avar vaiyappadumpoathu bathil vaiyaamalum, paadupadumpoathu bayamu’ruththaamalum, niyaayamaayth theerppuchseygi’ravarukkuth thammai oppuviththaar.

1Pe2.24 naam paavangga'lukkuch seththu, neethikkup pizhaiththirukkumpadikku, avarthaamea thamathu sareeraththilea nammudaiya paavangga'laich siluvaiyinmeal sumanthaar; avarudaiya thazhumbuga'laal ku'namaaneerga'l.

1Pe2.25 sitha’ru'nda aaduga'laippoaliruntheerga'l; ippozhuthoa ungga'l aaththumaakka'lukku meaypparum ka'nkaa'niyumaanavaridaththil thiruppappattirukki’reerga'l.

1Pe3.1 anthappadi manaiviga'lea, ungga'l sonthap purusharga'lukkuk keezhppadinthirungga'l; appozhuthu avarga'lil yaaraavathu thiruvasanaththi’rkuk keezhppadiyaathavarga'laayirunthaal, bayabakthiyoadu koodiya ungga'l ka’rpu'l'la nadakkaiyai avarga'l paarththu,

1Pe3.2 poathanaiyin’ri, manaiviga'lin nadakkaiyinaaleayea aathaayappaduththikko'l'lappaduvaarga'l.

1Pe3.3 mayiraip pinni, ponnaabara'nangga'lai a'ninthu, uyarntha vasthirangga'lai uduththikko'l'luthalaagiya pu’rambaana alanggarippu ungga'lukku alanggaaramaayiraamal,

1Pe3.4 azhiyaatha alanggarippaayirukki’ra saanthamum amaithalumu'l'la aaviyaagiya iruthayaththil ma’rainthirukki’ra ku'namea ungga'lukku alanggaaramaayirukkakkadavathu; athuvea theavanudaiya paarvaiyil vilaiyea’rappet’rathu.

1Pe3.5 ippadiyea poorvaththil theavanidaththil nambikkaiyaayiruntha parisuththa sthireega'lum thangga'ludaiya purusharga'lukkuk keezhppadinthu thangga'lai alanggariththaarga'l.

1Pe3.6 anthappadiyea saaraa'l aabirahaamai aa'ndavan en’ru solli, avanukkuk keezhppadinthirunthaa'l; neengga'l nanmaiseythu oru aabaththukkum bayappadaathiruntheerga'laanaal ava'lukkup pi'l'laiga'laayiruppeerga'l.

1Pe3.7 anthappadi purusharga'lea, manaiviyaanava'l belaveena paa'ndamaayirukki’rapadiyinaal, ungga'l jebangga'lukkuth thadaivaraathapadikku, neengga'l viveagaththoadu avarga'ludanea vaazhnthu, ungga'ludaneakooda avarga'lum niththiya jeevanaagiya kirubaiyaich suthanthariththukko'l'lugi’ravarga'laanapadiyinaal, avarga'lukkuch seyyavea'ndiya kanaththaich seyyungga'l.

1Pe3.8 mealum, neengga'lellaarum orumanappattavarga'lum, irakkamu'l'lavarga'lum, sagoathara sineagamu'l'lavarga'lum, manaurukkamu'l'lavarga'lum, i'nakkamu'l'lavarga'lumaayirunthu,

1Pe3.9 theemaikkuth theemaiyaiyum, uthaasanaththukku uthaasanaththaiyum sarikkattaamal, atha’rkup bathilaaga, neengga'l aaseervaathaththaich suthanthariththukko'l'lumpadi azhaikkappattirukki’ravarga'len’ru a’rinthu, aaseervathiyungga'l.

1Pe3.10 jeevanai virumbi, nalla naadka'laik kaa'navea'ndumen’rirukki’ravan pollaappukkuth than naavaiyum, kabadaththukkuth than uthaduga'laiyum vilakkikkaaththu,

1Pe3.11 pollaappaivittu neenggi, nanmaiseythu, samaathaanaththaith theadi, athaip pinthodarakkadavan.

1Pe3.12 karththarudaiya ka'nga'l neethimaanga'lmeal noakkamaayirukki’rathu, avarudaiya seviga'l avarga'l vea'nduthalukkuk kavanamaayirukki’rathu; theemaiseygi’ravarga'lukkoa karththarudaiya mugam viroathamaayirukki’rathu.

1Pe3.13 neengga'l nanmaiyaip pinpat’rugi’ravarga'laanaal, ungga'lukkuth theemaiseygi’ravan yaar?

1Pe3.14 neethiyinimiththamaaga neengga'l paadupattaal baakkiyavaanga'laayiruppeerga'l; avarga'ludaiya bayamu’ruththalukku neengga'l bayappadaamalum kalanggaamalum irunthu;

1Pe3.15 karththaraagiya theavanai ungga'l iruthayangga'lil parisuththampa'n'nungga'l; ungga'lilirukki’ra nambikkaiyaikku’riththu ungga'lidaththil visaariththuk keadki’ra yaavarukkum saanthaththoadum va'nakkaththoadum uththaravusolla eppozhuthum aayaththamaayirungga'l.

1Pe3.16 ki’risthuvukkeat’ra ungga'l nalla nadakkaiyaith thooshikki’ravarga'l ungga'lai akkiramakkaararen’ru ungga'lukku viroathamaaych sollugi’ra vishayaththil vedkappadumpadikku nalmanachsaadchiyudaiyavarga'laayirungga'l.

1Pe3.17 theemaiseythu paadanubavippathilum, theavanukkuch siththamaanaal, nanmaiseythu paadanubavippathea meanmaiyaayirukkum.

1Pe3.18 eanenil, ki’risthuvum nammaith theavanidaththil searkkumpadi aneethiyu'l'lavarga'lukkup bathilaaga neethiyu'l'lavaraayp paavangga'linimiththam orutharam paadupattaar; avar maamsaththilea kolaiyu'ndu, aaviyilea uyirppikkappattaar.

1Pe3.19 antha aaviyilea avar poayk kaavalilu'l'la aaviga'lukkup pirasanggiththaar.

1Pe3.20 antha aaviga'l, poorvaththilea noavaa peazhaiyai aayaththampa'n'num naadka'lilea, theavan neediya po’rumaiyoadea kaaththirunthapoathu, keezhppadiyaama’r poanavaiga'l; anthap peazhaiyilea silaraagiya ettuppearmaaththiram piraveasiththu jalaththinaalea kaakkappattaarga'l.

1Pe3.21 atha’rku oppanaiyaana gnaanasnaanamaanathu, maamsa azhukkai neekkuthalaayiraamal, theavanaippat’rum nalmanachsaadchiyin udanpadikkaiyaayirunthu, ippozhuthu nammaiyum iyeasuki’risthuvinudaiya uyirththezhuthalinaal iradchikki’rathu;

1Pe3.22 avar paraloagaththi’rkup poay, theavanudaiya valathupaarisaththil irukki’raar; theavathootharga'lum athigaarangga'lum vallamaiga'lum avarukkuk keezhppattirukki’rathu.

1Pe4.1 ippadiyirukka, ki’risthu namakkaaga maamsaththilea paadupattapadiyaal, neengga'lum appadippatta sinthaiyai aayuthamaagath thariththukko'l'lungga'l.

1Pe4.2 eanen’raal maamsaththil paadupadugi’ravan ini maamsaththilirukkum kaalamvaraikkum manusharudaiya ichchaiga'linpadi pizhaikkaamal theavanudaiya siththaththinpadiyea pizhaikkaththakkathaagap paavangga'lai vittoaynthiruppaan.

1Pe4.3 sen’ra vaazhnaad kaalaththilea naam pu’rajaathiga'ludaiya ishdaththinpadi nadanthuko'ndathu poathum; appozhuthu naam kaamavigaaraththaiyum thurichchaiga'laiyum nadappiththu, mathubaanampa'n'ni, ka'liyaattuchseythu, ve’riko'ndu, aruvaruppaana vikkiragaaraathanaiyaich seythuvanthoam.

1Pe4.4 anthath thunmaarkka u'laiyilea avarga'loadeakooda neengga'l vizhaamalirukki’rathinaalea avarga'l aachchariyappattu, ungga'laith thooshikki’raarga'l.

1Pe4.5 uyiroadirukki’ravarga'lukkum mariththoarga'lukkum niyaayaththeerppukkodukka aayaththamaayirukki’ravarukku avarga'l ka'nakkoppuvippaarga'l.

1Pe4.6 itha’rkaaga mariththoaraanavarga'l, manusharmunbaaga maamsaththilea aakkinaikku'l'laakkappattirunthum, theavanmunbaaga aaviyilea pizhaikkumpadiyaaga, avarga'lukkum suviseasham a’rivikkappattathu.

1Pe4.7 ellaavat’ri’rkum mudivu sameebamaayit’ru; aagaiyaal the'lintha buththiyu'l'lavarga'laayirunthu, jebampa'n'nuvatha’rku jaakkirathaiyu'l'lavarga'laayirungga'l.

1Pe4.8 ellaavat’ri’rkum mealaaga oruvariloruvar ookkamaana anbu'l'lavarga'laayirungga'l; anbu thira'laana paavangga'lai moodum.

1Pe4.9 mu’rumu’ruppillaamal oruvaraiyoruvar ubasariyungga'l.

1Pe4.10 avanavan pet’ra varaththinpadiyea neengga'l theavanudaiya pa’rpala kirubaiyu'l'la eevuga'laip pagirnthukodukkum nalla ukkiraa'nakkaararpoala, oruvarukkoruvar uthaviseyyungga'l.

1Pe4.11 oruvan poathiththaal theavanudaiya vaakkiyangga'linpadi poathikkakkadavan; oruvan uthaviseythaal theavan thantharu'lum belaththinpadi seyyakkadavan; ellaavat’rileayum iyeasuki’risthumoolamaayth theavan magimaippadumpadiyea seyveerga'laaga; avarukkea magimaiyum vallamaiyum sathaakaalangga'lilum u'ndaayiruppathaaga. aamen.

1Pe4.12 piriyamaanavarga'lea, ungga'laich soathikkumpadi ungga'l naduvil pat’riyerigi’ra akkiniyaikku’riththu eathoa puthumaiyen’ru thigaiyaamal,

1Pe4.13 ki’risthuvin magimai ve'lippadumpoathu neengga'l ka'likoornthu magizhumpadiyaaga avarudaiya paaduga'lukku neengga'l panggaa'liga'laanathaal santhoashappadungga'l.

1Pe4.14 neengga'l ki’risthuvin naamaththinimiththam ninthikkappattaal baakkiyavaanga'l; eanen’raal theavanudaiya aaviyaagiya magimaiyu'l'la aaviyaanavar ungga'lmeal thanggiyirukki’raar; avarga'laalea thooshikkappadugi’raar; ungga'laalea magimaippadugi’raar.

1Pe4.15 aathalaal ungga'lil oruvanum kolaipaathaganaayaavathu, thirudanaayaavathu, pollaanggu seythavanaayaavathu, anniya kaariyangga'lil thalaiyittukko'ndavanaayaavathu paadupadugi’ravanaayirukkakkoodaathu.

1Pe4.16 oruvan ki’risthavanaayiruppathinaal paadupattaal vedkappadaamalirunthu, athinimiththam theavanai magimaippaduththakkadavan.

1Pe4.17 niyaayaththeerppu theavanudaiya veettilea thuvakkungkaalamaayirukki’rathu; munthi nammidaththilea athu thuvakkinaal theavanudaiya suviseashaththi’rkuk keezhppadiyaathavarga'lin mudivu ennamaayirukkum?

1Pe4.18 neethimaanea iradchikkappaduvathu arithaanaal, bakthiyillaathavanum paaviyum enggea ni’rpaan?

1Pe4.19 aagaiyaal theavanudaiya siththaththinpadi paadanubavikki’ravarga'l nanmaiseygi’ravarga'laayth thangga'l aaththumaakka'lai u'nmaiyu'l'la sirushdikarththaavaagiya avarukku oppukkodukkakkadavarga'l.

1Pe5.1 ungga'lilu'l'la moopparukku udanmooppanum, ki’risthuvin paaduga'lukkuch saadchiyum, ini ve'lippadum magimaikkup panggaa'liyumaayirukki’ra naan buththisollugi’rathennaven’raal:

1Pe5.2 ungga'lidaththilu'l'la theavanudaiya manthaiyai neengga'l meayththu, kattaayamaay alla, manappoorvamaayum, avaladcha'namaana aathaayaththi’rkaaga alla, u’rchaaga manathoadum,

1Pe5.3 suthantharaththai i’rumaappaay aa'lugi’ravarga'laaga alla, manthaikku maathiriga'laagavum, ka'nkaa'nippuch seyyungga'l.

1Pe5.4 appadich seythaal pirathaana meayppar ve'lippadumpoathu magimaiyu'l'la vaadaatha kireedaththaip pe’ruveerga'l.

1Pe5.5 anthappadi, i'laignarea, moopparukkuk keezhppadiyungga'l. neengga'lellaarum oruvarukkoruvar keezhppadinthu, manaththaazhmaiyai a'ninthuko'l'lungga'l; perumaiyu'l'lavarga'lukkuth theavan ethirththu ni’rki’raar, thaazhmaiyu'l'lavarga'lukkoa kirubai a'likki’raar.

1Pe5.6 aagaiyaal, eat’rakaalaththilea theavan ungga'lai uyarththumpadikku, avarudaiya balaththa kaikku'l adanggiyirungga'l.

1Pe5.7 avar ungga'lai visaarikki’ravaraanapadiyaal, ungga'l kavalaiga'laiyellaam avarmeal vaiththuvidungga'l.

1Pe5.8 the'lintha buththiyu'l'lavarga'laayirungga'l, vizhiththirungga'l; eanenil, ungga'l ethiraa'liyaagiya pisaasaanavan kerchchikki’ra singgampoal evanai vizhunggalaamoa en’ru vagaitheadich sut’riththirigi’raan.

1Pe5.9 visuvaasaththil u’ruthiyaayirunthu, avanukku ethirththu nillungga'l; ulagaththilu'l'la ungga'l sagoathararidaththilea appadippatta paaduga'l ni’raivea’rivarugi’rathen’ru a’rinthirukki’reerga'lea.

1Pe5.10 ki’risthu iyeasuvukku'l nammaith thamathu niththiya magimaikku azhaiththavaraayirukki’ra sagala kirubaiyum porunthiya theavanthaamea kognchakkaalam paadanubavikki’ra ungga'laich seerppaduththi, sthirappaduththi, balappaduththi, nilaini’ruththuvaaraaga;

1Pe5.11 avarukku magimaiyum vallamaiyum sathaakaalangga'lilum u'ndaayiruppathaaga. aamen.

1Pe5.12 ungga'lukkup buththisollumpadikkum, neengga'l nilaiko'ndu ni’rki’ra kirubai theavanudaiya meyyaana kirubaithaanen’ru saadchiyidumpadikkum, naan surukkamaay ungga'lukku ezhuthi, enakkuth thoan’rugi’rapadi u'nmaiyu'l'la sagoatharanaagiya silvaanuvin kaiyilea koduththu anuppiyirukki’rean.

1Pe5.13 ungga'ludaneakoodath therinthuko'l'lappattirukki’ra baabiloanilu'l'la sabaiyum, en kumaaranaagiya maa’rkum ungga'lukku vaazhththuthal sollugi’raarga'l.

1Pe5.14 oruvaraiyoruvar anbin muththaththoadea vaazhththuthal seyyungga'l. ki’risthu iyeasuvukku'l'laana ungga'l anaivarukkum samaathaanamu'ndaavathaaga. aamen.
