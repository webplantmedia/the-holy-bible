Eph1.1 theavanudaiya siththaththinaalea iyeasuki'risthuvinudaiya appoasthalanaagiya pavul, ebeasuvilea ki'risthu iyeasuvukku'l visuvaasiga'laayirukki'ra parisuththavaanga'lukku ezhuthugi'rathaavathu:

Eph1.2 nammudaiya pithaavaagiya theavanaalum, karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvinaalum, ungga'lukkuk kirubaiyum samaathaanamum u'ndaavathaaga.

Eph1.3 nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvin pithaavaagiya theavanukku sthoaththiram; avar ki'risthuvukku'l unnathangga'lilea aavikkuriya sagala aaseervaathaththinaalum nammai aaseervathiththirukki'raar.

Eph1.4 thamakkumunbaaga naam anbil parisuththamu'l'lavarga'lum kut'ramillaathavarga'lumaayiruppatha'rku, avar ulagaththoat'raththukkumunnea ki'risthuvukku'l nammaith therinthuko'ndapadiyea,

Eph1.5 piriyamaanavarukku'l thaam namakkuth thantharu'lina thammudaiya kirubaiyin magimaikkup pugazhchchiyaaga,

Eph1.6 thammudaiya thayavu'l'la siththaththinpadiyea, nammai iyeasuki'risthumoolamaayth thamakkuch suvigaarapuththiraraagumpadi munku'riththirukki'raar.

Eph1.7 avarudaiya kirubaiyin aisuvariyaththinpadiyea ivarudaiya iraththaththinaalea paavamannippaagiya meedpu ivarukku'l namakku u'ndaayirukki'rathu.

Eph1.8 anthak kirubaiyai avar sagala gnaanaththoadum buththiyoadum engga'lidaththil perugappa'n'ninaar.

Eph1.9 kaalangga'l ni'raivea'rumpoathu vi'langgum niyamaththinpadi paraloagaththilirukki'ravaiga'lum booloagaththilirukki'ravaiga'lumaagiya sagalamum ki'risthuvukku'l'lea koottappadavea'ndumen'ru,

Eph1.10 thamakku'l'lea theermaaniththiruntha thammudaiya thayavu'l'la siththaththin iragasiyaththai engga'lukku a'riviththaar.

Eph1.11 mealum ki'risthuvinmeal munnea nambikkaiyaayiruntha naangga'l avarudaiya magimaikkup pugazhchchiyaayirukkumpadikku,

Eph1.12 thamathu siththaththin aaloasanaikkuththakkathaaga ellaavat'raiyum nadappikki'ra avarudaiya theermaanaththinpadiyea, naangga'l munku'rikkappattu, ki'risthuvukku'l avarudaiya suthantharamaagumpadi therinthuko'l'lappattoam.

Eph1.13 neengga'lum ungga'l iradchippin suviseashamaagiya saththiya vasanaththaik keattu, visuvaasiga'laanapoathu, vaakkuththaththampa'n'nappatta parisuththaaaviyaal avarukku'l muththiraipoadappatteerga'l.

Eph1.14 avarukkuch sonthamaanavarga'l avarudaiya magimaikkup pugazhchchiyaaga meedkappaduvaarga'l enbatha'rku aaviyaanavar nammudaiya suthantharaththin achchaaramaayirukki'raar.

Eph1.15 aanapadiyinaalea, karththaraagiya iyeasuvinmealu'l'la ungga'l visuvaasaththaiyum, parisuththavaanga'lellaarmealumu'l'la ungga'l anbaiyungku'riththu naan kea'lvippattu,

Eph1.16 idaividaamal ungga'lukkaaga sthoaththirampa'n'ni, en jebangga'lil ungga'lai ninaiththu,

Eph1.17 nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvin theavanum magimaiyin pithaavumaanavar thammai neengga'l a'rinthuko'lvatha'rkaana gnaanaththaiyum the'livaiyum a'likki'ra aaviyai ungga'lukkuth thantharu'lavea'ndumen'rum,

Eph1.18 thaam nammai azhaiththathinaalea namakku u'ndaayirukki'ra nambikkai innathen'rum, parisuththavaanga'lidaththil thamakku u'ndaayirukki'ra suthantharaththinudaiya magimaiyin aisuvariyam innathen'rum;

Eph1.19 thaam ki'risthuvai mariththoarilirunthu ezhuppi, avaridaththil nadappiththa thamathu balaththa saththuvaththin vallamaippadiyea visuvaasikki'ravarga'laagiya nammidaththilea kaa'nbikkum thammudaiya vallamaiyin mahaa meanmaiyaana magaththuvam innathen'rum, neengga'l a'riyumpadikku, avar ungga'lukkup piragaasamu'l'la manakka'nga'laik kodukkavea'ndumen'rum vea'ndikko'l'lugi'rean.

Eph1.20 ellaath thuraiththanaththukkum, athigaaraththukkum, vallamaikkum, karththaththuvaththukkum, immaiyilmaaththiramalla ma'rumaiyilum pearpet'rirukkum ellaa naamaththukkum mealaay avar uyarnthirukkaththakkathaaga,

Eph1.21 avarai unnathangga'lil thammudaiya valathupaarisaththil udkaarumpadi seythu,

Eph1.22 ellaavat'raiyum avarudaiya paathangga'lukkuk keezhppaduththi,

Eph1.23 ellaavat'raiyum ellaavat'raalum nirappugi'ravarudaiya ni'raivaagiya sareeramaana sabaikku avarai ellaavat'ri'rkum mealaana thalaiyaagath thantharu'linaar.

Eph2.1 akkiramangga'linaalum paavangga'linaalum mariththavarga'laayiruntha ungga'lai uyirppiththaar.

Eph2.2 avaiga'lil neengga'l mu'rkaalaththilea ivvulaga vazhakkaththi'rkeat'rapadiyaagavum, keezhppadiyaamaiyin pi'l'laiga'lidaththil ippozhuthu kiriyaiseygi'ra aagaayaththu athigaarap pirabuvaagiya aavikkeat'rapadiyaagavum nadanthuko'ndeerga'l.

Eph2.3 avarga'lukku'l'lea naamellaarum mu'rkaalaththilea namathu maamsa ichchaiyinpadiyea nadanthu, namathu maamsamum manasum virumbinavaiga'laich seythu, subaavaththinaalea mat'ravarga'laippoalak koabaakkinaiyin pi'l'laiga'laayirunthoam.

Eph2.4 theavanoa irakkaththil aisuvariyamu'l'lavaraay nammil anbukoorntha thammudaiya miguntha anbinaalea,

Eph2.5 akkiramangga'lil mariththavarga'laayiruntha nammaik ki'risthuvudaneakooda uyirppiththaar; kirubaiyinaalea iradchikkappatteerga'l.

Eph2.6 ki'risthu iyeasuvukku'l avar nammidaththil vaiththa thayavinaalea, thammudaiya kirubaiyin mahaa meanmaiyaana aisuvariyaththai varungkaalangga'lil vi'langgachseyvatha'rkaaga,

Eph2.7 ki'risthu iyeasuvukku'l nammai avaroadeakooda ezhuppi, unnathangga'lilea avaroadeakooda udkaaravum seythaar.

Eph2.8 kirubaiyinaalea visuvaasaththaikko'ndu iradchikkappatteerga'l; ithu ungga'laal u'ndaanathalla, ithu theavanudaiya eevu;

Eph2.9 oruvarum perumaipaaraattaathapadikku ithu kiriyaiga'linaal u'ndaanathalla;

Eph2.10 eanenil, na'rkiriyaiga'laich seygi'ratha'rku naam ki'risthu iyeasuvukku'l sirushdikkappattu, theavanudaiya seygaiyaayirukki'roam; avaiga'lil naam nadakkumpadi avar munnathaaga avaiga'lai aayaththampa'n'niyirukki'raar.

Eph2.11 aanapadiyinaal munnea maamsaththinpadi pu'rajaathiyaaraayirunthu, maamsaththil kaiyinaalea seyyappadugi'ra viruththaseathanamudaiyavarga'laal viruththaseathanamillaathavarga'lennappatta neengga'l,

Eph2.12 akkaalaththilea ki'risthuvaich searaathavarga'lum, isravealudaiya kaa'niyaadchikkup pu'rambaanavarga'lum, vaakkuththaththaththin udanpadikkaiga'lukku anniyarum, nambikkaiyillaathavarga'lum, ivvulagaththil theavanat'ravarga'lumaayiruntheerga'len'ru ninaiththukko'l'lungga'l.

Eph2.13 munnea thooramaayiruntha neengga'l ippozhuthu ki'risthu iyeasuvukku'l ki'risthuvin iraththaththinaalea sameebamaaneerga'l.

Eph2.14 eppadiyenil, avarea nammudaiya samaathaana kaara'naraagi, iruthi'raththaaraiyum on'raakki, pagaiyaaga nin'ra pirivinaiyaagiya naduchsuvaraith thagarththu,

Eph2.15 sattathittangga'laagiya niyaayappiramaa'naththaith thammudaiya maamsaththinaalea ozhiththu, iruthi'raththaaraiyum thamakku'l'laaga orea puthiya manushanaagach sirushdiththu, ippadich samaathaanampa'n'ni,

Eph2.16 pagaiyaich siluvaiyinaal kon'ru, athinaalea iruthi'raththaaraiyum orea sareeramaagath theavanukku oppuravaakkinaar.

Eph2.17 allaamalum avar vanthu, thooramaayiruntha ungga'lukkum, sameebamaayiruntha avarga'lukkum, samaathaanaththaich suviseashamaaga a'riviththaar.

Eph2.18 anthappadiyea naam iruthi'raththaarum orea aaviyinaalea pithaavinidaththil searum silaakkiyaththai avarmoolamaayp pet'rirukki'roam.

Eph2.19 aagaiyaal, neengga'l ini anniyarum paratheasiga'lumaayiraamal, parisuththavaanga'loadea orea nagaraththaarum theavanudaiya veettaarumaayirunthu,

Eph2.20 appoasthalar theerkkatharisiga'l enbavarga'ludaiya asthibaaraththinmeal kattappattavarga'lumaayirukki'reerga'l; atha'rku iyeasuki'risthu thaamea moolaikkallaayirukki'raar;

Eph2.21 avarmeal maa'ligai muzhuvathum isaivaay i'naikkappattu, karththarukku'l parisuththa aalayamaaga ezhumbugi'rathu;

Eph2.22 avarmeal neengga'lum aaviyinaalea theavanudaiya vaasasthalamaagak koottikkattappattuvarugi'reerga'l.

Eph3.1 ithinimiththam, pavulaagiya naan pu'rajaathiyaaraayirukki'ra ungga'lporuttuk ki'risthu iyeasuvinimiththam kattu'ndavanaayirukki'rean.

Eph3.2 ungga'lukkaaga enakku a'likkappattirukki'ra theyvakirubaikkuriya niyamamum innathen'ru keattiruppeerga'lea;

Eph3.3 athennavenil pu'rajaathiga'l suviseashaththinaalea udan suthanthararumaay, orea sareeraththi'rku'l'laanavarga'lumaay, ki'risthuvukku'l avar pa'n'nina vaakkuththaththaththukku udanpanggaa'liga'lumaayirukki'raarga'lengi'ra intha iragasiyaththai avar enakku ve'lippaduththi a'riviththaar.

Eph3.4 ithaikku'riththu naan munnamea surukkamaay ezhuthiyirukki'rean.

Eph3.5 athai neengga'l vaasikkaiyil ki'risthuvin iragasiyaththaikku'riththu enakku u'ndaayirukki'ra a'rivai a'rinthuko'l'lalaam;

Eph3.6 intha iragasiyam ippozhuthu avarudaiya parisuththa appoasthalarukkum theerkkatharisiga'lukkum aaviyaanavaraalea ve'lippaduththappattirukki'rathupoala, mu'rkaalangga'lil manupuththirarukku a'rivikkappadavillai.

Eph3.7 theavanudaiya balaththa saththuvaththaal enakku a'likkappatta varamaagiya avarudaiya kirubaiyinaalea inthach suviseashaththukku oozhiyakkaaranaanean.

Eph3.8 parisuththavaanga'lellaarilum si'riyavanaagiya naan ki'risthuvinudaiya a'lavat'ra aisuvariyaththaip pu'rajaathiga'lidaththil suviseashamaay a'rivikki'ratha'rkaaga inthak kirubai enakku a'likkappattirukki'rathu.

Eph3.9 theavan nammudaiya karththaraagiya ki'risthu iyeasuvukku'l ko'ndiruntha anaathi theermaanaththinpadiyea,

Eph3.10 unnathangga'lilu'l'la thuraiththanangga'lukkum athigaarangga'lukkum avarudaiya anantha gnaanamaanathu sabaiyinmoolamaay ippozhuthu theriyavarumporuttaaga,

Eph3.11 iyeasuki'risthuvaikko'ndu ellaavat'raiyum sirushdiththa theavanukku'l'lea aathikaalangga'l muthal ma'rainthiruntha iragasiyaththinudaiya aikkiyam innathen'ru, ellaarukkum ve'lippadaiyaagak kaa'nbikki'ratha'rku, inthak kirubai enakku a'likkappattirukki'rathu.

Eph3.12 avaraip pat'rum visuvaasaththaal avarukku'l namakkuth thairiyamum thidanambikkaiyoadea theavanidaththil searum silaakkiyamum u'ndaayirukki'rathu.

Eph3.13 aagaiyaal ungga'lnimiththam naan anubavikki'ra ubaththiravangga'linaal neengga'l soarnthupoagaathirukka vea'ndikko'l'lugi'rean; avaiga'l ungga'lukku magimaiyaayirukki'rathea.

Eph3.14 ithinimiththam naan paraloagaththilum booloagaththilumu'l'la muzhukkudumbaththukkum naamakaara'naraagiya,

Eph3.15 nammudaiya karththaraayirukki'ra iyeasuki'risthuvinudaiya pithaavai noakki muzhangkaalpadiyittu,

Eph3.16 neengga'l avarudaiya aaviyinaalea u'l'laana manushanil vallamaiyaayp balappadavum,

Eph3.17 visuvaasaththinaalea ki'risthu ungga'l iruthayangga'lil vaasamaayirukkavum, neengga'l anbilea vearoon'ri, nilaipet'ravarga'laagi,

Eph3.18 sagala parisuththavaanga'loadungkoodak ki'risthuvinudaiya anbin agalamum, nee'lamum, aazhamum, uyaramum innathen'ru u'narnthu;

Eph3.19 a'rivukkettaatha antha anbai a'rinthuko'l'la vallavarga'laagavum, theavanudaiya sagala paripoora'naththaalum ni'raiyappadavum, avar thamathu magimaiyinudaiya aisuvariyaththinpadiyea, ungga'lukku anukkiragam pa'n'navea'ndumen'ru vea'ndikko'l'lugi'rean.

Eph3.20 naam vea'ndikko'l'lugi'ratha'rkum ninaikki'ratha'rkum migavum athigamaay namakku'l'lea kiriyaiseygi'ra vallamaiyinpadiyea, namakkuch seyya vallavaraagiya avarukku,

Eph3.21 sabaiyilea ki'risthu iyeasuvin moolamaayth thalaimu'rai thalaimu'raikkum sathaakaalangga'lilum magimai u'ndaavathaaga. aamen.

Eph4.1 aathalaal, karththarnimiththam kattu'ndavanaagiya naan ungga'lukkuch sollugi'ra buththiyennavenil, neengga'l azhaikkappatta azhaippukkup paaththiravaanga'laay nadanthu,

Eph4.2 miguntha manaththaazhmaiyum saanthamum neediya po'rumaiyum udaiyavarga'laay, anbinaal oruvaraiyoruvar thaanggi,

Eph4.3 samaathaanakkattinaal aaviyin orumaiyaik kaaththukko'lvatha'rku jaakkirathaiyaayirungga'l.

Eph4.4 ungga'lukku u'ndaana azhaippinaalea neengga'l orea nambikkaikku azhaikkappattathupoala, orea sareeramum, orea aaviyum u'ndu;

Eph4.5 orea karththarum, orea visuvaasamum, orea gnaanasnaanamum,

Eph4.6 ellaarukkum orea theavanum pithaavum u'ndu; avar ellaarmealum, ellaaroadum, ungga'l ellaarukku'l'lum irukki'ravar.

Eph4.7 ki'risthuvinudaiya eevin a'lavukkuththakkathaaga nammil avanavanukkuk kirubai a'likkappattirukki'rathu.

Eph4.8 aathalaal, avar unnathaththi'rku ea'ri, si'raippattavarga'laich si'raiyaakki, manusharga'lukku varangga'lai a'liththaar en'ru solliyirukki'raar.

Eph4.9 ea'rinaar enbathinaalea avar atha'rkumunnea boomiyin thaazhvidangga'lil i'rangginaar en'ru vi'langgugi'rathallavaa?

Eph4.10 i'rangginavarea ellaavat'raiyum nirappaththakkathaaga, ellaa vaanangga'lukkum mealaaga unnathaththi'rku ea'rinavarumaayirukki'raar.

Eph4.11 mealum naam anaivarum theavanudaiya kumaaranaip pat'rum visuvaasaththilum a'rivilum orumaippattavarga'laagi, ki'risthuvinudaiya ni'raivaana va'larchchiyin a'lavukkuththakka poora'napurusharaagumvaraikkum,

Eph4.12 parisuththavaanga'l seerporunthumporuttu, suviseasha oozhiyaththin vealaikkaagavum, ki'risthuvin sareeramaagiya sabaiyaanathu bakthiviruththi adaivatha'rkaagavum,

Eph4.13 avar, silarai appoasthalaraagavum, silaraith theerkkatharisiga'laagavum, silaraich suviseashagaraagavum, silarai meaypparaagavum poathagaraagavum ea'rpaduththinaar.

Eph4.14 naam inik kuzhanthaiga'laayiraamal, manusharudaiya soothum vagnchikki'ratha'rkeathuvaana thanthiramumu'l'la poathagamaagiya palavitha kaat'rinaalea alaiga'laippoala adipattu alaigi'ravarga'laayiraamal,

Eph4.15 anbudan saththiyaththaik kaikko'ndu, thalaiyaagiya ki'risthuvukku'l ellaavat'rileayum, naam va'larugi'ravarga'laayirukkumpadiyaaga appadich seythaar.

Eph4.16 avaraalea sareeram muzhuthum, atha'rku uthaviyaayirukki'ra sagala ka'nukka'linaalum isaivaayk kootti i'naikkappattu, ovvoru avayavamum thanthan a'lavukkuththakkathaayk kiriyaiseygi'rapadiyea, athu anbinaalea thanakkup bakthiviruththi u'ndaakkugi'ratha'rkeathuvaagach sareerava'larchchiyai u'ndaakkugi'rathu.

Eph4.17 aathalaal, karththarukku'l naan ungga'lukkuch saadchiyaagach solli echcharikki'rathu ennavenil, mat'rap pu'rajaathiga'l thangga'l vee'naana sinthaiyilea nadakki'rathupoala neengga'l ini nadavaamalirungga'l.

Eph4.18 avarga'l buththiyil anthagaarappattu, thangga'l iruthayakadinaththinaal thangga'lil irukkum a'riyaamaiyinaalea theavanudaiya jeevanukku anniyaraayirunthu;

Eph4.19 u'narvillaathavarga'laay, sagalavitha asuththangga'laiyum aavaloadea nadappikkumpadi, thangga'laik kaamavigaaraththi'rku oppukkoduththirukki'raarga'l.

Eph4.20 neengga'loa ivvithamaayk ki'risthuvaik kat'rukko'l'lavillai.

Eph4.21 iyeasuvinidaththilu'l'la saththiyaththinpadiyea, neengga'l avaridaththil keatta'rinthu, avaraal poathikkappatteerga'lea.

Eph4.22 anthappadi, munthina nadakkaikkuriya moasampoakkum ichchaiga'laalea kettuppoagi'ra pazhaiya manushanai neengga'l ka'lainthupoattu,

Eph4.23 ungga'l u'l'laththilea puthithaana aaviyu'l'lavarga'laagi,

Eph4.24 meyyaana neethiyilum parisuththaththilum theavanudaiya saayalaagach sirushdikkappatta puthiya manushanaith thariththukko'l'lungga'l.

Eph4.25 an'riyum, naam oruvarukkoruvar avayavangga'laayirukki'rapadiyaal, poyyaik ka'lainthu, avanavan pi'ranudanea meyyaip peasakkadavan.

Eph4.26 neengga'l koabangko'ndaalum paavagnseyyaathirungga'l; sooriyan asthamikki'ratha'rku munnaaga ungga'l erichchal tha'niyakkadavathu;

Eph4.27 pisaasukku idangkodaamalum irungga'l.

Eph4.28 thirudugi'ravan inith thirudaamal, ku'raichchalu'l'lavanukkuk kodukkaththakkathaagath thanakku u'ndaayirukkumpadi, than kaiga'linaal nalamaana vealaiseythu, pirayaasappadakkadavan.

Eph4.29 ketta vaarththai on'rum ungga'l vaayilirunthu pu'rappadavea'ndaam; bakthiviruththikku eathuvaana nalla vaarththai u'ndaanaal athaiyea keadki'ravarga'lukkup pirayoajanamu'ndaagumpadi peasungga'l.

Eph4.30 an'riyum, neengga'l meedkappadum naa'lukken'ru muththiraiyaagap pet'ra theavanudaiya parisuththaaaviyaith thukkappaduththaathirungga'l.

Eph4.31 sagalavithamaana kasappum, koabamum, moorkkamum, kookkuralum, thoosha'namum, mat'ra enthath thurkku'namum ungga'laivittu neenggakkadavathu.

Eph4.32 oruvarukkoruvar thayavaayum manaurukkamaayum irunthu, ki'risthuvukku'l theavan ungga'lukku manniththathupoala, neengga'lum oruvarukkoruvar manniyungga'l.

Eph5.1 aathalaal, neengga'l piriyamaana pi'l'laiga'laippoalath theavanaip pinpat'rugi'ravarga'laagi,

Eph5.2 ki'risthu namakkaagath thammaith theavanukkuch sugantha vaasanaiyaana kaa'nikkaiyaagavum baliyaagavum oppukkoduththu nammil anbukoornthathupoala, neengga'lum anbilea nadanthuko'l'lungga'l.

Eph5.3 mealum, parisuththavaanga'lukku eat'rapadi, veasiththanamum, mat'rentha asuththamum, poru'laasaiyum aagiya ivaiga'lin pearmuthalaay ungga'lukku'l'lea sollappadavungkoodaathu.

Eph5.4 appadiyea vambum, buththiyeenamaana peachchum, pariyaasamum thagaathavaiga'l; sthoaththiragnseythalea thagum.

Eph5.5 vibasaarakkaaranaavathu, asuththanaavathu, vikkiragaaraathanaikkaaranaagiya poru'laasaikkaaranaavathu theavanudaiya raajyamaagiya ki'risthuvin raajyaththilea suthantharamadaivathillaiyen'ru a'rinthirukki'reerga'lea.

Eph5.6 ippadippattavaiga'linimiththamaagak keezhppadiyaamaiyin pi'l'laiga'lmeal theavakoabaakkinai varuvathaal, oruvanum ungga'lai vee'nvaarththaiga'linaalea moasampoakkaathapadikku echcharikkaiyaayirungga'l;

Eph5.7 avarga'lukkup panggaa'liga'laagaathirungga'l.

Eph5.8 mu'rkaalaththil neengga'l anthagaaramaayiruntheerga'l, ippozhuthoa karththarukku'l ve'lichchamaayirukki'reerga'l; ve'lichchaththin pi'l'laiga'laay nadanthuko'l'lungga'l.

Eph5.9 aaviyin kani, sagala na'rku'naththilum neethiyilum u'nmaiyilum vi'langgum.

Eph5.10 karththarukkup piriyamaanathu innathen'ru neengga'l soathiththuppaarungga'l.

Eph5.11 kaniyat'ra anthagaarak kiriyaiga'lukku udanpadaamal, avaiga'laik kadinthuko'l'lungga'l.

Eph5.12 avarga'laal o'lippidaththil seyyappadum kiriyaiga'laich sollugi'rathum avaladcha'namaayirukki'rathea.

Eph5.13 avaiga'lellaam kadinthuko'l'lappattu ve'lichchaththinaal ve'liyaranggamaagum; ve'liyaranggamaakkugi'rathellaam ve'lichchamaayirukki'rathu.

Eph5.14 aathalaal, thoonggugi'ra nee vizhiththu, mariththoarai vittu ezhunthiru, appozhuthu ki'risthu unnaip piragaasippippaaren'ru solliyirukki'raar.

Eph5.15 aanapadiyinaalea, neengga'l gnaanamat'ravarga'laippoala nadavaamal, gnaanamu'l'lavarga'laippoalak kavanamaay nadanthuko'l'lappaarththu,

Eph5.16 naadka'l pollaathavaiga'laanathaal kaalaththaip pirayoajanappaduththikko'l'lungga'l.

Eph5.17 aagaiyaal, neengga'l mathiyat'ravarga'laayiraamal, karththarudaiya siththam innathen'ru u'narnthuko'l'lungga'l.

Eph5.18 thunmaarkkaththi'rku eathuvaana mathubaana ve'riko'l'laamal, aaviyinaal ni'rainthu;

Eph5.19 sanggeethangga'linaalum keerththanaiga'linaalum gnaanappaattuga'linaalum oruvarukkoruvar buththisollikko'ndu, ungga'l iruthayaththil karththaraip paadik keerththanampa'n'ni,

Eph5.20 nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvin naamaththinaalea eppozhuthum ellaavat'ri'rkaagavum pithaavaagiya theavanai sthoaththiriththu,

Eph5.21 theyva bayaththoadea oruvarukkoruvar keezhppadinthirungga'l.

Eph5.22 manaiviga'lea, karththarukkuk keezhppadigi'rathupoala, ungga'l sonthap purusharukkung keezhppadiyungga'l.

Eph5.23 ki'risthu sabaikkuth thalaiyaayirukki'rathupoala, purushanum manaivikkuth thalaiyaayirukki'raan; avarea sareeraththi'rkum iradchagaraayirukki'raar.

Eph5.24 aagaiyaal, sabaiyaanathu ki'risthuvukkuk keezhppadigi'rathupoala manaiviga'lum thangga'l sonthap purusharga'lukku enthak kaariyaththileayum keezhppadinthirukkavea'ndum.

Eph5.25 purusharga'lea, ungga'l manaiviga'lil anbukoorungga'l; appadiyea ki'risthuvum sabaiyil anbukoornthu,

Eph5.26 thaam athaith thiruvasanaththaikko'ndu tha'n'neer muzhukkinaal suththigariththu, parisuththamaakkugi'ratha'rkum,

Eph5.27 ka'raithirai muthalaanavaiga'l on'rum illaamal parisuththamum pizhaiyat'rathumaana magimaiyu'l'la sabaiyaaga athaith thamakkumun ni'ruththikko'lvatha'rkum thammaiththaamea atha'rkaaga oppukkoduththaar.

Eph5.28 appadiyea, purusharga'lum thangga'l manaiviga'laith thangga'l sonthach sareerangga'laagap paaviththu, avarga'lil anbukooravea'ndum; than manaiviyil anbukoorugi'ravan thannilthaan anbukoorugi'raan.

Eph5.29 than sontha maamsaththaip pagaiththavan oruvanumillaiyea; karththar sabaiyaip poashiththuk kaappaat'rugi'rathupoala ovvoruvanum than maamsaththaip poashiththuk kaappaat'rugi'raan.

Eph5.30 naam avarudaiya sareeraththin avayavangga'laayum, avarudaiya maamsaththi'rkum avarudaiya elumbuga'lukkum uriyavarga'laayum irukki'roam.

Eph5.31 ithinimiththam manushan than thagappanaiyum than thaayaiyum vittu, than manaiviyudan isainthu, iruvarum orea maamsamaayiruppaarga'l.

Eph5.32 intha iragasiyam periyathu; naan ki'risthuvaippat'riyum sabaiyaippat'riyum sollugi'rean.

Eph5.33 eppadiyum, ungga'lilum avanavan thannidaththil anbukooruvathupoala, than manaiviyinidaththilum anbukoorakkadavan; manaiviyum purushanidaththil bayabakthiyaayirukkakkadava'l.

Eph6.1 pi'l'laiga'lea, ungga'l pet'raarukkuk karththarukku'l keezhppadiyungga'l, ithu niyaayam.

Eph6.2 unakku nanmai u'ndaayiruppatha'rkum, boomiyilea un vaazhnaa'l neediththiruppatha'rkum,

Eph6.3 un thagappanaiyum un thaayaiyum kanampa'n'nuvaayaaga enbathea vaakkuththaththamu'l'la muthalaang ka'rpanaiyaayirukki'rathu.

Eph6.4 pithaakka'lea, neengga'lum ungga'l pi'l'laiga'laik koabappaduththaamal, karththarukkeat'ra sidchaiyilum poathanaiyilum avarga'lai va'larppeerga'laaga.

Eph6.5 vealaikkaararea, neengga'l ki'risthuvukkuk keezhppadigi'rathupoala, sareeraththinpadi ungga'l ejamaanga'laayirukki'ravarga'lukkum bayaththoadum nadukkaththoadum kabadat'ra manathoadum keezhppadinthu;

Eph6.6 manusharukkup piriyamaayirukka virumbugi'ravarga'laagap paarvaikku oozhiyagnseyyaamal, ki'risthuvin oozhiyakkaararaaga, manappoorvamaayth theavanudaiya siththaththinpadi seyyungga'l.

Eph6.7 adimaiyaanavanaanaalum, suyaatheenamu'l'lavanaanaalum, avanavan seygi'ra nanmaiyinpadiyea karththaridaththil palanai adaivaanen'ru a'rinthu,

Eph6.8 manusharukken'ru oozhiyagnseyyaamal, karththarukken'rea nalmanathoadea oozhiyagnseyyungga'l.

Eph6.9 ejamaanga'lea, appadiyea neengga'lum, vealaikkaararukkuch seyyavea'ndiyavaiga'laich seythu, avarga'lukkum ungga'lukkum ejamaanaanavar paraloagaththil irukki'raaren'rum, avaridaththil padchapaatham illaiyen'rum a'rinthu, kadugnsollai vittuvidungga'l.

Eph6.10 kadaisiyaaga, en sagoathararea, karththarilum avarudaiya saththuvaththin vallamaiyilum balappadungga'l.

Eph6.11 neengga'l pisaasin thanthirangga'loadu ethirththuni'rkath thiraa'niyu'l'lavarga'laagumpadi, theavanudaiya sarvaayuthavarkkaththaiyum thariththukko'l'lungga'l.

Eph6.12 eanenil, maamsaththoadum iraththaththoadumalla, thuraiththanangga'loadum, athigaarangga'loadum, ippirapagnchaththin anthagaara loagaathibathiga'loadum, vaanama'ndalangga'lilu'l'la pollaatha aaviga'lin seanaiga'loadum namakkup poaraattam u'ndu.

Eph6.13 aagaiyaal, theenggunaa'lilea avaiga'lai neengga'l ethirkkavum, sagalaththaiyum seythumudiththavarga'laay ni'rkavum thiraa'niyu'l'lavarga'laagumpadikku, theavanudaiya sarvaayuthavarkkaththaiyum eduththukko'l'lungga'l.

Eph6.14 saththiyam ennum kachchaiyai ungga'l araiyil kattinavarga'laayum, neethiyennum maarkkavasaththaith thariththavarga'laayum;

Eph6.15 samaathaanaththin suviseashaththi'rkuriya aayaththam ennum paatharadchaiyaik kaalga'lilea thoduththavarga'laayum;

Eph6.16 pollaanggan eyyum akkiniyaasthirangga'laiyellaam aviththuppoadaththakkathaay, ellaavat'ri'rkum mealaaga visuvaasamennum keadagaththaip pidiththukko'ndavarga'laayum nillungga'l.

Eph6.17 iradcha'niyamennum thalaichseeraavaiyum, theavavasanamaagiya aaviyin pattayaththaiyum eduththukko'l'lungga'l.

Eph6.18 enthach samayaththilum sagalavithamaana vea'nduthaloadum vi'n'nappaththoadum aaviyinaalea jebampa'n'ni, athanporuttu miguntha manau'ruthiyoadum sagala parisuththavaanga'lukkaagavum pa'n'num vea'nduthaloadum vizhiththukko'ndirungga'l.

Eph6.19 suviseashaththi'rkaagach sanggiliyaal kattappattirukki'ra sthaanaabathiyaagiya naan athaippat'rip peasavea'ndiyapadi thairiyamaayp peasaththakkathaaga,

Eph6.20 naan thairiyamaay en vaayaith thi'ranthu suviseashaththin iragasiyaththai a'rivikki'ratha'rku vaakku enakkuk kodukkappadumpadi enakkaagavum vi'n'nappampa'n'nungga'l.

Eph6.21 an'riyum, enakkaduththa kaariyangga'lum, en sugaseythiga'lum ungga'lukkuth theriyavarumpadikku, avaiga'laiyellaam namakkup piriyamaana sagoatharanum karththarukku'l u'nmaiyu'l'la oozhiyakkaaranumaayirukki'ra theegikku ungga'lukku a'rivippaan.

Eph6.22 neengga'l engga'l seythiga'lai a'riyavum, avan ungga'l iruthayangga'lukku aa'ruthal seyyavum, avanai ungga'lidaththil anuppinean.

Eph6.23 pithaavaagiya theavanaalum karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvinaalum, sagoathararukkuch samaathaanamum visuvaasaththoadu koodiya anbum u'ndaavathaaga.

Eph6.24 nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvinidaththil azhiyaatha anbudanea anbukoorugi'ra yaavaroadum kirubai u'ndaayiruppathaaga. aamen.
