Phm1.1 ki’risthu iyeasuvinimiththam kattappattavanaayirukki’ra pavulum, sagoatharanaagiya theemoaththeayum, engga'lukkup piriyamu'l'lavanum udanvealaiyaa'lumaayirukki’ra pileamoanukkum,

Phm1.2 piriyamu'l'la appiyaa'lukkum, engga'l udan poarchseavaganaagiya arkkippuvukkum, ummudaiya veettilea koodivarugi’ra sabaikkum ezhuthugi’rathaavathu:

Phm1.3 nammudaiya pithaavaagiya theavanaalum, karththaraagiya iyeasuki’risthuvinaalum, ungga'lukkuk kirubaiyum samaathaanamum u'ndaavathaaga.

Phm1.4 karththaraagiya iyeasuvinidaththilum, ellaap parisuththavaanga'lidaththilumu'l'la ummudaiya anbaiyum ummudaiya visuvaasaththaiyum naan kea'lvippattu,

Phm1.5 en jebangga'lil ummai ninaiththu, eppozhuthum en theavanukku sthoaththiragnseythu,

Phm1.6 ungga'lilu'l'la sagala nanmaiga'lum theriyappadugi’rathinaalea ummudaiya visuvaasaththin anniyoanniyam ki’risthuiyeasuvukkaagap payanpadavea'ndumen’ru vea'nduthalseygi’rean.

Phm1.7 sagoatharanea, parisuththavaanga'ludaiya u'l'langga'l ummaalea i'laippaa’rinapadiyaal, ummudaiya anbinaalea miguntha santhoashamum aa’ruthalum adainthirukki’roam.

Phm1.8 aagaiyaal, pavulaagiya naan muthirvayathu'l'lavanaagavum, iyeasuki’risthuvinimiththam ippozhuthu kattappattavanaagavum irukki’rapadiyaal,

Phm1.9 neer seyyaththakkathai umakkuk katta'laiyidumpadikkuk ki’risthuvukku'l naan thu'niyaththakkavanaayirunthaalum, appadich seyyaamal, anbinimiththam man’raadugi’rean.

Phm1.10 ennaven’raal, kattappattirukkaiyil naan pet’ra en maganaagiya oneasimukkaaga ummai man’raadugi’rean.

Phm1.11 munnea avan umakkup pirayoajanamillaathavan, ippozhuthoa umakkum enakkum pirayoajanamu'l'lavan.

Phm1.12 avanai naan ummidaththi’rku anuppugi’rean; en u'l'lampoalirukki’ra avanai neer eat’rukko'l'lum.

Phm1.13 suviseashaththinimiththam kattappattirukki’ra enakku oozhiyagnseyyumpadi umakkup bathilaaga avanai ennidaththil vaiththukko'l'lavea'ndumen’rirunthean.

Phm1.14 aanaalum neer seyyum nanmaiyaik kattaayaththinaalalla, manappoorvamaaych seyyaththakkathaaga, naan ummudaiya sammathiyillaamal on’rum seyya enakku manathillai.

Phm1.15 avan en’ren’raikkum ummudaiyavanaayirukkumpadikkum, inimeal avan adimaiyaanavanaagavalla, adimaiyaanavanukku mealaanavanaagavum piriyamu'l'la sagoatharanaagavumirukkumpadikkum kognchakkaalam ummaivittup pirinthupoanaanaakkum.

Phm1.16 enakku avan piriyamaana sagoatharanaanaal, umakkuch sareeraththinpadiyeayum karththarukku'l'lum evva'lavu piriyamu'l'lavanaayirukkavea'ndum!

Phm1.17 aathalaal, neer ennai ummoadea aikkiyamaanavanen’ru e'n'ninaal, ennai eat’rukko'lvathupoala avanaiyum eat’rukko'l'lum.

Phm1.18 avan umakku yaathoru aniyaayagnseythathum, ummidaththil kadanpattathum u'ndaanaal, athai en ka'nakkilea vaiththukko'l'lum.

Phm1.19 pavulaagiya naan ithai en sonthakkaiyaalea ezhuthinean, naan athaich seluththith theerppean. neer ummaiththaamea enakkuk kadanaagach seluththavea'ndumen’ru naan umakkuch sollavea'ndiyathillaiyea.

Phm1.20 aam, sagoatharanea, karththarukku'l ummaalea enakkup pirayoajanamu'ndaagattum; karththarukku'l en u'l'laththai i'laippaa’rappa'n'num.

Phm1.21 naan sollugi’rathilum athigamaaych seyveeren’ru a’rinthu, itha’rku neer i'nangguveeren’ru nichchayiththu, umakku ezhuthiyirukki’rean.

Phm1.22 mealum, ungga'l vi'n'nappangga'linaalea naan ungga'lukku anukkiragikkappaduveanen’ru nambiyirukki’rapadiyaal, naan irukkumpadikku oaridaththai enakkaaga aayaththampa'n'num.

Phm1.23 ki’risthu iyeasuvinimiththam ennoadeakoodak kaavalil vaikkappattirukki’ra eppaappiraavum,

Phm1.24 en udanvealaiyaadka'laagiya maa’rkuvum, aristharkkum, theamaavum, lookkaavum umakku vaazhththuthal sollugi’raarga'l.

Phm1.25 nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki’risthuvin kirubai ungga'l aaviyudaneakooda iruppathaaga. aamen.
