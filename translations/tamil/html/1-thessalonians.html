1Th1.1 pavulum, silvaanum, theemoaththeayum, pithaavaagiya theavanukku'l'lum karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvukku'l'lum irukki'ra thesaloanikkeayar sabaikku ezhuthugi'rathaavathu: nammudaiya pithaavaagiya theavanaalum karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvinaalum ungga'lukkuk kirubaiyum samaathaanamum u'ndaavathaaga.

1Th1.2 theavanukkup piriyamaana sagoathararea, ungga'l visuvaasaththin kiriyaiyaiyum, ungga'l anbin pirayaasaththaiyum, nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvinmealu'l'la ungga'l nambikkaiyin po'rumaiyaiyum, nammudaiya pithaavaagiya theavanukkumunbaaga naangga'l idaividaamal ninaivukoornthu,

1Th1.3 neengga'l therinthuko'l'lappattavarga'len'ru naangga'l a'rinthu,

1Th1.4 engga'l jebangga'lil idaividaamal ungga'laikku'riththu vi'n'nappampa'n'ni, ungga'lellaarukkaagavum eppozhuthum theavanai sthoaththirikki'roam.

1Th1.5 engga'l suviseasham ungga'lidaththil vasanaththoadeamaaththiramalla, vallamaiyoadum, parisuththa aaviyoadum, muzhu nichchayaththoadum vanthathu; naangga'lum ungga'lukku'l'lea irunthapoathu ungga'lnimiththam eppadippattavarga'laayirunthoamen'ru a'rinthirukki'reerga'lea.

1Th1.6 neengga'l miguntha ubaththiravaththilea, parisuththa aaviyin santhoashaththoadea, thiruvasanaththai eat'rukko'ndu, engga'laiyum karththaraiyum pinpat'rugi'ravarga'laagi,

1Th1.7 ivvithamaay makkethoaniyaavilum akaayaavilumu'l'la visuvaasiga'l yaavarukkum maathiriga'laaneerga'l.

1Th1.8 eppadiyenil, ungga'lidaththilirunthu karththarudaiya vasanam makkethoaniyaavilum akaayaavilum thoniththathumallaamal, naangga'l athaikku'riththu on'rum sollavea'ndiyathaayiraathapadikku, theavanaippat'rina ungga'l visuvaasam enggum pirasiththamaayit'ru.

1Th1.9 eanenil, avarga'lthaamea engga'laikku'riththu, ungga'lidaththil naangga'l adaintha piraveasam innathenbathaiyum, jeevanu'l'la meyyaana theavanukku oozhiyagnseyvatha'rku, neengga'l vikkiragangga'laivittuth theavanidaththi'rku mananthirumbinathaiyum,

1Th1.10 avar mariththoarilirunthezhuppinavarum, inivarum koabaakkinaiyinin'ru nammai neenggalaakki iradchikki'ravarumaayirukki'ra avarudaiya kumaaranaagiya iyeasu paraloagaththilirunthu varuvathai neengga'l ethirpaarththukko'ndirukki'rathaiyum, a'rivikki'raarga'lea.

1Th2.1 sagoathararea, naangga'l ungga'lidaththil piraveasiththathu vee'naayirukkavillaiyen'ru neengga'lea a'rinthirukki'reerga'l.

1Th2.2 ungga'lukkuth therinthirukki'rapadi, munnea pilippipatta'naththilea naangga'l paadupattu ninthaiyadainthirunthum, vegu poaraattaththoadea theavanudaiya suviseashaththai ungga'lukkuch sollumpadi, nammudaiya theavanukku'l thairiyangko'ndirunthoam.

1Th2.3 engga'l poathagam vagnchagaththinaalum thuraasaiyinaalum u'ndaagavillai, athu kabadamu'l'lathaayumirukkavillai.

1Th2.4 suviseashaththai engga'lidaththil oppuvikkaththakkathaay, theavan engga'lai uththamaren're'n'ninapadiyea, naangga'l manusharukku alla, engga'l iruthayangga'laich soathiththa'rigi'ra theavanukkea piriyamu'ndaagap peasugi'roam.

1Th2.5 ungga'lukkuth therinthirukki'rapadi, naangga'l orukkaalum ichchagamaana vasanangga'laich sollavumillai, poru'laasaiyu'l'lavarga'laay maayampa'n'navumillai; theavanea saadchi.

1Th2.6 naangga'l ki'risthuvin appoasthalaraaga ungga'lukkup baaramaayirukkak koodiyavarga'laanaalum, ungga'lidaththilaavathu, mat'ravarga'lidaththilaavathu, manusharaal varum magimaiyai naangga'l theadavillai.

1Th2.7 ungga'lidaththil padchamaay nadanthuko'ndoam; paal kodukki'ra thaayaanava'l than pi'l'laiga'laik kaappaat'rugi'rathupoala,

1Th2.8 naangga'l ungga'lmeal vaagnchaiyaayirunthu, theavanudaiya suviseashaththai ungga'lukkuk koduththathumallaamal, neengga'l engga'lukkup piriyamaanavarga'laanapadiyinaalea, engga'l jeevanaiyum ungga'lukkuk kodukka manathaayirunthoam.

1Th2.9 sagoathararea, naangga'l patta pirayaasamum varuththamum ungga'lukku gnaabagamaayirukkum; ungga'lil oruvanukkum baaramaayiraathapadikku, iravum pagalum naangga'l vealaiseythu, theavanudaiya suviseashaththai ungga'lidaththil pirasanggiththoam.

1Th2.10 visuvaasiga'laagiya ungga'lukku'l'lea naangga'l evva'lavu parisuththamum neethiyum pizhaiyinmaiyumaay nadanthoamenbatha'rku neengga'lum saadchi, theavanum saadchi.

1Th2.11 mealum, thammudaiya raajyaththi'rkum magimaikkum ungga'lai azhaiththa theavanukku neengga'l paaththiraraay nadakkavea'ndumen'ru,

1Th2.12 thagappan than pi'l'laiga'lukkuch sollugi'rathupoala, naangga'l ungga'lil ovvoruvanukkum buththiyum thea'ruthalum echcharippum sonnathai a'rinthirukki'reerga'l.

1Th2.13 aagaiyaal, neengga'l theavavasanaththai engga'laalea kea'lvippattu eat'rukko'ndapoathu, athai manushar vasanamaaga eat'rukko'l'laamal, theavavasanamaagavea eat'rukko'ndathinaalea naangga'l idaividaamal theavanukku sthoaththiragn seluththugi'roam; athu meyyaagavea theavavasananthaan, visuvaasikki'ra ungga'lukku'l'lea athu belanum seygi'rathu.

1Th2.14 eppadiyenil, sagoathararea, yootheayaa theasaththil ki'risthu iyeasuvukku'l'laana theavanudaiya sabaiga'lai neengga'l pinpat'rinavarga'laagi, avarga'l yootharaalea eppadip paadupattaarga'loa, appadiyea neengga'lum ungga'l suya janangga'laalea paadupatteerga'l.

1Th2.15 antha yootharga'l karththaraagiya iyeasuvaiyum, thangga'l theerkkatharisiga'laiyum kolaiseythavarga'lum, engga'laith thunbappaduththinavarga'lum, theavanukkea'rkaathavarga'lum, manushar yaavarukkum viroathiga'lumaayirunthu,

1Th2.16 pu'rajaathiyaar iradchikkappaduvatha'rku naangga'l avarga'loadea peasaathapadikkuth thadaipa'n'nugi'raarga'l; ivvithamaay ekkaalaththilum thangga'l paavangga'lai ni'raivaakkugi'raarga'l; avarga'lmeal koabaakkinai poora'namaay vanthirukki'rathu.

1Th2.17 sagoathararea, naangga'l iruthayaththinpadi ungga'loadirunthu, sareeraththinpadi kognchakkaalam ungga'laivittup pirinthirunthapadiyinaalea, ungga'l mugaththaip paarkkavea'ndumen'ru miguntha aasaiyoadea athigamaayp pirayaththanampa'n'ninoam.

1Th2.18 aagaiyaal, naangga'l ungga'lidaththil vara ira'ndorutharam manathaayirunthoam, pavulaagiya naanea vara manathaayirunthean; saaththaanoa engga'laith thadaipa'n'ninaan.

1Th2.19 engga'lukku nambikkaiyum santhoashamum magizhchchiyin kireedamumaayiruppavarga'l yaar? nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthu varumpoathu avarudaiya sannithaanaththilea neengga'lallavaa appadiyiruppeerga'l;

1Th2.20 neengga'lea engga'lukku magimaiyum santhoashamumaayirukki'reerga'l.

1Th3.1 aagaiyaal, naangga'l inip po'ruththirukkakkoodaamal, aththeanea patta'naththil thaniththuvidappadugi'rathu nanmaiyen'ru ninaiththu,

1Th3.2 intha ubaththiravangga'linaalea oruvanum asaikkappadaathapadikku ungga'laith thidappaduththavum, ungga'l visuvaasaththaippat'ri ungga'lukkup buththisollavum, nammudaiya sagoatharanum theava oozhiyakkaaranum ki'risthuvin suviseashaththil engga'l udanvealaiyaa'lumaagiya theemoaththeayuvai anuppinoam.

1Th3.3 ippadippatta ubaththiravangga'laich sagikka naam niyamikkappattirukki'roamen'ru neengga'l a'rinthirukki'reerga'lea.

1Th3.4 namakku ubaththiravam varumen'ru naangga'l ungga'lidaththilirunthapoathu, ungga'lukku munna'riviththoam; appadiyea vanthu nearittathen'rum a'rinthirukki'reerga'l.

1Th3.5 aagaiyaal, naan inip po'ruththirukkakkoodaamal, engga'l vealai vee'naayp poagaththakkathaagach soathanaikkaaran ungga'laich soathanaikkudpaduththinathu'ndoaven'ru, ungga'l visuvaasaththai a'riyumpadikku, avanai anuppinean.

1Th3.6 ippozhuthu theemoaththeayu ungga'lidaththilirunthu engga'lidaththil vanthu, ungga'l visuvaasaththaiyum anbaiyum ku'riththum, neengga'l eppozhuthum engga'laip padchamaay ninaiththukko'ndu, naangga'l ungga'laik kaa'na vaagnchaiyaayirukki'rathupoala neengga'lum engga'laik kaa'na vaagnchaiyaayirukki'reerga'lenbathaikku'riththum, engga'lukku na'rseythi sonnathinaalea,

1Th3.7 sagoathararea, engga'lukku nearitta ellaa ikkattilum ubaththiravaththilum ungga'l visuvaasaththinaalea ungga'laikku'riththu aa'ruthaladainthoam.

1Th3.8 neengga'l karththarukku'l nilaiththirunthaal naangga'l pizhaiththiruppoam.

1Th3.9 mealum, nammudaiya theavanukku munbaaga naangga'l ungga'laikku'riththu adainthirukki'ra miguntha santhoashaththi'rkaaga, naangga'l theavanukku evvithamaay sthoaththiram seluththuvoam?

1Th3.10 ungga'l mugaththaik ka'ndu, ungga'l visuvaasaththin ku'raivuga'lai ni'raivaakkumporuttu, iravum pagalum migavum vea'ndikko'l'lugi'roamea.

1Th3.11 nammudaiya pithaavaagiya theavanum nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvum ungga'lidaththi'rku engga'lai nearaaga vazhinadaththuvaaraaga.

1Th3.12 naangga'l ungga'lidaththil vaiththirukki'ra anbukkoppaay, neengga'lum oruvaridaththil oruvar vaikkum anbilum mat'rellaa manusharidaththil vaikkum anbilum karththar ungga'laip perugavum nilaiththoanggavum seythu,

1Th3.13 ivvithamaay nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthu thamathu parisuththavaanga'l anaivaroadungkooda varumpoathu, neengga'l nammudaiya pithaavaagiya theavanukku munbaagap pizhaiyat'ra parisuththamu'l'lavarga'laayirukkumpadi ungga'l iruthayangga'lai sthirappaduththuvaaraaga.

1Th4.1 an'riyum, sagoathararea, neengga'l inninna piragaaramaay nadakkavum, theavanukkup piriyamaayirukkavum vea'ndumen'ru, neengga'l engga'laal keattu eat'rukko'ndapadiyea, athigamathigamaayth thea'rumpadikku, karththaraagiya iyeasuvukku'l ungga'lai vea'ndikko'ndu buththisollugi'roam.

1Th4.2 karththaraagiya iyeasuvinaalea naangga'l ungga'lukkuk koduththa katta'laiga'lai a'rinthirukki'reerga'lea.

1Th4.3 neengga'l parisuththamu'l'lavarga'laagavea'ndumenbathea theavanudaiya siththamaayirukki'rathu. anthappadi, neengga'l veasimaarkkaththukku vilagiyirunthu,

1Th4.4 theavanai a'riyaatha agngnaaniga'laippoala moaga ichchaikkudpadaamal,

1Th4.5 ungga'lil avanavan thanthan sareera paa'ndaththaip parisuththamaayum kanamaayum aa'nduko'l'lumpadi a'rinthu:

1Th4.6 intha vishayaththil oruvanum mee'raamalum than sagoatharanai vagnchiyaamalum irukkavea'ndum; munnamea naangga'l ungga'lukkuch solli, saadchiyaaga echchariththapadiyea ippadippatta vishayangga'lellaavat'raiyungku'riththuk karththar neethiyaich sarikkattugi'ravaraayirukki'raar.

1Th4.7 theavan nammai asuththaththi'rkalla parisuththaththi'rkea azhaiththirukki'raar.

1Th4.8 aagaiyaal asattaipa'n'nugi'ravan manusharai alla, thamathu parisuththaaaviyai namakkuth thantharu'lina theavanaiyea asattaipa'n'nugi'raan.

1Th4.9 sagoathara sineagaththaikku'riththu naan ungga'lukku ezhuthavea'nduvathillai; neengga'l oruvariloruvar anbaayirukkumpadikkuth theavanaal poathikkappattavarga'laayirukki'reerga'lea.

1Th4.10 anthappadi neengga'l makkethoaniyaa naadenggumu'l'la sagoathararellaarukkum seythuvarugi'reerga'l. sagoathararea, anbilea neengga'l innum athigamaayp perugavum;

1Th4.11 pu'rambeayirukki'ravarga'laip pat'ri yoakkiyamaay nadanthu, on'rilum ungga'lukkuk ku'raivillaathirukkumpadikku,

1Th4.12 naangga'l ungga'lukkuk katta'laiyittapadiyea, amaithalu'l'lavarga'laayirukkumpadi naadavum, ungga'l sontha aluvalga'laip paarkkavum, ungga'l sonthak kaiga'linaalea vealaiseyyavum vea'ndumen'ru ungga'lukkup buththisollugi'roam.

1Th4.13 an'riyum, sagoathararea, niththiraiyadainthavarga'linimiththam neengga'l nambikkaiyat'ravarga'laana mat'ravarga'laippoalath thukkiththu, a'rivillaathirukka enakku manathillai.

1Th4.14 iyeasuvaanavar mariththu pinbu ezhunthirunthaaren'ru visuvaasikki'roamea; appadiyea iyeasuvukku'l niththiraiyadainthavarga'laiyum theavan avaroadeakoodak ko'nduvaruvaar.

1Th4.15 karththarudaiya vaarththaiyai munnittu naangga'l ungga'lukkuch sollugi'rathaavathu: karththarudaiya varugaimattum uyiroadirukkum naam niththiraiyadainthavarga'lukku munthikko'lvathillai.

1Th4.16 eanenil, karththar thaamea aaravaaraththoadum, pirathaana thoothanudaiya saththaththoadum, theava ekkaa'laththoadum vaanaththilirunthu i'ranggivaruvaar; appozhuthu ki'risthuvukku'l mariththavarga'l muthalaavathu ezhunthiruppaarga'l.

1Th4.17 pinbu uyiroadirukkum naamum karththarukku ethirko'ndupoaga, meagangga'lmeal avarga'loadeakooda aagaayaththil eduththukko'l'lappattu, ivvithamaay eppozhuthum karththarudaneakooda iruppoam.

1Th4.18 aagaiyaal, intha vaarththaiga'linaalea neengga'l oruvaraiyoruvar theat'rungga'l.

1Th5.1 sagoathararea, ivaiga'l nadakkungkaalangga'laiyum samayangga'laiyungku'riththu ungga'lukku ezhuthavea'nduvathillai.

1Th5.2 iravilea thirudan varugi'ravithamaayk karththarudaiya naa'l varumen'ru neengga'lea nan'raay a'rinthirukki'reerga'l.

1Th5.3 samaathaanamum savukkiyamum u'nden'ru avarga'l sollumpoathu, karppavathiyaanava'lukku veathanai varugi'rathupoala, azhivu sadithiyaay avarga'lmeal varum; avarga'l thappippoavathillai.

1Th5.4 sagoathararea, antha naa'l thirudanaippoala ungga'laip pidiththukko'l'laththakkathaaga, neengga'l anthagaaraththilirukki'ravarga'lallavea.

1Th5.5 neengga'lellaarum ve'lichchaththin pi'l'laiga'lum, pagalin pi'l'laiga'lumaayirukki'reerga'l; naam iravukkum iru'lukkum u'l'laanavarga'lallavea.

1Th5.6 aagaiyaal, mat'ravarga'l thoonggugi'rathupoala naam thoonggaamal, vizhiththukko'ndu the'linthavarga'laayirukkakkadavoam.

1Th5.7 thoonggugi'ravarga'l iraaththiriyilea thoongguvaarga'l; ve'riko'l'lugi'ravarga'l iraaththiriyilea ve'riko'l'luvaarga'l.

1Th5.8 pagalukkuriyavarga'laagiya naamoa the'linthavarga'laayirunthu, visuvaasam anbu ennum maarkkavasaththaiyum, iradchippin nambikkaiyennum thalaichseeraavaiyum thariththukko'ndirukkakkadavoam.

1Th5.9 theavan nammaik koabaakkinaikken'ru niyamikkaamal, nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthumoolamaay iradchippadaivatha'rken'ru niyamiththaar.

1Th5.10 naam vizhiththiruppavarga'laanaalum, niththiraiyadainthavarga'laanaalum, thammudaneakooda naam eagamaayp pizhaiththirukkumpadi avar namakkaaga mariththaarea.

1Th5.11 aagaiyaal neengga'l seythuvarugi'rapadiyea, oruvaraiyoruvar theat'ri, oruvarukkoruvar bakthiviruththi u'ndaagumpadi seyyungga'l.

1Th5.12 an'riyum, sagoathararea, ungga'lukku'l'lea pirayaasappattu, karththarukku'l ungga'lai visaara'naiseygi'ravarga'laayirunthu, ungga'lukkup buththisollugi'ravarga'lai neengga'l mathiththu,

1Th5.13 avarga'ludaiya kiriyaiyinimiththam avarga'lai migavum anbaay e'n'nikko'l'lumpadi ungga'lai vea'ndikko'l'lugi'roam. ungga'lukku'l'lea samaathaanamaayirungga'l.

1Th5.14 mealum, sagoathararea, naangga'l ungga'lukkup poathikki'rathennaven'raal, ozhunggillaathavarga'lukkup buththisollungga'l, thidanat'ravarga'laith theat'rungga'l, balaveenaraith thaanggungga'l, ellaaridaththilum neediya saanthamaayirungga'l.

1Th5.15 oruvanum mat'roruvan seyyum theemaikkuth theemaiseyyaathapadi paarungga'l; ungga'lukku'l'lum mat'ra yaavarukku'l'lum eppozhuthum nanmaiseyya naadungga'l.

1Th5.16 eppozhuthum santhoashamaayirungga'l.

1Th5.17 idaividaamal jebampa'n'nungga'l.

1Th5.18 ellaavat'rileayum sthoaththiragn seyyungga'l; appadich seyvathea ki'risthu iyeasuvukku'l ungga'laikku'riththuth theavanudaiya siththamaayirukki'rathu.

1Th5.19 aaviyai aviththuppoadaathirungga'l.

1Th5.20 theerkkatharisanangga'lai a'rpamaaye'n'naathirungga'l.

1Th5.21 ellaavat'raiyum soathiththup paarththu, nalamaanathaip pidiththukko'l'lungga'l.

1Th5.22 pollaanggaayth thoan'rugi'ra ellaavat'raiyum vittu vilagungga'l.

1Th5.23 samaathaanaththin theavan thaamea ungga'lai mut'rilum parisuththamaakkuvaaraaga. ungga'l aavi aaththumaa sareeram muzhuvathum, nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthu varumpoathu kut'ramat'rathaayirukkumpadi kaakkappaduvathaaga.

1Th5.24 ungga'lai azhaikki'ravar u'nmaiyu'l'lavar, avar appadiyea seyvaar.

1Th5.25 sagoathararea, engga'lukkaaga vea'ndikko'l'lungga'l.

1Th5.26 sagoathararellaaraiyum parisuththa muththaththoadea vaazhththungga'l.

1Th5.27 intha nirubam parisuththamaana sagoatharar yaavarukkum vaasikkappadumpadi seyyavea'ndumen'ru karththarpearil aa'naiyittu ungga'lukkuch sollugi'rean.

1Th5.28 nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvinudaiya kirubai ungga'ludaneakooda iruppathaaga. aamen.
