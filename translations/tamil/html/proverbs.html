Pr1.1 thaaveethin kumaaranum isravealin raajaavumaagiya saalomoanin neethimozhiga'l:

Pr1.2 ivaiga'laal gnaanaththaiyum poathagaththaiyum a’rinthu, buththimathiga'lai u'narnthu,

Pr1.3 viveagam, neethi, niyaayam, nithaanam enbavaiga'laippat’riya ubatheasaththai adaiyalaam.

Pr1.4 ivaiga'l peathaiga'lukku vinaavaiyum, vaalibarukku a’rivaiyum viveagaththaiyum kodukkum.

Pr1.5 buththimaan ivaiga'laik keattu, a’rivil thea’ruvaan; viveagi nallaaloasanaiga'lai adainthu;

Pr1.6 neethimozhiyaiyum, athin arththaththaiyum, gnaaniga'lin vaakkiyangga'laiyum, avarga'l uraiththa puthaiporu'lga'laiyum a’rinthuko'lvaan.

Pr1.7 karththarukkup bayappaduthalea gnaanaththin aarambam; moodar gnaanaththaiyum poathagaththaiyum asattaipa'n'nugi’raarga'l.

Pr1.8 en maganea, un thagappan buththiyaik kea'l, un thaayin poathagaththaith tha'l'laathea.

Pr1.9 avaiga'l un sirasukku alanggaaramaana mudiyum, un kazhuththukkuch sarappa'niyumaayirukkum.

Pr1.10 en maganea, paaviga'l unakku nayangkaattinaalum nee sammathiyaathea.

Pr1.11 engga'loadea vaa, iraththagnsinthumpadi naam pathivirunthu, kut’ramat’rirukki’ravarga'lai mugaantharamin’rip pidikkumpadi o'liththiruppoam;

Pr1.12 paathaa'lam vizhungguvathupoal naam avarga'lai uyiroadea vizhungguvoam; kuzhiyil i’ranggugi’ravarga'l vizhunggappaduvathupoal avarga'lai muzhumaiyum vizhungguvoam;

Pr1.13 vilaiyuyarntha sagalavithap poru'lga'laiyum ka'ndadaivoam; ko'l'laip poru'linaal nammudaiya veeduga'lai nirappuvoam.

Pr1.14 engga'loadea panggaa'liyaayiru; nammellaarukkum orea pai irukkum en’ru avarga'l solvaarga'laagil;

Pr1.15 en maganea, nee avarga'loadea vazhinadavaamal, un kaalai avarga'l paathaikku vilakkuvaayaaga.

Pr1.16 avarga'l kaalga'l theengguseyya oadi, iraththagnsinthath theevirikki’rathu.

Pr1.17 evvagaiyaana padchiyaanaalum sari, athin ka'nga'lukku munbaaga valaiyai virippathu viruthaa.

Pr1.18 ivarga'loa thangga'l iraththaththi’rkea pathivirukki’raarga'l, thangga'l piraa'nanukkea o'livaiththirukki’raarga'l.

Pr1.19 poru'laasaiyu'l'la ellaarudaiya vazhiyum ithuvea; ithu thannaiyudaiyavarga'lin uyirai vaanggum.

Pr1.20 gnaanamaanathu ve'liyea nin’ru kooppidugi’rathu, veethiga'lil saththamidugi’rathu.

Pr1.21 athu santhadiyu'l'la therukka'lin santhilum, olimugavaasalilum nin’ru kooppittu, patta'naththil than vaarththaiga'lai vasaniththuch sollugi’rathu:

Pr1.22 peathaiga'lea, neengga'l peathaimaiyai virumbuvathum, ninthanaikkaararea, neengga'l ninthanaiyil piriyappaduvathum, mathiyeenarea, neengga'l gnaanaththai ve’ruppathum, ethuvaraikkum irukkum.

Pr1.23 en kadinthuko'l'luthalukkuth thirumbungga'l; ithoa, en aaviyai ungga'lukku aru'luvean, en vaarththaiga'lai ungga'lukkuth therivippean.

Pr1.24 naan kooppittum, neengga'l keadkamaattoam engi’reerga'l; naan en kaiyai neettiyum kavanikki’ravan oruvanum illai.

Pr1.25 en aaloasanaiyaiyellaam neengga'l tha'l'li, en kadinthuko'l'luthalai ve’ruththeerga'l.

Pr1.26 aagaiyaal, naanum ungga'l aabaththukkaalaththil nagaiththu, neengga'l bayappadungkaariyam varumpoathu aagadiyampa'n'nuvean.

Pr1.27 neengga'l bayappadungkaariyam pusalpoal varumpoathum, aabaththu soo’raava'lipoal ungga'lukku nearidumpoathum, nerukkamum idukka'num ungga'lmeal varumpoathum, aagadiyampa'n'nuvean.

Pr1.28 appozhuthu avarga'l ennai noakkik kooppiduvaarga'l; naan ma’ruuththaravu kodukkamaattean; avarga'l athikaalaiyilea ennaith theaduvaarga'l, ennaik kaa'namaattaarga'l.

Pr1.29 avarga'l a’rivai ve’ruththaarga'l, karththarukkup bayappaduthalaith therinthu ko'l'laama’rpoanaarga'l.

Pr1.30 en aaloasanaiyai avarga'l virumbavillai; en kadinthuko'l'luthalaiyellaam asattaipa'n'ninaarga'l.

Pr1.31 aagaiyaal avarga'l thangga'l vazhiyin palanaip pusippaarga'l; thangga'l yoasanaiga'linaal thirupthiyadaivaarga'l.

Pr1.32 peathaiga'lin maa’rupaadu avarga'laik kollum, moodarin nirvisaaram avarga'lai azhikkum.

Pr1.33 enakkuch sevikodukki’ravan evanoa, avan vikkinamin’ri vaasampa'n'ni, aabaththi’rkup bayappadaamal amaithiyaayiruppaan.

Pr2.1 en maganea, nee un seviyai gnaanaththi’rkuch saayththu, un iruthayaththaip buththikku amaiyappa'n'numporuttu,

Pr2.2 nee en vaarththaiga'lai eat’rukko'ndu, en katta'laiga'lai unnidaththil paththirappaduththi,

Pr2.3 gnaanaththai vaa en’ru kooppittu, buththiyaich saththamittu azhaiththu,

Pr2.4 athai ve'l'liyaippoal naadi, puthaiyalga'laith theadugi’rathupoal theaduvaayaagil,

Pr2.5 appozhuthu karththarukkup bayappaduthal innathen’ru nee u'narnthu, theavanai a’riyum a’rivaik ka'ndadaivaay.

Pr2.6 karththar gnaanaththaith tharugi’raar; avar vaayinin’ru a’rivum buththiyum varum.

Pr2.7 avar neethimaanga'lukken’ru meygngnaanaththai vaiththuvaiththirukki’raar; uththamamaay nadakki’ravarga'lukku avar keadagamaayirukki’raar.

Pr2.8 avar niyaayaththin ne’riga'laith tha’rkaaththu, thammudaiya parisuththavaanga'lin paathaiyaik kaappaat’rugi’raar.

Pr2.9 appozhuthu neethiyaiyum, niyaayaththaiyum, nithaanaththaiyum, sagala nalvazhiga'laiyum a’rinthuko'l'luvaay.

Pr2.10 gnaanam un iruthayaththil piraveasiththu, a’rivu un aaththumaavukku inbamaayirukkumpoathu,

Pr2.11 nalyoasanai unnaik kaappaat’rum, buththi unnaip paathukaakkum.

Pr2.12 athinaal nee thunmaarkkanudaiya vazhikkum, maa’rupaadu peasugi’ra manushanukkum,

Pr2.13 anthagaara vazhiga'lil nadakka neethine’riga'lai vittu,

Pr2.14 theemaiseyya magizhnthu, thunmaarkkarudaiya maa’rupaaduga'lil ka'likoorugi’ravarga'lukkum,

Pr2.15 maa’rupaadaana paathaiga'lilum koa'nalaana vazhiga'lilum nadakki’ravarga'lukkum nee thappuvikkappaduvaay.

Pr2.16 than i'lavayathin naayaganai vittu, than theavanudaiya udanpadikkaiyai ma’ranthu,

Pr2.17 ichchagamaana vaarththaiga'laippeasum anniya pe'n'naagiya parasthireekkum thappuvikkappaduvaay.

Pr2.18 ava'ludaiya veedu mara'naththukkum, ava'ludaiya paathaiga'l mariththoaridaththi’rkum saaygi’rathu.

Pr2.19 ava'lidaththil poagi’ravarga'lil oruvarum thirumbugi’rathillai, jeevapaathaiga'lil vanthu searugi’rathumillai.

Pr2.20 aathalaal nee nallavarga'lin vazhiyilea nadanthu, neethimaanga'lin paathaiga'laik kaaththukko'lvaayaaga.

Pr2.21 sevvaiyaanavarga'l boomiyilea vaasampa'n'nuvaarga'l; uththamarga'l athilea thanggiyiruppaarga'l.

Pr2.22 thunmaarkkaroa boomiyilirunthu a’ruppu'ndupoavaarga'l; thuroagiga'l athil iraathapadikku nirmoolamaavaarga'l.

Pr3.1 en maganea, en poathagaththai ma’ravaathea; un iruthayam en katta'laiga'laik kaakkakkadavathu.

Pr3.2 avaiga'l unakku neediththa naadka'laiyum, theerkkaayusaiyum, samaathaanaththaiyum perugappa'n'num.

Pr3.3 kirubaiyum saththiyamum unnaivittu vilagaathiruppathaaga; nee avaiga'lai un kazhuththilea poo'ndu, avaiga'lai un iruthayamaagiya palagaiyil ezhuthikko'l.

Pr3.4 athinaal theavanudaiya paarvaiyilum manusharudaiya paarvaiyilum thayaiyum na’rbuththiyum pe’ruvaay.

Pr3.5 un suyabuththiyinmeal saayaamal, un muzhu iruthayaththoadum karththaril nambikkaiyaayirunthu,

Pr3.6 un vazhiga'lilellaam avarai ninaiththukko'l; appozhuthu avar un paathaiga'laich sevvaippaduththuvaar.

Pr3.7 nee unnai gnaaniyen’ru e'n'naathea; karththarukkup bayanthu, theemaiyai vittu vilagu.

Pr3.8 athu un naabikku aaroakkiyamum, un elumbuga'lukku oonumaagum.

Pr3.9 un poru'laalum, un ellaa vi'laivin mutha’rpalanaalum karththaraik kanampa'n'nu.

Pr3.10 appozhuthu un ka'lagnchiyangga'l poora'namaay nirambum; un aalaiga'lil thiraadcharasam pura'ndoadum.

Pr3.11 en maganea, nee karththarudaiya sidchaiyai a’rpamaaga e'n'naathea, avar kadinthuko'l'lumpoathu soarnthupoagaathea.

Pr3.12 thagappan thaan neasikki’ra puththiranaich sidchikki’rathupoala, karththarum evanidaththil anbukoorugi’raaroa avanaich sidchikki’raar.

Pr3.13 gnaanaththaik ka'ndadaigi’ra manushanum, buththiyaich sambaathikki’ra manushanum baakkiyavaanga'l.

Pr3.14 athin varththagam ve'l'li varththagaththilum, athin aathaayam pasumponnilum uththamamaanathu.

Pr3.15 muththukka'laippaarkkilum athu vilaiyea’rappet’rathu; nee ichchikkaththakkathon’rum atha’rku nigaralla.

Pr3.16 athin valathukaiyil theerkkaayusum, athin idathukaiyil selvamum kanamum irukki’rathu.

Pr3.17 athin vazhiga'l inithaana vazhiga'l, athin paathaiga'lellaam samaathaanam.

Pr3.18 athu thannai adainthavarga'lukku jeevavirudcham, athaip pat’rikko'l'lugi’ra evanum baakkiyavaan.

Pr3.19 karththar gnaanaththinaalea boomiyai asthibaarappaduththi, buththiyinaalea vaanangga'lai sthaabiththaar.

Pr3.20 avarudaiya gnaanaththinaalea aazhangga'l pirinthathu, aagaayamum paniyaip peygi’rathu.

Pr3.21 en maganea, ivaiga'l un ka'nga'lai vittup piriyaathiruppathaaga; meygngnaanaththaiyum nallaaloasanaiyaiyum kaaththukko'l.

Pr3.22 avaiga'l un aaththumaavukku jeevanum, un kazhuththukku alanggaaramumaayirukkum.

Pr3.23 appozhuthu nee bayamin’ri un vazhiyil nadappaay, un kaal ida’raathu.

Pr3.24 nee padukkumpoathu bayappadaathiruppaay; nee paduththukko'l'lumpoathu un niththirai inbamaayirukkum.

Pr3.25 sadithiyaana thigilum, thushdarga'lin paazhkkadippum varumpoathu nee agnchavea'ndaam.

Pr3.26 karththar un nambikkaiyaayirunthu, un kaal sikkikko'l'laathapadi kaappaar.

Pr3.27 nanmaiseyyumpadi unakkuth thiraa'niyirukkumpoathu, athaich seyyaththakkavarga'lukkuch seyyaamal iraathea.

Pr3.28 unnidaththil poru'l irukkaiyil un ayalaanai noakki: nee poayth thirumbavaa, naa'laikkuth tharuvean en’ru sollaathea.

Pr3.29 achchamin’ri unnidaththil vaasampa'n'nugi’ra un ayalaanukku viroathamaaga theenggu ninaiyaathea.

Pr3.30 oruvan unakkuth theengguseyyaathirukka, kaara'namin’ri avanoadea vazhakkaadaathea.

Pr3.31 kodumaiyu'l'lavanmeal po’raamai ko'l'laathea; avanudaiya vazhiga'lil on’raiyum therinthuko'l'laathea.

Pr3.32 maa’rupaadu'l'lavan karththarukku aruvaruppaanavan; neethimaanga'loadea avarudaiya iragasiyam irukki’rathu.

Pr3.33 thunmaarkkanudaiya veettil karththarin saabam irukki’rathu, neethimaanga'ludaiya vaasasthalaththaiyoa avar aaseervathikki’raar.

Pr3.34 igazhvoarai avar igazhugi’raar; thaazhmaiyu'l'lavarga'lukkoa kirubaiya'likki’raar.

Pr3.35 gnaanavaanga'l kanaththaich suthantharippaarga'l; mathikeadaroa kanaveenaththai adaivaarga'l.

Pr4.1 pi'l'laiga'lea, neengga'l thagappan poathagaththaik keattu, buththiyai adaiyumpadi kavaniyungga'l.

Pr4.2 naan ungga'lukku na’rpoathagaththaith tharugi’rean; en ubatheasaththai vidaathirungga'l.

Pr4.3 naan en thagappanukkup piriyamaana kumaaranum, en thaaykku migavum arumaiyaana orea pi'l'laiyumaanavan.

Pr4.4 avar enakkup poathiththuch sonnathu: un iruthayam en vaarththaiga'laik kaaththukko'l'lakkadavathu; en katta'laiga'laik kaikko'l, appozhuthu pizhaippaay.

Pr4.5 gnaanaththaich sambaathi, buththiyaiyum sambaathi; en vaayin vaarththaiga'lai ma’ravaamalum vittu vilagaamalum iru.

Pr4.6 athai vidaathea, athu unnaith tha’rkaakkum; athinmeal piriyamaayiru, athu unnaik kaaththukko'l'lum.

Pr4.7 gnaanamea mukkiyam, gnaanaththaich sambaathi; ennaththaich sambaathiththaalum buththiyaich sambaathiththukko'l.

Pr4.8 nee athai meanmaippaduththu, athu unnai meanmaippaduththum; nee athaith thazhuvikko'ndaal, athu unnaik kanampa'n'num.

Pr4.9 athu un thalaikku alanggaaramaana mudiyaik kodukkum; athu magimaiyaana kireedaththai unakkuch soottum.

Pr4.10 en maganea, kea'l, en vaarththaiga'lai eat’rukko'l; appozhuthu un aayusin varushangga'l athigamaagum.

Pr4.11 gnaanamaarkkaththai naan unakkup poathiththean; sevvaiyaana paathaiga'lilea unnai nadaththinean.

Pr4.12 nee avaiga'lil nadakkumpoathu un nadaiga'lukku idukka'n u'ndaavathillai; nee avaiga'lil oadinaalum ida’ramaattaay.

Pr4.13 buththimathiyai u’ruthiyaayp pat’rikko'l, athai vittuvidaathea; athaik kaaththukko'l, athuvea unakku jeevan.

Pr4.14 thunmaarkkarudaiya paathaiyil piraveasiyaathea; theeyoarudaiya vazhiyil nadavaathea.

Pr4.15 athai ve’ruththuvidu, athin vazhiyaayp poagaathea; athai vittu vilagik kadanthupoa.

Pr4.16 pollaappuch seythaalozhiya avarga'lukku niththirai varaathu; avarga'l yaaraiyaagilum vizhappa'n'naathirunthaal avarga'l thookkam kalainthupoam.

Pr4.17 avarga'l aagaamiyaththin appaththaip pusiththu, kodumaiyin irasaththaik kudikki’raarga'l.

Pr4.18 neethimaanga'ludaiya paathai naduppagalvaraikkum athigamathigamaayp piragaasikki’ra sooriyappiragaasampoalirukkum.

Pr4.19 thunmaarkkarudaiya paathaiyoa kaariru'laippoalirukkum; thaangga'l ida’ruvathu innathil en’ru a’riyaarga'l.

Pr4.20 en maganea, en vaarththaiga'laik kavani; en vasanangga'lukku un seviyaich saay.

Pr4.21 avaiga'l un ka'nga'lai vittup piriyaathiruppathaaga; avaiga'lai un iruthayaththukku'l'lea kaaththukko'l.

Pr4.22 avaiga'laik ka'ndupidikki’ravarga'lukku avaiga'l jeevanum, avarga'l udalukkellaam aaroakkiyamumaam.

Pr4.23 ellaak kaavaloadum un iruthayaththaik kaaththukko'l, athinidaththinin’ru jeevaoot’ru pu’rappadum.

Pr4.24 vaayin thaa’rumaa’ruga'lai unnai vittagat’ri, uthaduga'lin maa’rupaattai unakkuth thoorappaduththu.

Pr4.25 un ka'nga'l nearaay noakkakkadavathu; un ka'n'nimaiga'l unakku munnea sevvaiyaayp paarkkakkadavathu.

Pr4.26 un kaal nadaiyaich seerthookkippaar; un vazhiga'lellaam nilaivarappattiruppathaaga.

Pr4.27 valathupu’ramaavathu idathupu’ramaavathu saayaathea; un kaalaith theemaikku vilakkuvaayaaga.

Pr5.1 en maganea, en gnaanaththaik kavaniththu, en buththikku un seviyaich saay;

Pr5.2 appozhuthu nee viveagaththaip pea'nikko'lvaay, un uthaduga'l a’rivaik kaaththukko'l'lum.

Pr5.3 parasthireeyin uthaduga'l theankoodupoal ozhugum; ava'l vaay e'n'neyilum miruthuvaayirukkum.

Pr5.4 ava'l seygaiyin mudivoa ettiyaippoalak kasappum, irupu’ramung karukku'l'la pattayampoal koormaiyumaayirukkum.

Pr5.5 ava'l kaaladiga'l mara'naththukku i’ranggum; ava'l nadaiga'l paathaa'laththaip pat’rippoagum.

Pr5.6 nee jeevamaarkkaththaich sinthiththuk ko'l'laathapadikku, ava'ludaiya nadaiga'l maa’rimaa’ri vigaarappadum; avaiga'lai a’riyamudiyaathu.

Pr5.7 aathalaal pi'l'laiga'lea, ippozhuthu enakkuch sevikodungga'l; en vaayin vasanangga'lai vittu neenggaathirungga'l.

Pr5.8 un vazhiyai ava'lukkuth thoorappaduththu; ava'ludaiya veettin vaasalaik kittich searaathea.

Pr5.9 searnthaal un meanmaiyai anniyarga'lukkum, un aayusin kaalaththaik kodoorarukkum koduththuviduvaay.

Pr5.10 anniyar un selvaththinaal thirupthiyadaivaarga'l; un pirayaasaththin palan pu’raththiyaarudaiya veettil searum.

Pr5.11 mudivilea un maamsamum un sareeramum uruvazhiyumpoathu nee thukkiththu:

Pr5.12 aiyoa, poathagaththai naan ve’ruththeanea, kadinthuko'l'luthalai en manam aladchiyampa'n'ninathea!

Pr5.13 en poathagarin sollai naan kea'laamalum, enakku ubatheasampa'n'ninavarga'lukku en seviyaich saayaamalum poaneanea!

Pr31.12 ava'l uyiroadirukki'ra naa'lellaam avanukkuth theemaiyaiyalla, nanmaiyaiyea seygi'raa'l.

Pr31.13 aattumayiraiyum sa'nalaiyum theadi, than kaiga'linaal u’rchaagaththoadea vealaiseygi'raa'l.

Pr31.14 ava'l viyaabaarak kappalga'laippoalirukki'raa'l; thooraththilirunthu than aagaaraththaik ko'nduvarugi'raa'l.

Pr31.15 iruttoadea ezhunthu than veettaarukku aagaarangkoduththu, than vealaikkaariga'lukkup padiya'lakki'raa'l.

Pr31.16 oru vayalai visaariththu athai vaanggugi'raa'l; than kaiga'lin sambaaththiyaththinaal thiraadchaththoattaththai naattugi'raa'l.

Pr31.17 thannaip belaththaal idaikkattikko'ndu, than kaiga'laip balappaduththugi'raa'l.

Pr31.18 than viyaabaaram pirayoajanamu'l'lathen'ru a'rinthirukki'raa'l; iravilea ava'l vi'lakku a'naiyaathirukkum.

Pr31.19 than kaiga'lai iraattinaththil vaikki'raa'l; ava'l viralga'l kathiraip pidikkum.

Pr31.20 si'rumaiyaanavarga'lukkuth than kaiyaith thi'ranthu, eazhaiga'lukkuth than karangga'lai neettugi'raa'l.

Pr31.21 than veettaar anaivarukkum irattaippurai uduppirukki'rathaal, than veettaarinimiththam ku'lirukkup bayappadaa'l.

Pr31.22 iraththinak kamba'langga'laith thanakku u'ndupa'n'nugi'raa'l; melliya pudavaiyum iraththaambaramum ava'l uduppu.

Pr31.23 ava'l purushan theasaththu moopparga'loadea niyaayasthalangga'lil udkaarnthirukkaiyil pearpet'ravanaayirukki'raan.

Pr31.24 melliya pudavaiga'lai u'ndupa'n'ni vi’rki'raa'l; kachchaiga'lai varththagaridaththil oppuvikki'raa'l.

Pr31.25 ava'l udai balamum alanggaaramumaayirukki'rathu; varungkaalaththaippat’riyum magizhugi'raa'l.

Pr31.26 than vaayai gnaanam vi'langgath thi'rakki'raa'l; thayaiyu'l'la poathagam ava'l naavinmeal irukki'rathu.

Pr31.27 ava'l soambalin appaththaip pusiyaamal, than veettukkaariyam eppadi nadakki'rathu en'ru ka'n'noakkamaayirukki'raa'l.

Pr31.28 ava'l pi'l'laiga'l ezhumbi, ava'laip baakkiyavathi engi'raarga'l; ava'l purushanum ava'laippaarththu:

Pr31.29 aneagam pe'nga'l ku'nasaaliga'laayirunthathu'ndu; neeyoa avarga'l ellaarukkum mea’rpattava'l en'ru ava'laip pugazhugi'raan.

Pr31.30 saunthariyam vagnchanaiyu'l'lathu, azhagum vee'n, karththarukkup bayappadugi'ra sthireeyea pugazhappaduvaa'l.

Pr31.31 ava'l kaiga'lin palanai ava'lukkuk kodungga'l; ava'ludaiya seygaiga'l vaasalga'lil ava'laip pugazhakkadavathu.
