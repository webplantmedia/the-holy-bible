Song1.1 saalomoan paadina unnathappaattu.

Song1.2 avar thamathu vaayin muththangga'laal ennai muththamiduvaaraaga: umathu neasam thiraadcharasaththaippaarkkilum inbamaanathu.

Song1.3 umathu parima'lathailangga'l inbamaana vaasanaiyu'l'lavaiga'l; umathu naamam oot’ru'nda parima'lathailamaayirukki’rathu; aagaiyaal kanniyarga'l ummai neasikki’raarga'l.

Song1.4 ennai izhuththukko'l'lum, umakkup pinnea oadivaruvoam; raajaa ennaith thamathu a’raiga'lil azhaiththukko'nduvanthaar; naangga'l umakku'l ka'likoornthu magizhuvoam; thiraadcharasaththaippaarkkilum umathu neasaththai ninaippoam; uththamarga'l ummai neasikki’raarga'l.

Song1.5 erusaleamin kumaaraththiga'lea! keathaarin koodaarangga'laippoalavum, saalomoanin thiraiga'laippoalavum naan ka’ruppaayirunthaalum, azhagaayirukki’rean.

Song1.6 naan ka’ruppaayirukki’rean en’ru paaraatheayungga'l; veyil enmea’rpattathu; en thaayin pi'l'laiga'l enmeal koabamaayirunthu, ennaith thiraadchaththoattangga'lukkuk kaava’rkaariyaaga vaiththaarga'l; en sonthath thiraadchaththoattaththaiyoa naan kaakkavillai.

Song1.7 en aaththuma neasarea! umathu manthaiyai enggea meayththu, athai maththiyaanaththil enggea madakkugi’reer? enakkuch sollum; umathu thoazharin manthaiga'l arugea alainthuthirigi’rava'laippoala naan irukkavea'ndiyathenna?

Song1.8 sthireega'lil roobavathiyea! athai nee a’riyaayaagil, manthaiyin kaaladiga'laith thodarnthupoay, meaypparga'ludaiya koodaarangga'la'ndaiyil un aattukkuttiga'lai meayavidu.

Song1.9 en piriyamea! paarvoanudaiya irathangga'lil poo'ndirukki’ra pariga'l pavugnchukku unnai oppidugi’rean.

Song1.10 aabara'naathiga'l poo'nda un kannangga'lum, aarangga'l poo'nda un kazhuththum azhagaayirukki’rathu.

Song1.11 ve'l'lip pottuga'lu'l'la pon aabara'nangga'lai unakkup pa'n'nuvoam.

Song1.12 raajaa thamathu panthiyilirukkunthanaiyum ennudaiya na'lathathailam than vaasanaiyai veesum.

Song1.13 en neasar enakku en sthanangga'lin naduvil thanggum ve'l'laippoa'lach se'ndu.

Song1.14 en neasar enakku enggeathi oor thiraadchaththoattangga'lil mu'laikkum maruthoan’rip poongkoththu.

Song1.15 en piriyamea! nee roobavathi; nee roobavathi; un ka'nga'l pu’raakka'nga'l.

Song1.16 neer roobamu'l'lavar; en neasarea! neer inbamaanavar; nammudaiya magncham pasumaiyaanathu.

Song1.17 nammudaiya veettin uththirangga'l keathurumaram, nammudaiya machchu theavathaarumaram.

Song2.1 naan saaroanin roajaavum, pa'l'laththaakkuga'lin leelipushpamumaayirukki’rean.

Song2.2 mu'l'luga'lukku'l'lea leelipushpam eppadiyirukki’rathoa, appadiyea kumaaraththiga'lukku'l'lea enakkup piriyamaanava'lum irukki’raa'l.

Song2.3 kaattumarangga'lukku'l'lea kichchilimaram eppadiyirukki’rathoa, appadiyea kumaararukku'l'lea en neasar irukki’raar; athin nizhalilea vaagnchaiyaay udkaarugi’rean, athin kani en vaaykku mathuramaayirukki’rathu.

Song2.4 ennai virunthusaalaikku azhaiththukko'ndupoanaar; enmeal pa’rantha avarudaiya kodi neasamea.

Song2.5 thiraadcharasaththaal ennaith theat’rungga'l, kichchilippazhangga'laal ennai aat’rungga'l; neasaththaal soagamadainthirukki’rean.

Song2.6 avar idathukai en thalaiyinkeezh irukki’rathu; avar valathukai ennai a'naiththukko'l'lugi’rathu.

Song2.7 erusaleamin kumaaraththiga'lea! enakkup piriyamaanava'lukku manathaagumattum, neengga'l ava'lai vizhikkappa'n'naamalum ezhuppaamalumirukkumpadi ve'limaanga'lmealum ve'liyin maraiga'lmealum ungga'lai aa'naiyidugi’rean.

Song2.8 ithu en neasarudaiya saththam! ithoa, avar malaiga'lmeal kuthiththum meaduga'lmeal thu'l'liyum varugi’raar.

Song2.9 en neasar ve'limaanukkum maraikkuttikkum oppaayirukki’raar; ithoa, avar engga'l mathilukkup pu’rambea nin’ru, palaga'ni vazhiyaayp paarththu, kiraathiyin vazhiyaayth thamathu malarntha mugaththaik kaa'nbikki’raar.

Song2.10 en neasar ennoadea peasi: en piriyamea! en roobavathiyea! ezhunthuvaa.

Song2.11 ithoa, maarikaalam sen’rathu, mazhaipeythu ozhinthathu.

Song2.12 boomiyilea pushpangga'l kaa'nappadugi’rathu; kuruviga'l paadungkaalam vanthathu, kaattuppu’raavin saththam namathu theasaththil keadkappadugi’rathu.

Song2.13 aththimaram kaaykaayththathu; thiraadchakkodiga'l pooppooththu vaasanaiyum parima'likki’rathu; en piriyamea! en roobavathiyea! nee ezhunthuvaa.

Song2.14 kanmalaiyin vedippuga'lilum sigarangga'lin ma’raividangga'lilum thanggugi’ra en pu’raavea! un mugaroobaththai enakkuk kaa'nbi, un saththaththai naan keadkattum; un saththam inbamum, un mugaroobam azhagumaayirukki’rathu en’raar.

Song2.15 thiraadchaththoattangga'laik kedukki’ra kuzhinariga'laiyum si’runariga'laiyum namakkup pidiyungga'l; nammudaiya thiraadchaththoattangga'l poovum pignchumaayirukki’rathea.

Song2.16 en neasar ennudaiyavar, naan avarudaiyava'l. avar leelipushpangga'lukku'l'lea meaygi’raar.

Song2.17 en neasarea! pagal ku'lirchchiyaagi, nizhal saaynthupoagumvaraikkum, neer thirumbi, kun’rum pi'lappumaana kanmalaiga'lil kuthiththuvarum kalaimaanukkum maraiga'lin kuttikkum samaanamaayirum.

Song3.1 iraakkaalangga'lil en padukkaiyilea en aaththuma neasaraith theadinean; theadiyum naan avaraik kaa'navillai.

Song3.2 naan ezhunthu nagaraththin veethiga'lilum therukka'lilum thirinthu, en aaththuma neasaraith theaduvean en’rean; theadiyum naan avaraik kaa'navillai.

Song3.3 nagaraththilea thirigi’ra kaavalaa'lar ennaik ka'ndaarga'l: en aaththuma neasaraik ka'ndeerga'laa en’ru avarga'laik keattean.

Song3.4 naan avarga'lai vittuk kognchathooram kadanthupoanavudanea, en aaththuma neasaraik ka'ndean; avarai naan en thaayin veettilum ennaip pet’rava'lin a’raiyilum ko'nduvanthu vidumattum vidaamal pat’rikko'ndean.

Song3.5 erusaleamin kumaaraththiga'lea! enakkup piriyamaanava'lukku manathaagumattum neengga'l ava'lai vizhikkappa'n'naamalum ezhuppaamalumirukkumpadi, ve'limaanga'lmealum ve'liyin maraiga'lmealum ungga'lai aa'naiyidugi’rean.

Song3.6 ve'l'laippoa'laththinaalum saambiraa'niyinaalum varththagarudaiya sagalavitha kanthappodiyinaalum u'ndaagiya vaasanaiyai veesi, thoobasthambangga'laippoal vanaantharaththilirunthu varugi’ra ivar yaar?

Song3.7 ithoa, saalomoanudaiya magncham; isravealin savuriyavaanga'lil a’rubathu savuriyavaanga'l athaich sut’ri ni’rki’raarga'l.

Song3.8 ivarga'lellaarum pattayam pidiththu, yuththaththukkup pazhaginavarga'laayirukki’raarga'l; iraakkaala bayaththinimiththam avanavanudaiya pattayam avanavan araiyilirukki’rathu.

Song3.9 saalomoan raajaa thanakku leebanoanin maraththinaal oru irathaththaip pa'n'nuviththaar.

Song3.10 athin thoo'nga'lai ve'l'liyinaalum, athin thattaip ponninaalum, athin aasanaththai iraththaambaraththinaalum pa'n'nuviththaar; athin udpu’raththilea erusaleamin kumaaraththiga'linimiththam neasam ennum samukkaa'lam viriththirunthathu.

Song3.11 seeyoan kumaaraththiga'lea! neengga'l pu’rappattuppoay, raajaavaagiya saalomoanin kaliyaa'nanaa'lilum, manamagizhchchiyin naa'lilum, avarudaiya thaayaar avarukkuch soottina mudiyoadirukki’ra avaraip paarungga'l.

Song4.1 nee roobavathi, en piriyamea! nee roobavathi; un mukkaattin naduvea un ka'nga'l pu’raakka'nga'laayirukki’rathu; un koonthal keeleayaath malaiyil thazhaimeayum ve'l'laattu manthaiyaippoalirukki’rathu.

Song4.2 un pa’rka'l, mayir kaththarikkappattapin ku'lippaattappattuk karaiyea’rugi’ravaiga'lum, on’raagilum maladaayiraamal ellaam irattaikkuttiyeen’ravaiga'lumaana aattumanthaiyaippoalirukki’rathu.

Song4.3 un uthaduga'l sivappunoolukkuch samaanamum, un vaakku inbamumaayirukki’rathu; un mukkaattin naduvea un kannangga'l vediththa maatha'lampazhampoalirukki’rathu.

Song4.4 un kazhuththu, baraakkiramasaaliga'lin keadagangga'laagiya aayiram parisaiga'l thookkiyirukki’ra aayuthasaalaiyaakkappatta thaaveethin koapurampoalirukki’rathu.

Song4.5 un ira'ndu sthanangga'lum leelipushpangga'lil meayum ve'limaanin irattaikkuttiga'lukkuch samaanam.

Song4.6 pagal ku'lirchchiyaagi nizhal saaynthupoagumvaraikkum, naan ve'l'laippoa'lamalaiyilum saambiraa'nimalaiyilum poayiruppean.

Song4.7 en piriyamea! nee poora'na roobavathi; unnil pazhuthon’rumillai.

Song4.8 leebanoanilirunthu ennoadea vaa, en ma'navaa'liyea! leebanoanilirunthu ennoadea vaa. amanaavin kodumudiyilirunthum, seaneer ermoanin kodumudiyilirunthum, singgangga'lin thaabarangga'lilirunthum, sivinggiga'lin malaiga'lilirunthum keezhea paar.

Song4.9 en iruthayaththaik kavarnthuko'ndaay; en sagoathariyea! en ma'navaa'liyea! un ka'nga'lilon’rinaalum un kazhuththilu'l'la oru sarappa'niyinaalum en iruthayaththaik kavarnthuko'ndaay.

Song4.10 un neasam evva'lavu inbamaayirukki’rathu; en sagoathariyea! en ma'navaa'liyea! thiraadcharasaththaippaarkkilum un neasam evva'lavu mathuramaayirukki’rathu! sagala kanthavarkkangga'laippaarkkilum un parima'lathailangga'l evva'lavu vaasanaiyaayirukki’rathu!

Song4.11 en ma'navaa'liyea! un uthaduga'lilirunthu thean ozhugugi’rathu, un naavinkeezh theanum paalum irukki’rathu, un vasthirangga'lin vaasanai leebanoanin vaasanaikkoppaayirukki’rathu.

Song4.12 en sagoathariyea! en ma'navaa'liyea! nee adaikkappatta thoattamum, ma’raivu kattappatta neeroot’rum, muththirikkappatta ki'na’rumaayirukki’raay.

Song4.13 un thoattam maatha'lagnsediga'lum, arumaiyaana kanimarangga'lum, maruthoan’rich sediga'lum, na'lathachsediga'lum,

Song4.14 na'lathamum, kunggumamum, vasambum, lavanggamum, sagalavitha thoobavarkka marangga'lum, ve'l'laippoa'lachsediga'lum, santhana virudchangga'lum, sagalavitha kanthavarkkachsediga'lumu'l'la singgaaravanamaayirukki’rathu.

Song4.15 thoattangga'lukku neeroot’rum, jeevaththa'n'neerin thuravum, leebanoanilirunthu oadivarum vaaykkaalga'lum u'ndaayirukki’rathu.

Song4.16 vaadaiyea! ezhumbu; then’ralea! vaa; kanthappisinga'l vadiya en thoattaththil veesu; en neasar thammudaiya thoattaththukku vanthu, thamathu arumaiyaana kaniga'laip pusippaaraaga.

Song5.1 en sagoathariyea! en ma'navaa'liyea! naan en thoattaththil vanthean, en ve'l'laippoa'laththaiyum en kanthavarkkangga'laiyum searththean; en theankoottai en theanoadu pusiththean; en thiraadcharasaththai en paaloadum kudiththean. sineagitharea! pusiyungga'l; piriyamaanavarga'lea! kudiyungga'l, poorththiyaayk kudiyungga'l.

Song5.2 naan niththiraipa'n'ninean, en ithayamoa vizhiththirunthathu; kathavaith thattugi’ra en neasarin saththaththaik keattean: en sagoathariyea! en piriyamea! en pu’raavea! en uththamiyea! kathavaith thi’ra; en thalai paniyinaalum, en thalaimayir iravil peyyum thoo’ralinaalum nanainthirukki’rathu en’raar.

Song5.3 en vasthiraththaik kazhat’rip poattean; naan eppadi athaith thirumbavum uduppean, en paathangga'laik kazhuvinean, naan eppadi avaiga'laith thirumbavum azhukkaakkuvean en’rean.

Song5.4 en neasar thamathu kaiyaik kathavuth thuvaaraththin vazhiyaay neettinaar, appozhuthu en u'l'lam avar nimiththam pongginathu.

Song5.5 en neasarukkuk kathavaiththi’rakka naan ezhunthean; poottina kaippidiga'lmeal en kaiga'lilirunthu ve'l'laippoa'lamum, en viralga'lilirunthu vaasanaiyu'l'la ve'l'laippoa'lamum vadinthathu.

Song5.6 en neasarukkuk kathavaith thi’ranthean; en neasaroa illai, poayvittaar; avar sonna vaarththaiyaal, en aaththumaa soarnthupoayit’ru. avaraith theadinean, avaraik kaa'navillai; avaraik kooppittean, avar enakku ma’ruuththaravu kodukkavillai.

Song5.7 nagaraththil thirigi’ra kaavalaa'lar ennaik ka'ndu, ennai adiththu, ennaik kaayappaduththinaarga'l; alanggaththin kaava’rkaarar enmealiruntha en poarvaiyai eduththukko'ndaarga'l.

Song5.8 erusaleamin kumaaraththiga'lea! en neasaraik ka'ndeerga'laanaal, naan neasaththaal soagamadainthirukki’rean en’ru avarukkuch sollumpadi ungga'lai aa'naiyidugi’rean.

Song5.9 sthireega'lukku'l roobavathiyea! mat’ra neasaraippaarkkilum un neasar ethinaal viseashiththavar? nee ippadi engga'lai aa'naiyida, mat’ra neasaraippaarkkilum un neasar ethinaal viseashiththavar?

Song5.10 en neasar ve'nmaiyum sivappumaanavar; pathinaayirampearga'lil si’ranthavar.

Song5.11 avar thalai thanggamayamaayirukki’rathu; avar thalaimayir suru'l suru'laayum, kaagaththaippoal karumaiyaayumirukki’rathu.

Song5.12 avarudaiya ka'nga'l tha'n'neer ni’raintha nathiga'lin oaramaayth thanggum pu’raakka'nga'lukku oppaanavaiga'lum, paalil kazhuvappattavaiga'lum, nearththiyaayp pathikkappattavaiga'lumaayirukki’rathu.

Song5.13 avar kannangga'l kanthavarkkap paaththiga'laippoalavum, vaasanaiyu'l'la pushpangga'laippoalavumirukki’rathu; avar uthaduga'l leelipushpangga'laip poan’rathu, vaasanaiyu'l'la ve'l'laippoa'lam athilirunthu vadigi’rathu.

Song5.14 avar karangga'l padigappachchai pathiththa pon va'laiyalga'laippoalirukki’rathu; avar anggam inthiraneela iraththinangga'l izhaiththa piragaasamaana yaanaith thanthaththaippoalirukki’rathu.

Song5.15 avar kaalga'l pasumpon aathaarangga'linmeal ni’rki’ra ve'l'laikkal thoo'nga'laippoalirukki’rathu; avar roobam leebanoanaippoalavum keathurukka'laippoalavum si’rappaayirukki’rathu.

Song5.16 avar vaay migavum mathuramaayirukki’rathu; avar mut’rilum azhagu'l'lavar. ivarea en neasar; erusaleamin kumaaraththiga'lea! ivarea en sineagithar.

Song6.1 un neasar enggea poanaar? sthireega'lil roobavathiyea! un neasar evvidam poayvittaar? unnoadeakooda naangga'lum avaraith theaduvoam.

Song6.2 thoattangga'lil meayavum, leelipushpangga'laik koyyavum, en neasar thamathu thoattaththukkum kanthavarkkap paaththiga'lukkum poanaar.

Song6.3 naan en neasarudaiyava'l, en neasar ennudaiyavar; avar leelipushpangga'lukku'l'lea meaygi’raar.

Song6.4 en piriyamea! nee thirsaavaippoal saunthariyamum, erusaleamaippoal vadivamum, kodiga'l pa’rakkum padaiyaippoal kediyumaanava'l.

Song6.5 un ka'nga'lai ennaivittuth thiruppu, avaiga'l ennai ven’rathu; un a'lagabaaram keeleayaath malaiyilea thazhaimeayum ve'l'laattu manthaiyaippoalirukki’rathu.

Song6.6 un pa’rka'l ku'lippaattappattuk karaiyea’rugi’ravaiga'lum, on’raanaalum maladaayiraamal irattaik kuttiyeen’ravaiga'lumaana aattumanthaiyaippoalirukki’rathu.

Song6.7 un mukkaattin naduvea un kannangga'l vediththa maatha'lampazhampoalirukki’rathu.

Song6.8 raajasthireega'l a’rubathupearum, ma’rumanaiyaattiga'l e'nbathupearumu'ndu; kanniyarukkuth thogaiyillai.

Song6.9 en pu’raavoa, en uththamiyoa oruththiyea; ava'l than thaaykku orea pi'l'lai; ava'l thannaip pet’rava'lukku arumaiyaanava'l; kumaaraththiga'l ava'laik ka'ndu, ava'lai vaazhththinaarga'l; raajasthireega'lum ma’rumanaiyaattiga'lum ava'laip poat’rinaarga'l.

Song6.10 santhiranaippoal azhagum, sooriyanaippoal piragaasamum, kodiga'l pa’rakkum padaiyaippoal kediyumu'l'lava'laay, aru'noathayampoal uthikki’ra iva'l yaar?

Song6.11 pa'l'laththaakkilea pazhuththa kaniga'laip paarkkavum, thiraadchachsediga'l thu'lirvittu, maatha'lagnsediga'l pooththathaa en’ru a’riyavum, vaathumaith thoattaththukkup poanean.

Song6.12 ninaiyaathathukkumunnea en aaththumaa ennai amminathaabin irathangga'lukku oppaakkit’ru.

Song6.13 thirumbivaa, thirumbivaa, soolamiththiyea! naangga'l unnaip paarkkumpadikku, thirumbivaa, thirumbivaa. soolamiththiyil neengga'l ennaththaip paarkki’reerga'l? ava'l ira'ndu seanaiyin koottaththukkuch samaanamaanava'l.

Song7.1 raajakumaaraththiyea! paatharadchaiga'litta un paathangga'l migavum azhagaayirukki’rathu; un iduppin vadivu visiththirath thozhi’rkaararin vealaiyaagiya poosha'nampoalirukki’rathu.

Song7.2 un naabi thiraadcharasam ni’raintha vattakkalasampoalirukki’rathu; un vayi’ru leelipushpangga'l soozhntha koathumai ambaarampoalirukki’rathu.

Song7.3 un ira'ndu sthanangga'lum ve'limaanin irattaikkuttiga'lukkuch samaanamaayirukki’rathu.

Song7.4 un kazhuththu yaanaiththanthaththinaal seytha koapuraththaippoalavum, un ka'nga'l esboanilea bathrabeem vaasala'ndaiyilirukkum ku'langga'laippoalavum, un mookku thamaskuvin thisaiyai noakkum leebanoanin koapuraththaippoalavum irukki’rathu.

Song7.5 un sirasu karmeal malaiyaippoalirukki’rathu; un thalaimayir iraththaambaramayamaayirukki’rathu; raajaa nadaikkaava'nangga'lil mayanggini’rki’raar.

Song7.6 manamagizhchchiyai u'ndaakkum en piriyamea! nee evva'lavu roobavathi, nee evva'lavu inbamu'l'lava'l.

Song7.7 un uyaram panaimaraththozhunggupoalavum, un sthanangga'l thiraadchakkulaiga'lpoalavum irukki’rathu.

Song7.8 naan panaimaraththilea’ri, athin madalga'laip pidippean en’rean; ippozhuthum un sthanangga'l thiraadchakkulaiga'lpoalavum, un mookkin vaasanai kichchilippazhangga'lpoalavum irukki’rathu.

Song7.9 un tho'ndai, en neasar kudikkaiyil methuvaayi’ranggugi’rathum, u’ranggugi’ravarga'lin uthaduga'laip peasappa'n'nugi’rathumaana, nalla thiraadcharasaththaippoalirukki’rathu.

Song7.10 naan en neasarudaiyava'l, avar piriyam enmealirukki’rathu.

Song7.11 vaarum en neasarea! vayalve'liyil poay, kiraamangga'lil thangguvoam.

Song7.12 athikaalaiyilea thiraadchaththoattangga'lukkup poavoam; thiraadchakkodi thu'lirththu athin poo malarnthathoa en’rum, maatha'lagnsediga'l pooppooththathoa en’rum paarppoam; anggea en neasaththin uchchithangga'lai umakkuth tharuvean.

Song7.13 thoothaayeem pazham vaasanai veesum; namathu vaasalga'la'ndaiyilea puthiyavaiga'lum pazhaiyavaiga'lumaana sagalavitha arumaiyaana kaniga'lumu'ndu; en neasarea! avaiga'lai umakku vaiththirukki’rean.

Song8.1 aa, neer en thaayin paalkudiththa en sagoatharanaippoaliruntheeraanaal, naan ummai ve'liyilea santhiththu muththamiduvean; ennai ninthikkavumaattaarga'l.

Song8.2 naan ummaik koottikko'ndu, en thaayin veettukku azhaiththukko'ndupoavean; neer ennaip poathippeer, kanthavarkkamitta thiraadcharasaththaiyum, en maatha'lampazharasaththaiyum umakkuk kudikkakkoduppean.

Song8.3 avar idathukai en thalaiyin keezhirukkum, avar valathukai ennai a'naikkum.

Song8.4 erusaleamin kumaaraththiga'lea! enakkup piriyamaanava'lukku manathaagumattum neengga'l ava'lai vizhikkappa'n'naamalum ezhuppaamalum irukka ungga'lai aa'naiyidugi’rean.

Song8.5 than neasarmeal saarnthuko'ndu vanaantharaththilirunthu varugi’ra iva'l yaar? kichchilimaraththinkeezh ummai ezhuppinean; anggea umathu thaay ummaip pet’raa'l; anggea ummaip pet’rava'l veathanaippattu ummaip pet’raa'l.

Song8.6 neer ennai umathu iruthayaththinmeal muththiraiyaippoalavum, umathu puyaththinmeal muththiraiyaippoalavum vaiththukko'l'lum; neasam mara'naththaippoal valithu; neasavairaakkiyam paathaa'laththaippoal kodithaayirukki’rathu; athin thazhal akkiniththazhalum athin juvaalai kadum juvaalaiyumaayirukki’rathu.

Song8.7 thira'laana tha'n'neerga'l neasaththai avikkamaattaathu, ve'l'langga'lum athaith tha'nikkamaattaathu; oruvan than veettilu'l'la aasthiga'laiyellaam neasaththukkaagak koduththaalum, athu mut’rilum asattaipa'n'nappadum.

Song8.8 namakku oru si’riya sagoathariyu'ndu, ava'lukku sthanangga'lillai; nammudaiya sagoathariyaik keadkum naa'lil ava'lukkaaga naam enna seyvoam?

Song8.9 ava'l oru mathilaanaal, athinmeal ve'l'likkoattaiyaik kattuvoam; ava'l kathavaanaal, keathuruppalagaiga'lai atha’rku i'naippoam.

Song8.10 naan mathilthaan, en sthanangga'l koapurangga'l; avarudaiya ka'nga'lil kadaadchampe’ralaanean.

Song8.11 baagaal aamoanilea saalomoanukku oru thiraadchaththoattam u'ndaayirunthathu, anthath thoattaththaik kaavalaa'liga'l vasaththilea athin palanukkaaga, ovvoruvan aayiram ve'l'likkaasu ko'nduvarumpadi vittaar.

Song8.12 en thiraadchaththoattam enakku munbaaga irukki’rathu; saalomoanea! umakku antha aayiramum, athin kaniyaik kaakki’ravarga'lukku irunoo’rum searum.

Song8.13 thoattangga'lil vaasampa'n'nugi’rava'lea! thoazhar un saththaththaik keadki’raarga'l; naanum athaik keadkattum.

Song8.14 en neasarea! theeviriyum, kanthavarkkangga'lin malaiga'lmealu'l'la ve'limaanukkum maraiga'lin kuttikkum samaanamaayirum.
