Job1.1 ooths theasaththilea yoabu ennum pearko'nda oru manushan irunthaan; antha manushan uththamanum sanmaarkkanum, theavanukkup bayanthu, pollaappukku vilagugi’ravanumaayirunthaan.

Job1.2 avanukku eazhu kumaararum, moon’ru kumaaraththiga'lum pi’ranthaarga'l.

Job1.3 avanukku eazhaayiram aaduga'lum, moovaayiram ottagangga'lum, ainnoo’ru earmaaduga'lum, ainnoo’ru kazhuthaiga'lumaagiya mirugajeevanga'l irunthathuman’ri, thira'laana pa'nividaikkaararum irunthaarga'l; athinaal antha manushan kizhakkaththip puththirar ellaarilum periyavanaayirunthaan.

Job1.4 avan kumaarar, avanavan thanthan naa'lilea thanthan veettilea virunthuseythu, thangga'l moon’ru sagoathariga'laiyum thangga'loadea poajanam pa'n'numpadi azhaippaarga'l.

Job1.5 virunthuseygi’ra avaravarudaiya naa'lmu’rai mudigi’rapoathu, yoabu: oruvea'lai en kumaarar paavagnseythu, theavanaith thangga'l iruthayaththilea thooshiththiruppaarga'l en’ru solli, avarga'lai azhaiththanuppi, parisuththappaduththi, athikaalamea ezhunthu, avarga'l ellaarudaiya ilakkaththinpadiyeayum sarvaangga thaganabaliga'laich seluththuvaan; inthappiragaaramaaga yoabu annaadka'lilellaam seythuvaruvaan.

Job1.6 orunaa'l theavapuththirar karththarudaiya sannithiyil vanthu nin’rapoathu, saaththaanum avarga'l naduvilea vanthu nin’raan.

Job1.7 karththar saaththaanaip paarththu: nee enggeayirunthu varugi’raay en’raar. saaththaan karththarukkup pirathiyuththaramaaga: boomiyenggum ulaavi, athil sut’riththirinthu varugi’rean en’raan.

Job1.8 karththar saaththaanai noakki: en thaasanaagiya yoabinmeal kavanam vaiththaayoa? uththamanum sanmaarkkanum, theavanukkup bayanthu, pollaappukku vilagugi’ravanumaagiya avanaippoalap boomiyil oruvanum illai en’raar.

Job1.9 atha’rkuch saaththaan karththarukkup pirathiyuththaramaaga: yoabu viruthaavaagavaa theavanukkup bayanthu nadakki’raan?

Job1.10 neer avanaiyum avan veettaiyum avanukku u'ndaana ellaavat’raiyum sut’ri vealiyadaikkavillaiyoa? avan kaiga'lin kiriyaiyai aaseervathiththeer; avanudaiya sambaththu theasaththil perugit’ru.

Job1.11 aanaalum ummudaiya kaiyai neetti avanukku u'ndaanavaiyellaam thoduveeraanaal, appozhuthu avan umathu mugaththukku ethirea ummaith thooshikkaanoa paarum en’raan.

Job1.12 karththar saaththaanai noakki: ithoa, avanukku u'ndaanavaiyellaam un kaiyilirukki’rathu; avanmealmaaththiram un kaiyai neettaathea en’raar; appozhuthu saaththaan karththarudaiya sannithiyaivittup pu’rappattuppoanaan.

Job1.13 pinbu orunaa'l yoabudaiya kumaararum, avan kumaaraththiga'lum, thangga'l mooththa sagoatharan veettilea pusiththu, thiraadcharasam kudikki’rapoathu,

Job1.14 oru aa'l avanidaththil vanthu: eruthuga'l uzhugi’rapoathu, kazhuthaiga'l avaiga'lin pakkaththilea meaynthuko'ndirukkaiyil,

Job1.15 sabeayar avaiga'lmeal vizhunthu, avaiga'laich saayththukko'ndupoanaarga'l; vealaiyaadka'laiyum pattayakkarukkinaal vettippoattaarga'l; naan oruvanmaaththiram thappi, athai umakku a’rivikkumpadi vanthean en’raan.

Job1.16 ivan ippadip peasikko'ndirukkaiyil, vea’roruvan vanthu: vaanaththilirunthu theavanudaiya akkini vizhunthu, aaduga'laiyum vealaiyaadka'laiyum sutteriththuppoattathu; naan oruvanmaaththiram thappi, athai umakku a’rivikkumpadi vanthean en’raan.

Job1.17 ivan ippadip peasikko'ndirukkaiyil, vea’roruvan vanthu: kaltheayar moon’ru pavugnchaay vanthu, ottagangga'lmeal vizhunthu, avaiga'lai oattikko'ndupoanaarga'l; vealaiyaadka'laiyum pattayakkarukkinaal vettippoattaarga'l; naan oruvanmaaththiram thappi, athai umakku a’rivikkumpadi vanthean en’raan.

Job1.18 ivan ippadip peasikko'ndirukkaiyil, vea’roruvan vanthu: ummudaiya kumaararum ummudaiya kumaaraththiga'lum, thangga'l mooththa sagoatharan veettilea pusiththuth thiraadcharasam kudikki’rapoathu,

Job1.19 vanaantharavazhiyaayp perungkaat’ru vanthu, antha veettin naalu moolaiyilum adikka, athu pi'l'laiga'linmeal vizhunthathinaal avarga'l i’ranthupoanaarga'l; naan oruvanmaaththiram thappi, athai umakku a’rivikkumpadi vanthean en’raan.

Job1.20 appozhuthu yoabu ezhunthirunthu, than saalvaiyaik kizhiththu, than thalaiyaich siraiththu, tharaiyilea vizhunthu pa'ninthu:

Job1.21 nirvaa'niyaay en thaayin karppaththilirunthu vanthean; nirvaa'niyaay avvidaththukkuth thirumbuvean; karththar koduththaar, karththar eduththaar; karththarudaiya naamaththukku sthoaththiram en’raan.

Job1.22 ivaiyellaavat’rilum yoabu paavagnseyyavumillai, theavanaippat’rik ku’raisollavumillai.

Job2.1 pinnorunaa'lilea theavapuththirar karththarudaiya sannithiyil vanthu nin’rapoathu, saaththaanum avarga'l naduvilea karththarudaiya sannithiyil vanthu nin’raan.

Job2.2 karththar saaththaanaip paarththu: nee enggeayirunthu varugi’raay en’raar; saaththaan karththarukkup pirathiyuththaramaaga: boomiyenggum ulaavi, athil sut’riththirinthu varugi’rean en’raan.

Job2.3 appozhuthu karththar saaththaanai noakki: nee en thaasanaagiya yoabinmeal kavanam vaiththaayoa? uththamanum sanmaarkkanum, theavanukkup bayanthu, pollaappukku vilagugi’ravanumaana manushanaagiya avanaippoala boomiyil oruvanumillai; mugaantharamillaamal avanai nirmoolamaakkumpadi nee ennai eavinapoathilum, avan innum than uththamaththilea u’ruthiyaay ni’rki’raan en’raar.

Job2.4 saaththaan karththarukkup pirathiyuththaramaaga: thoalukkup bathilaagath thoalaiyum, than jeevanukkup bathilaagath thanakku u'ndaana ellaavat’raiyum, manushan koduththuviduvaan.

Job2.5 aanaalum neer ummudaiya kaiyai neetti, avan elumbaiyum avan maamsaththaiyum thoduveeraanaal, appozhuthu avan umathu mugaththukku ethirea ummaith thooshikkaanoa paarum en’raan.

Job2.6 appozhuthu karththar saaththaanai noakki: ithoa, avan un kaiyilirukki’raan; aagilum avan piraa'nanaimaaththiram thappavidu en’raar.

Job2.7 appozhuthu saaththaan karththarudaiya sannithiyaivittup pu’rappattu, yoabin u'l'langkaalthodanggi avan uchchanthalaimattum kodiya parukka'laal avanai vaathiththaan.

Job2.8 avan oru oattai eduththu, thannaich su’ra'ndikko'ndu saambalil udkaarnthaan.

Job2.9 appozhuthu avan manaivi avanaip paarththu: neer innum ummudaiya uththamaththil u’ruthiyaay ni’rki’reeroa? theavanaith thooshiththu jeevanai vidum en’raa'l.

Job2.10 atha’rku avan: nee payiththiyakkaari peasugi’rathupoalap peasugi’raay; theavan kaiyilea nanmaiyaip pet’ra naam theemaiyaiyum pe’ravea'ndaamoa en’raan; ivaiga'l ellaavat’rilum yoabu than uthaduga'linaal paavagnseyyavillai.

Job2.11 yoabudaiya moon’ru sineagitharaagiya theamaaniyanaana elippaasum, soogiyanaana bilthaathum, naagamaaththiyanaana soappaarum, yoabukku nearitta theemaiga'l yaavaiyum kea'lvippattapoathu, avanukkaagap parithabikkavum, avanukku aa’ruthalsollavum, oruvaroadoruvar yoasanaipa'n'nikko'ndu, avaravar thangga'l sthalangga'lilirunthu vanthaarga'l.

Job2.12 avarga'l thooraththil varugaiyil thangga'l ka'nga'lai ea’reduththup paarththapoathu, avanai uruththeriyaamal, saththamittu azhuthu, avaravar thangga'l saalvaiyaik kizhiththu, vaanaththaip paarththu: thangga'l thalaiga'lmeal puzhuthiyaith thoot’rikko'ndu,

Job2.13 vanthu, avan thukkam mahaakodiya thukkam en’ru ka'ndu, oruvarum avanoadu oru vaarththaiyaiyum peasaamal, iravupagal eazhunaa'l, avanoadukoodath tharaiyilea udkaarnthirunthaarga'l.

Job3.1 atha’rkuppinbu yoabu than vaayaith thi’ranthu, thaan pi’rantha naa'laich sabiththu,

Job3.2 vasaniththuch sonnathu ennaven’raal:

Job3.3 naan pi’ranthanaa'lum oru aa'npi'l'lai u’rpaththiyaayit’ren’ru sollappatta raaththiriyum azhivathaaga.

Job3.4 antha naa'l anthagaarappaduvathaaga; theavan uyaraththilirunthu athai visaariyaamalum, o'li athinmeal piragaasiyaamalum,

Job3.5 anthagaaramum mara'na iru'lum athaik ka’raippaduththi, mappu athai moodi, manthaaranaa'lin bayanggarangga'l athai arukka'lippaakkuvathaaga.

Job3.6 antha raaththiriyai anthagaaram pidippathaaga; varushaththin naadka'lil athu santhoashappadugi’ra naa'laayiraamalum maathangga'lin ka'nakkilea athu varaamalum poavathaaga.

Job3.7 antha raaththiri thanimaiyaayiruppathaaga; athilea kembeerasaththam illaama’rpoavathaaga.

Job3.8 naa'laich sabikki’ravarga'lum, liviyaathaanai ezhumbappa'n'naththakkavarga'lum, athaich sabippaarga'laaga.

Job3.9 athin asthamanakaalaththil thoan’riya nadchaththirangga'l iru'ndu, athu ethirpaarththiruntha ve'lichcham u'ndaagaamalum, vidiya’rkaalaththu ve'luppai athu kaa'naamalum iruppathaaga.

Job3.10 naan iruntha karppaththin vaasalai athu adaikkaamalum, en ka'nga'l kaa'ngi’ra varuththaththai ma’raiththuvidaamalum irunthathea.

Job3.11 naan karppaththilthaanea azhiyaamalum, karppaththilirunthu pu’rappadugi’rapoathea saagaamalum poanathenna?

Job3.12 ennai eanthikko'l'la madiyum, naan paalu'n'na sthanangga'lum u'ndaayirunthathenna?

Job3.13 appadiyillaathirunthaal, asaiyaamal kidanthu amarnthirunthu,

Job3.14 paazhnilangga'lil thangga'lukku maa'ligaiyaikkattina boomiyin raajaakka'loadum manthirimaarga'loadum,

Job3.15 allathu, ponnai udaiyavarga'lum, thangga'l veeduga'lai ve'l'liyinaal nirappinavarga'lumaana pirabukka'loadungkooda naan ippozhuthu thoonggi i'laippaa’ruveanea.

Job3.16 allathu, ve'lippadaatha muthiraappi'ndampoalavum, ve'lichchaththaik kaa'naatha sisukka'lpoalavum iruppeanea.

Job3.17 thunmaarkkarudaiya thontharavu anggea oaynthirukki’rathu; belanat’ru vidaayththuppoanavarga'l anggea i'laippaa’rugi’raarga'l.

Job3.18 kattu'ndirunthavarga'l anggea eagamaaga amainthirukki’raarga'l; odukkugi’ravanudaiya saththam anggea keadkappadugi’rathillai.

Job3.19 si’riyavanum periyavanum anggea sariyaayirukki’raarga'l; adimai than ejamaanukku neenggalaayirukki’raan.

Job3.20 mara'naththukku aasaiyaayk kaaththirunthu, puthaiyalaith theadugi’rathupoala athaith theadiyum adaiyaama’rpoagi’ravarga'lum,

Job3.21 pireathakkuzhiyaik ka'ndupidiththathinaal migavum ka'likoornthu,

Job3.22 atha’rkaagach santhoashappadugi’ra nirpbaakkiyarumaagiya ivarga'lukku ve'lichchamum, manachsagnchalamu'l'la ivarga'lukku jeevanum kodukkappadugi’rathinaal palan enna?

Job3.23 than vazhiyaik kaa'nakkoodaathapadikku, theavanaal va'lainthuko'l'lappattavanukku ve'lichchaththinaal palan enna?

Job3.24 en poajanaththukku munnea enakkup perumoochchu u'ndaagi’rathu; en katha’ruthal ve'l'lampoal pura'ndupoagi’rathu.

Job3.25 naan bayantha kaariyam enakku nearittathu; naan agnchinathu enakku vanthathu.

Job3.26 enakkuch sugamumillai, i'laippaa’ruthalumillai, amaithalumillai; enakkuth thaththa'lippea nearittathu.

Job4.1 appozhuthu theamaaniyanaagiya elippaas pirathiyuththaramaaga:

Job4.2 naangga'l ummudanea peasaththu'ninthaal, aayaasappaduveeroa? aanaalum peasaamal adakkikko'l'laththakkavan yaar?

Job4.3 ithoa, neer aneagarukkup buththisolli, i'laiththa kaiga'laith thidappaduththineer.

Job4.4 vizhugi’ravanai ummudaiya vaarththaiga'laal ni’rkappa'n'ni, tha'l'laadugi’ra muzhangkaalga'laip balappaduththineer.

Job4.5 ippozhuthoa thunbam umakku nearittapadiyinaal aayaasappadugi’reer; athu ummaith thottathinaal kalanggugi’reer.

Job4.6 ummudaiya theavabakthi ummudaiya u’ruthiyaayum, ummudaiya vazhiga'lin uththamam ummudaiya nambikkaiyaayum irukkavea'ndiyathallavoa?

Job4.7 kut’ramillaamal azhinthavan u'ndoa? sanmaarkkar athampa'n'nappattathu eppoa? ithai ninaiththuppaarum.

Job4.8 naan ka'ndirukki’rapadi, aniyaayaththai uzhuthu, theevinaiyai vithaiththavarga'l, athaiyea a’rukki’raarga'l.

Job4.9 theavanudaiya suvaasaththinaalea avarga'l azhinthu, avarudaiya naasiyin kaat’rinaalea nirmoolamaagi’raarga'l.

Job4.10 singgaththin kerchchippum, thushdasinggaththin muzhakkamum adanggum; baalasinggangga'lin pa’rka'lum thagarnthu poam.

Job4.11 kizhachsinggam iraiyillaamaiyaal maa'ndupoam, baalasinggangga'l sitha’ru'ndupoam.

Job4.12 ippoathum oru vaarththai ennidaththil iragasiyamaay a’rivikkappattathu, athinudaiya melliya oasai en seviyil vizhunthathu.

Job4.13 manusharmeal ayarntha niththirai i’ranggugaiyil, iraaththarisanangga'lil palavitha thoat’rangga'l u'ndaagumpoathu,

Job4.14 thigilum nadukkamum ennaip pidiththathu, en elumbuga'lellaam nadungginathu.

Job4.15 appozhuthu oru aavi en mugaththukku munbaagak kadanthathu, en udalin mayir silirththathu.

Job4.16 athu oru uruppoala en ka'nga'lukkumun nin’rathu, aanaalum athin roobam innathen’ru vi'langgavillai; amaithalu'ndaayit’ru, appozhuthu naan keatta saththamaavathu:

Job4.17 manushan theavanaippaarkkilum neethimaanaayiruppaanoa? manupuththiran thannai u'ndaakkinavaraippaarkkilum suththamaayiruppaanoa?

Job4.18 kea'lum, avar thammudaiya pa'nividaikkaararidaththil nambikkai vaippathillai; thammudaiya thootharinmealum buththiyeenaththaich sumaththugi’raarea,

Job4.19 puzhuthiyil asthibaaram poattu, ma'n veeduga'lil vaasampa'n'ni, pottuppoochchiyaal arikkappadugi’ravarga'lmeal avar nambikkai vaippathu eppadi?

Job4.20 kaalaimuthal maalaivaraikkum madinthu, kavanippaar oruvarumillaamal, niththiya azhivadaigi’raarga'l.

Job4.21 avarga'lilirukki’ra avarga'ludaiya meanmai poayvidumallavoa? gnaanamadaiyaamal saagi’raarga'lea en’ru sonnaan.

Job5.1 ippoathu kooppidum, umakku uththaravu koduppaar u'ndoa paarppoam? parisuththavaanga'lil yaarai noakkip paarppeer?

Job5.2 koabam nirmoodanaik kollum; po’raamai buththiyillaathavanai athampa'n'num.

Job5.3 nirmoodan oruvan vearoon’rugi’rathai naan ka'ndu udanea avan vaasasthalaththaich sabiththean.

Job5.4 avan pi'l'laiga'l iradchippukkuth thooramaagi, thappuvippaarillaamal, vaasalilea no’rukkappattaarga'l.

Job5.5 pasiththavan avan vi'laichchalai mudchediga'lukku'l'lumirunthu pa’riththuth thin’raan; pa’rikaaran avan aasthiyai vizhungginaan.

Job5.6 theenggu puzhuthiyilirunthu u'ndaagi’rathumillai; varuththam ma'n'nilirunthu mu'laikki’rathumillai.

Job5.7 akkinippo’riga'l mealea pa’rakki’rathupoala, manushan varuththam anubavikkap pi’ranthirukki’raan.

Job5.8 aanaalum naan theavanai naadi, en niyaayaththaith theavanidaththil oppuvippean.

Job5.9 aaraaynthu mudiyaatha periya kaariyangga'laiyum, e'n'nimudiyaatha athisayangga'laiyum avar seygi’raar.

Job5.10 thaazhnthavarga'lai uyaraththil vaiththu, thukkikki’ravarga'lai iradchiththu uyarththugi’raar.

Job5.11 avar boomiyinmeal mazhaiyai varushikkappa'n'ni, ve'linilangga'linmeal tha'n'neerga'lai varuvikki’raar.

Job5.12 thanthirakkaararin kaiga'l kaariyaththai mudiya nadaththakkoodaathapadikku, avarga'ludaiya ubaayangga'lai avar abaththamaakkugi’raar.

Job5.13 avar gnaaniga'lai avarga'ludaiya thanthiraththilea pidikki’raar; thiriyaavarakkaararin aaloasanai kavizhkkappadum.

Job5.14 avarga'l paga’rkaalaththilea anthagaaraththukku'l'laagi, maththiyaana vea'laiyilea iravil thadavugi’rathupoalath thadavith thirigi’raarga'l.

Job5.15 aanaalum e'liyavanai avarga'l vaayilirukki’ra pattayaththukkum, belavaanin kaikkum vilakki iradchikki’raar.

Job5.16 athinaal thariththiranukku nambikkai u'ndu; theemaiyaanathu than vaayai moodum.

Job5.17 ithoa, theavan tha'ndikki’ra manushan baakkiyavaan; aagaiyaal sarvavallavarudaiya sidchaiyai a’rpamaaga e'n'naathirum.

Job5.18 avar kaayappaduththik kaayangkattugi’raar; avar adikki’raar, avarudaiya kai aat’rugi’rathu.

Job5.19 aa’ru ikkattuga'lukku ummai neenggalaakkuvaar; eazhaavathilum pollaappu ummaith thodaathu.

Job5.20 pagnchakaalaththilea avar ummai mara'naththukkum, yuththaththilea pattayaththin vettukkum vilakki meedpaar.

Job5.21 naavin savukkukkum ma’raikkappaduveer; paazhaakkuthal varumpoathum bayappadaamaliruppeer.

Job5.22 paazhaakkuthalaiyum pagnchaththaiyum paarththu nagaippeer; kaattumirugangga'lukkum bayappadaamaliruppeer.

Job5.23 ve'liyin kalluga'loadum umakku udanpadikkaiyirukkum; ve'liyin mirugangga'lum ummoadea samaathaanamaayirukkum.

Job5.24 ummudaiya koodaaram samaathaanaththoadirukkak kaa'nbeer; ummudaiya vaasasthalaththai visaarikkumpoathu ku’raivaik kaa'namaatteer.

Job31.2 appozhuthu unnathangga'lilirunthu theavan a'likkum panggum, unnathaththilirunthu sarvavallavar kodukkum suthantharamum kidaikkumoa?

Job39.1 varaiyaaduga'l eenungkaalaththai a’rivaayoa? maanga'l kuttipoadugi’rathaik kavaniththaayoa?

Job39.2 avaiga'l sinaippattirunthu varugi’ra maathangga'lai nee e'n'ni, avaiga'l eenungkaalaththai a’rivaayoa?

Job39.3 avaiga'l nonthu kuninthu thangga'l kuttiga'laip poattu, thangga'l veathanaiga'lai neekkividum.

Job39.4 avaiga'lin kuttiga'l balaththu, vanaththilea va'larnthu, avaiga'la'ndaikkuth thirumba varaama’rpoayvidum.

Job39.5 kaattukkazhuthaiyaith thannichchaiyaayth thiriyavittavar yaar? anthak kaattukkazhuthaiyin kattuga'lai avizhththavar yaar?

Job39.6 atha’rku naan vanaantharaththai veedaagavum, uvarnilaththai vaasasthalamaagavum koduththean.

Job39.7 athu patta'naththin iraichchalai aladchiyampa'n'ni, oattugi’ravanudaiya kookkuralai mathikki’rathillai.

Job39.8 athu malaiga'lilea than meaychchalaik ka'ndupidiththu, sagalavithap pachchaippoo'nduga'laiyum theadiththiriyum.

Job39.9 kaa'ndaamirugam unnidaththil seavikkach sammathikkumoa? athu un munna'naikku munbaaga iraaththanggumoa?

Job39.10 padaichchaalga'lai uzha nee kaa'ndaamirugaththaik kayi’rupoattu earilea poottuvaayoa? athu unakku isainthu parampadikkumoa?

Job39.11 athu athiga belamu'l'lathen’ru nee nambi athinidaththil vealai vaangguvaayoa?

Job39.12 un thaaniyaththai athu un veettil ko'nduvanthu, un ka'lagnchiyaththil searkkum en’ru nee athai nambuvaayoa?

Job39.13 theekkuruviga'l thangga'l settaiga'lai asaivaatti oadugi’ra oattam, naarai than settaiga'laalum i’raguga'laalum pa’rakki’ratha’rkuch samaanamallavoa?

Job39.14 athu than muttaiga'laith tharaiyilea ittu, avaiga'lai ma'nalilea analu’raikka vaiththuvittuppoay,

Job39.15 kaalaal mithipattu udainthupoam enbathaiyum, kaattumirugangga'l avaiga'lai mithiththuvidum enbathaiyum ninaikki’rathillai.

Job39.16 athu than kugnchuga'l thannudaiyathallaathathupoala avaiga'laik kaakkaatha kadinaku'namu'l'lathaayirukkum; avaiga'lukkaaga atha’rkuk kavalaiyillaathapadiyinaal athu patta varuththam viruthaavaam.

Job39.17 theavan atha’rkup buththiyaik kodaamal, gnaanaththai vilakkivaiththaar.

Job39.18 athu settai viriththu ezhumbumpoathu, kuthiraiyaiyum athinmeal ea’riyirukki’ravanaiyum aladchiyampa'n'num.

Job39.19 kuthiraikku nee veeriyaththaik koduththaayoa? athin tho'ndaiyil kumu’ralai vaiththaayoa?

Job39.20 oru vettukki'liyai mirattugi’rathupoala athai mirattuvaayoa? athinudaiya naasiyin serukku bayanggaramaayirukki’rathu.

Job39.21 athu tharaiyilea thaa'ladiththu, than balaththil ka'liththu, aayuthangga'laith thariththavarukku ethiraagap pu’rappadum.

Job39.22 athu kalanggaamalum, pattayaththukkup pinvaanggaamalumirunthu, bayappaduthalai aladchiyampa'n'num.

Job39.23 amba’raaththoo'niyum, minnugi’ra eettiyum, keadagamum athinmeal kalakalakkumpoathu,

Job39.24 karvamum moorkkamungko'ndu tharaiyai vizhunggividugi’rathupoal anumaaniththu, ekkaa'laththin thonikku agnchaamal paayum.

Job39.25 ekkaa'lam thonikkumpoathu athu nigiyen’ru kanaikkum; yuththaththaiyum, padaiththalaivarin aarpparippaiyum, seanaiga'lin aaravaaraththaiyum thooraththilirunthu moappam pidikkum.

Job39.26 un buththiyinaalea raajaa'li pa’ranthu, the’rkukku ethiraagath than settaiga'lai virikki’rathoa?

Job39.27 un ka’rpanaiyinaalea kazhugu uyarap pa’ranthu, uyaraththilea than koottaik kattumoa?

Job39.28 athu kanmalaiyilum, kanmalaiyin sigaraththilum, ara'naana sthalaththilum thanggi vaasampa'n'num.

Job39.29 anggeayirunthu iraiyai noakkum; athin ka'nga'l thooraththilirunthu athaip paarkkum.

Job39.30 athin kugnchuga'l iraththaththai u’rignchum; pi'nam enggeayoa anggea kazhugu searum en’raar.

Job40.1 pinnum karththar yoabukku uththaramaaga:

Job40.2 sarvavallavaroadea vazhakkaadi avarukkup buththi padippikki’ravan yaar? theavanpearil kut’ram pidikki’ravan ivaiga'lukku uththaravu sollakkadavan en’raar.

Job40.3 appozhuthu yoabu karththarukkup pirathiyuththaramaaga:

Job40.4 ithoa, naan neesan; naan umakku enna ma’ruuththaravu solluvean; en kaiyinaal en vaayaip poththikko'l'lugi’rean.

Job40.5 naan ira'ndorutharam peasinean; ini naan pirathiyuththaram kodaamalum peasaamalum iruppean en’raan.

Job40.6 appozhuthu karththar perungkaat’ril irunthu yoabukku uththaravu aru'linaar.

Job40.7 ippoathum purushanaippoala nee araikattikko'l; naan unnaik keadpean; nee enakku uththaravu sollu.

Job40.8 nee en niyaayaththai avamaakkuvaayoa? nee unnai neethimaanaakkikko'l'lumpadikku enmeal kut’ragnsumaththuvaayoa?

Job40.9 theavanudaiya puyaththaippoal unakkup puyamu'ndoa? avaraippoal idimuzhakkamaaych saththamidakkoodumoa?

Job40.10 ippoathum nee mukkiyaththaalum magaththuvaththaalum unnai alanggariththu, magimaiyaiyum kanaththaiyum thariththukko'ndu,

Job40.11 nee un koabaththin ukkiraththai veesi, aganthaiyu'l'lavanaiyellaam theadippaarththuth thaazhththivittu,

Job40.12 perumaiyu'l'lavanaiyellaam kavaniththu, avanaip pa'niyappa'n'ni, thunmaarkkarai avarga'lirukki’ra sthalaththilea mithiththuvidu.

Job40.13 nee avarga'lai eagamaayp puzhuthiyilea puthaiththu, avarga'l mugangga'lai antharanggaththilea kattippoadu.

Job40.14 appozhuthu un valathukai unakku iradchippu u'ndupa'n'num en’ru solli, naan unnaip pugazhuvean.

Job40.15 ippoathum bigemoaththai nee kavaniththuppaar; unnai u'ndaakkinathupoala athaiyum u'ndaakkinean; athu maattaippoal pullaith thinnum.

Job40.16 ithoa, athinudaiya belan athin iduppilum, athin veeriyam athin vayit’rin narambuga'lilum irukki’rathu.

Job40.17 athu than vaalaik keathurumaraththaippoal neettugi’rathu; athin iduppu narambuga'l pinnikko'ndirukki’rathu.

Job40.18 athin elumbuga'l kettiyaana ve'ngalaththaippoalavum, athin asthiga'l iruppuk kambiga'laippoalavum irukki’rathu.

Job40.19 athu theavanudaiya kiriyaiga'lil pirathaanamaana oru kiriyai, athai u'ndaakkinavar atha’rku oru pattayaththaiyum koduththaar.

Job40.20 kaattumirugangga'l yaavum vi'laiyaadugi’ra malaiga'l atha’rku meaychchalai vi'laivikkum.

Job40.21 athu nizhalu'l'la sediga'lin keezhum, naa'nalin ma’raivilum, u'laiyilum paduththukko'l'lum.

Job40.22 thazhaiga'lin nizhal athaik kavinthu, nathiyin alariga'l athaich soozhnthuko'l'lum.

Job40.23 ithoa, nathi pura'nduvanthaalum athu bayanthoadaathu; yoarthaan nathiyaththanai tha'n'neer athin mugaththil moathinaalum athu asaiyaamalirukkum.

Job40.24 athin ka'nga'l paarththirukka athai yaar pidikkakkoodum? mookka'naangkayi’rupoada athin mookkai yaar kuththakkoodum?

Job41.1 liviyaathaanai thoo'ndilinaal pidikkakkoodumoa? athin naakkai nee vidugi’ra kayit’rinaalea pidikkakkoodumoa?

Job41.2 athin mookkai naarkkayi’rupoattuk kattakkoodumoa? ku’rattinaal athin thaadaiyai uruvak kuththakkoodumoa?

Job41.3 athu unnaip paarththu aneaga vi'n'nappagn seyyumoa? unnai noakki ichchagavaarththaiga'laich sollumoa?

Job41.4 athu unnoadea udanpadikkaipa'n'numoa? athaich sathaakaalamum adimaiko'lvaayoa?

Job41.5 oru kuruviyoadea vi'laiyaadugi’rathupoal, nee athanoadea vi'laiyaadi, athai nee un pe'nmakka'la'ndaiyilea kattivaippaayoa?

Job41.6 koottaa'liga'l athaip pidikkap pirayaththanappattu, athai viyaabaariga'lukkup panggiduvaarga'loa?

Job41.7 nee athin thoalai aneaga ambuga'linaalum, athin thalaiyai e’rivallaiyangga'linaalum e’rivaayoa?

Job41.8 athinmeal un kaiyaippoadu, yuththaththai ninaiththukko'l; ini appadich seyyath thu'niyamaattaay.

Job41.9 ithoa, athaip pidikkalaam en’ru nambinavan moasampoay, athaip paarththavudanea vizhuvaan allavoa?

Job41.10 athai ezhuppaththakka thairiyavaan illaathirukka, enakku munbaaga ni’rpavan yaar?

Job41.11 thanakkup bathilkodukkappadumpadi, munthi enakkuk koduththavan yaar? vaanaththin keezhu'l'lavaiga'l ellaam ennudaiyavaiga'l.

Job41.12 athin anggangga'lum, athin veeriyamum, athin udal isaivin nearththiyum innathen’ru naan sollaamal ma’raikkamaattean.

Job41.13 athu moodiyirukki’ra athin poarvaiyaik ki'lappakkoodiyavan yaar? athin ira'ndu thaadaiga'lin naduvea kadivaa'lam poadaththakkavan yaar?

Job41.14 athin mugaththin kathavaith thi’rakkakkoodiyavan yaar? sut’rilumirukki’ra athin pa’rka'l bayanggaramaanavaiga'l.

Job41.15 muththiraip pathippuppoala azhuththangko'ndu adarththiyaayirukki’ra athin parisaiga'lin ara'nippu mahaa si’rappaayirukki’rathu.

Job41.16 avaiga'l naduvea kaat’rum pugamaattaatha nerukkamaay avaiga'l on’roadon’ru i'naikkappattirukki’rathu.

Job41.17 avaiga'l on’roadon’ru ottikko'ndu i'naipiriyaamal pidiththukko'ndirukki’rathu.

Job41.18 athu thummugaiyil o'li veesum, athin ka'nga'l aru'noathayaththin puruvangga'laippoal irukki’rathu.

Job41.19 athin vaayilirunthu erigi’ra panthangga'l pu’rappattu, akkinippo’riga'l pa’rakkum.

Job41.20 kothikki’ra sattiyilum kopparaiyilum irunthu pu’rappadugi’rathupoala, athin naasiga'lilirunthu pugai pu’rappadum.

Job41.21 athin suvaasam kariga'laik ko'luththum, athin vaayilirunthu juvaalai pu’rappadum.

Job41.22 athin kazhuththilea belan kudiko'ndirukkum; bayanggaram atha’rkumun kooththaadum.

Job41.23 athin uda’rkoo’ruga'l, asaiyaatha kettiyaay on’roadon’ru ottikko'ndirukkum.

Job41.24 athin negnchu kallaippoalavum, eanthiraththin adikkallaippoalavum kettiyaayirukkum.

Job41.25 athu ezhumbumpoathu balasaaliga'l agnchi bayaththinaal mayanggith thigaippaarga'l.

Job41.26 athaith thaakkugi’ravanudaiya pattayam, eetti, vallaiyam, kavasam, on’rum atha’rkumun ni’rkaathu.

Job41.27 athu irumbai vaikkoalaagavum, ve'ngalaththai u'luththa maramaagavum e'n'num.

Job41.28 ambu athaith thuraththaathu; kava'nka’rka'l atha’rkuth thurumbaagum.

Job41.29 athu perunthadiga'laith thaa'ladiga'laaga e'n'ni, eettiyin asaivai igazhum.

Job41.30 athin keezhaagak koormaiyaana ka’rka'l kidanthaalum, athu seat’rinmeal oadugi’rathupoalak karukkaana avaiga'linmealum oadum.

Job41.31 athu aazhaththai ulaippaanaiyaippoal ponggappa'n'ni, kadalaith thailampoalak kalakkividum.

Job41.32 athu thanakkup pinnaagap paathaiyaith thulanggappa'n'num; aazhamaanathu ve'luppaana naraiyaippoal vi'langgum.

Job41.33 boomiyinmeal atha’rku oppaanathu on’rumillai; athu nirpbayamaayirukka u'ndupa'n'nappattathu.

Job41.34 athu meattimaiyaanathaiyellaam a’rpamaay e'n'nugi’rathu; athu aganggaaramu'l'la jeevanga'lukkellaam raajaavaayirukki’rathu en’raar.

Job42.1 appozhuthu yoabu karththarukkup pirathiyuththaramaaga:

Job42.2 theavareer sagalaththaiyum seyya vallavar; neer seyya ninaiththathu thadaipadaathu enbathai a’rinthirukki’rean.

Job42.3 a’rivillaamal aaloasanaiyai ma’raikki’ra ivan yaar? aagaiyaal naan enakkuth theriyaathathaiyum, en buththikku ettaathathaiyum, naan a’riyaathathaiyum alappinean engi’rean.

Job42.4 neer enakkuch sevikodum, appozhuthu naan peasuvean; naan ummaik kea'lvikeadpean, neer enakku uththaravu sollum.

Job42.5 en kaathinaal ummaikku’riththuk kea'lvippattean; ippozhuthoa en ka'n ummaik kaa'ngi’rathu.

Job42.6 aagaiyaal naan ennai aruvaruththu, thoo'lilum saambalilum irunthu manasthaabappadugi’rean en’raan.

Job42.7 karththar intha vaarththaiga'lai yoaboadea peasinapin, karththar theamaaniyanaana elippaasai noakki: unmealum un ira'ndu sineagitharmealum enakkuk koabam moo'lugi’rathu; en thaasanaagiya yoabu peasinathupoal, neengga'l ennaikku’riththu nithaanamaayp peasavillai.

Job42.8 aathalaal neengga'l eazhu kaa'laiga'laiyum, eazhu aattukkadaakka'laiyum therinthuko'ndu, en thaasanaagiya yoabinidaththil poay, ungga'lukkaagach sarvaangga thaganabaliga'lai idungga'l; en thaasanaagiya yoabum ungga'lukkaaga vea'nduthal seyvaan; naan avan mugaththaip paarththu, ungga'lai ungga'l buththiyeenaththukkuth thakkathaaga nadaththaathiruppean; en thaasanaagiya yoabu peasinathupoal, neengga'l ennaikku’riththu nithaanamaayp peasavillai en’raar.

Job42.9 appozhuthu theamaaniyanaana elippaasum soogiyanaana bilthaathum naagamaaththiyanaana soappaarum poay, karththar thangga'lukkuch sonnapadiyea seythaarga'l; appozhuthu karththar yoabin mugaththaip paarththaar.

Job42.10 yoabu than sineagitharukkaaga vea'nduthal seythapoathu, karththar avan si’raiyiruppai maat’rinaar. yoabukku mun iruntha ellaavat’raippaarkkilum ira'ndaththanaiyaayk karththar avanukkuth thantharu'linaar.

Job42.11 appozhuthu avanudaiya ellaach sagoathararum sagoathariga'lum, mun avanukku a’rimugamaana anaivarum avanidaththil vanthu, avan veettilea avanoadea poajanampa'n'ni, karththar avanmeal varappa'n'nina sagala theengginimiththam avanukkaaga anggalaayththu, avanukku aa’ruthal solli, avaravar ovvoru thanggakkaasaiyum, avaravar ovvoru pon aabara'naththaiyum avanukkuk koduththaarga'l.

Job42.12 karththar yoabin munnilaimaiyaip paarkkilum avan pinnilaimaiyai aaseervathiththaar; pathinaalaayiram aaduga'lum, aa’raayiram ottagangga'lum, aayiram earga'lum, aayiram kazhuthaiga'lum avanukku u'ndaayina.

Job42.13 eazhu kumaararum, moon’ru kumaaraththiga'lum avanukkup pi’ranthaarga'l.

Job42.14 mooththa maga'lukku emeemaa'l en’rum, ira'ndaam maga'lukkuk kethseeyaa'l en’rum, moon’raam maga'lukkuk keareanaappuk en’rum pearittaan.

Job42.15 theasaththil enggum yoabin kumaaraththiga'laippoal saunthariyamaana pe'nga'l kaa'nappadavillai; avarga'l thagappan avarga'l sagoathararin naduvilea avarga'lukkuch suthantharam koduththaan.

Job42.16 itha’rkuppinbu yoabu noot’runaa’rpathu varusham uyiroadirunthu, naalu thalaimu’raiyaagath than pi'l'laiga'laiyum than pi'l'laiga'ludaiya pi'l'laiga'laiyum ka'ndaan.

Job42.17 yoabu nedunaa'lirunthu, poora'na vayathu'l'lavanaay mariththaan.
