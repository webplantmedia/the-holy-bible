Hag1.1 raajaavaagiya thariyu arasaa'nda ira'ndaam varusham aa'raam maatham muthalaantheathiyilea, karththarudaiya vaarththai aagaay ennum theerkkatharisiyin moolamaay seyalththiyealin kumaaranaagiya serubaabeal ennum yoothaavin thalaivanukkum, yoathsathaakkin kumaaranaagiya yoasuvaa ennum pirathaana aasaariyanukkum u'ndaagi, avar sonnathu ennaven'raal:

Hag1.2 intha janangga'l karththarudaiya aalayaththaik kattugi'ratha'rku eat'rakaalam innum varavillai engi'raarga'l en'ru seanaiga'lin karththar sollugi'raar.

Hag1.3 aanaalum aagaay ennum theerkkatharisiyin moolamaayk karththarudaiya vaarththai u'ndaagi, avar sollugi'raar:

Hag1.4 intha veedu paazhaayk kidakkumpoathu, neengga'l machchuppaavappatta ungga'l veeduga'lil kudiyirukkumpadiyaana kaalam ithuvoa?

Hag1.5 ippoathum seanaiga'lin karththar sollugi'raar: ungga'l vazhiga'laich sinthiththuppaarungga'l.

Hag1.6 neengga'l thira'laay vithaiththum kognchamaay a'ruththukko'nduvarugi'reerga'l; neengga'l pusiththum thirupthiyaagavillai; kudiththum paripoora'namadaiyavillai; neengga'l vasthiram uduththiyum oruvanukkum ku'lirvidavillai; kooliyaich sambaathikki'ravan poththalaana paiyilea poadugi'ravanaay athaich sambaathikki'raan.

Hag1.7 ungga'l vazhiga'laich sinthiththup paarungga'l en'ru seanaiga'lin karththar sollugi'raar.

Hag1.8 neengga'l malaiyinmeal ea'rippoay, marangga'lai vettikko'nduvanthu, aalayaththaik kattungga'l; athinpearil naan piriyamaayiruppean, athinaal en magimai vi'langgum en'ru karththar sollugi'raar.

Hag1.9 athigamaay varumen'ru neengga'l ethirpaarththirunthum, ithoa, kogncham kidaiththathu; neengga'l a'ruththu veettukkuk ko'nduvanthum, naan athai oothippoadugi'rean; ethinimiththamen'raal, en veedu paazhaaykkidakkumpoathu, neengga'l ellaarum avanavan than than veetti'rku oadippoagi'reerga'lea, ithinimiththamea en'ru seanaiga'lin karththar sollugi'raar.

Hag1.10 aathalaal ungga'lmeal irukki'ra vaanam paniyaip peyyaamalum, boomi palanaik kodaamalum poayit'ru.

Hag1.11 naan nilaththinmealum, malaiga'linmealum, thaaniyaththinmealum, puthu thiraadcharasaththinmealum, e'n'neyinmealum, boomiyil vi'laigi'ra ellaavat'rinmealum, manusharinmealum, mirugangga'linmealum, kaippaadu anaiththinmealum va'radchiyai varuviththean en'raar.

Hag1.12 appozhuthu seyalththiyealin kumaaranaagiya serubaabealum, yoathsathaakkin kumaaranaagiya yoasuvaa ennum pirathaana aasaariyanum, janaththil meethiyaana anaivarum thangga'l theavanaagiya karththarudaiya saththaththukkum, thangga'l theavanaagiya karththar anuppina aagaay ennum theerkkatharisiyinudaiya vaarththaiga'lukkum sevikoduththaarga'l, janangga'l karththarukku munbaagap bayanthirunthaarga'l.

Hag1.13 appozhuthu karththarudaiya thoothanaagiya aagaay, karththar thoothanuppiya vaarththaiyinpadi janangga'lai noakki: naan ungga'loadea irukki'rean en'ru karththar sollugi'raar en'raan.

Hag1.14 pinbu karththar seyalththiyealin kumaaranaagiya serubaabeal ennum yoothaavin thalaivanudaiya aaviyaiyum, yoathsathaakkin kumaaranaagiya yoasuvaa ennum pirathaana aasaariyanudaiya aaviyaiyum, janaththil meethiyaana ellaarudaiya aaviyaiyum ezhuppinaar; avarga'l vanthu, thangga'l theavanaagiya seanaiga'ludaiya karththarin aalayaththilea vealaiseythaarga'l.

Hag1.15 thariyu raajaavin ira'ndaam varusham aa'raam maatham irubaththunaalaantheathiyilea ithu nadanthathu.

Hag2.1 eazhaam maatham irubaththoaraantheathiyilea aagaay ennum theerkkatharisiyin moolamaay, karththarudaiya vaarththai u'ndaayit'ru; avar:

Hag2.2 nee seyalththiyealin kumaaranaagiya serubaabeal ennum yoothaavin thalaivanoadum, yoathsathaakkin kumaaranaagiya yoasuvaa ennum pirathaana aasaariyanoadum, janaththil meethiyaanavarga'loadum sollavea'ndiyathu ennaven'raal:

Hag2.3 intha aalayaththin munthina magimaiyaik ka'ndavarga'lil ungga'lukku'l'lea meenthirukki'ravarga'l yaar? ippozhuthu ithu ungga'lukku eppadik kaa'ngi'rathu? atha'rku ithu ungga'l paarvaiyil on'rumillaathathupoal kaa'ngi'rathallavaa?

Hag2.4 aanaalum serubaabealea, nee thidanko'l en'ru karththar sollugi'raar; yoathsathaakkin kumaaranaagiya yoasuvaa ennum pirathaana aasaariyanea, nee thidanko'l; theasaththin ellaa janangga'lea, neengga'l thidanko'l'lungga'l, vealaiyai nadaththungga'l en'ru karththar sollugi'raar; naan ungga'ludanea irukki'rean en'ru seanaiga'lin karththar sollugi'raar.

Hag2.5 neengga'l egipthilirunthu pu'rappadugi'rapoathu naan ungga'loadea udanpadikkaipa'n'nina vaarththaiyinpadiyea, en aaviyaanavarum ungga'l naduvil nilaiko'ndiruppaar; bayappadaatheayungga'l.

Hag2.6 seanaiga'lin karththar sollugi'raar: kognchakkaalaththukku'l'lea innum orutharam naan vaanaththaiyum boomiyaiyum samuththiraththaiyum vettaantharaiyaiyum asaiyappa'n'nuvean.

Hag2.7 sagala jaathiga'laiyum asaiyappa'n'nuvean, sagala jaathiga'laalum virumbappattavar varuvaar; intha aalayaththai magimaiyinaal ni'raiyappa'n'nuvean en'ru seanaiga'lin karththar sollugi'raar.

Hag2.8 ve'l'liyum ennudaiyathu, ponnum ennudaiyathu en'ru seanaiga'lin karththar sollugi'raar.

Hag2.9 munthina aalayaththin magimaiyaippaarkkilum, inthap pinthina aalayaththin magimai perithaayirukkum en'ru seanaiga'lin karththar sollugi'raar; ivvidaththilea samaathaanaththaik katta'laiyiduvean en'ru seanaiga'lin karththar uraikki'raar en'ru sol en'raar.

Hag2.10 thariyuvin ira'ndaam varusham onbathaam maatham irubaththunaalaantheathiyilea, aagaay ennum theerkkatharisiyin moolamaayk karththarudaiya vaarththai u'ndaayit'ru; avar:

Hag2.11 oruvan than vasthiraththin thonggalilea parisuththa maamsaththaik ko'ndupoagaiyil than vasthiraththin thonggal appaththaiyaagilum, saathaththaiyaagilum, thiraadcharasaththaiyaagilum, e'n'neyaiyaagilum, mat'rentha poajanapathaarththaththaiyaagilum thottaal athu parisuththamaagumoa en'ru nee aasaariyaridaththil veatha niyaayaththaippat'rik kea'l en'ru seanaiga'lin karththar sollugi'raar en'raar.

Hag2.12 atha'rku aasaariyarga'l pirathiyuththaramaaga: parisuththamaagaathu en'raarga'l.

Hag2.13 pi'naththaal theettuppattavan avaiga'lil ethaiyaagilum thottaal, athu theettuppadumoa en'ru aagaay pinnum keattaan; atha'rku aasaariyarga'l pirathiyuththaramaaga: theettuppadum en'raarga'l.

Hag2.14 appozhuthu aagaay: appadiyea intha janangga'lum intha jaathiyaarum en samugaththil irukki'raarga'l en'ru karththar sollugi'raar; avarga'ludaiya kaiga'lin ellaak kiriyaiga'lum appadiyea irukki'rathu; avarga'l anggea ko'nduvanthu padaikki'rathum theettuppattirukki'rathu.

Hag2.15 ippoathum karththarudaiya aalayaththaik kattumpadi oru kallinmeal oru kal vaikkappattathu muthal nadanthathai ungga'l manathilea sinthiththuppaarungga'l.

Hag2.16 antha naadka'l muthal oruvan irubathu marakkaalaagak ka'nda ambaaraththinidaththil vanthapoathu, paththu marakkaalmaaththiram irunthathu; oruvan aalaiyin thottiyil aimbathu kudam mo'l'la aalaiyinidaththilea vanthapoathu irubathu kudammaaththiram irunthathu.

Hag2.17 karukkaayinaalum vishappaniyinaalum kalmazhaiyinaalum ungga'lai ungga'l kaiga'lin vealaiyilellaam adiththean; aanaalum neengga'l ennidaththil manathaith thiruppaamal poaneerga'l en'ru karththar sollugi'raar.

Hag2.18 ippoathum itha'rku munthinakaalaththil nadanthathai ungga'l manathilea sinthiththuppaarungga'l; onbathaam maatham irubaththunaalaantheathiyaagiya innaa'lmuthal karththarudaiya aalayaththin asthibaaram poadappatta annaa'lvaraikkum sen'rakaalaththil nadanthathai ungga'l manathilea sinthiththuppaarungga'l.

Hag2.19 ka'lagnchiyaththil innum vithaiththaaniyam u'ndoa? thiraadchachsediyum aththimaramum maatha'lagnsediyum olivamaramum kanikodukkavillaiyea; naan in'rumuthal ungga'lai aaseervathippean en'ru sollugi'raar en'raan.

Hag2.20 irubaththunaalaantheathiyaagiya annaa'lilea karththarudaiya vaarththai ira'ndaam visai aagaay enbavanukku u'ndaagi, avar:

Hag2.21 nee yoothaavin thalaivanaagiya serubaabealoadea sollavea'ndiyathu ennaven'raal, naan vaanaththaiyum boomiyaiyum asaiyappa'n'ni,

Hag2.22 raajyangga'lin singgaasanaththaik kavizhththu, jaathiga'ludaiya raajyangga'lin belaththai azhiththu, irathaththaiyum athil ea'riyirukki'ravarga'laiyum kavizhththuppoaduvean; kuthiraiga'loadea avaiga'linmeal ea'riyiruppavarga'lum avaravar thangga'l thangga'l sagoatharanin pattayaththinaalea vizhuvaarga'l.

Hag2.23 seanaiga'lin karththar sollugi'raar: seyalththiyealin kumaaranaagiya serubaabeal ennum en oozhiyakkaaranea, unnai naan annaa'lilea searththukko'ndu, unnai muththirai moathiramaaga vaippean en'ru karththar sollugi'raar; naan unnaith therinthuko'ndean en'ru seanaiga'lin karththar uraikki'raar en'ru sol en'raar.
