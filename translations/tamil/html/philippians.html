Php1.1 iyeasuki’risthuvin oozhiyakkaararaagiya pavulum theemoaththeayum, pilippi patta'naththil ki’risthu iyeasuvukku'l'laana parisuththavaanga'l anaivarukkum, ka'nkaa'niga'lukkum, uthavikkaararukkum ezhuthugi’rathaavathu:

Php1.2 nammudaiya pithaavaagiya theavanaalum karththaraagiya iyeasuki’risthuvinaalum ungga'lukkuk kirubaiyum samaathaanamum u'ndaavathaaga.

Php1.3 suviseasham ungga'lukku a’rivikkappatta naa'lmuthal ithuvaraikkum neengga'l atha’rku udanpattavarga'laanapadiyaal,

Php1.4 naan pa'n'nugi’ra ovvoru vi'n'nappaththilum ungga'l anaivarukkaagavum eppoathum santhoashaththoadea vi'n'nappampa'n'ni,

Php1.5 ungga'lil na’rkiriyaiyaith thodangginavar athai iyeasuki’risthuvin naa'lpariyantham mudiya nadaththivaruvaaren’ru nambi,

Php1.6 naan ungga'lai ninaikki’rapozhuthellaam en theavanai sthoaththirikki’rean.

Php1.7 en kattuga'lilum, naan suviseashaththi’rkaaga uththaravusolli athaith thidappaduththivarugi’rathilum, neengga'l anaivarum enakku a'likkappatta kirubaiyil panggu'l'lavarga'laanathaal, ungga'lai en iruthayaththil thariththukko'ndirukki’rapadiyinaalea, ungga'lellaaraiyung ku’riththu naan ippadi ninaikki’rathu enakkuth thaguthiyaayirukki’rathu.

Php1.8 iyeasuki’risthuvin urukkamaana anbilea ungga'lellaarmealum evva'lavoa vaagnchaiyaayirukki’rean enbatha’rkuth theavanea enakkuch saadchi.

Php1.9 mealum, uththamamaanavaiga'lai neengga'l oppukko'l'laththakkathaaga ungga'l anbaanathu a’rivilum ellaa u'narvilum innum athigamathigamaayp perugavum,

Php1.10 theavanukku magimaiyum thuthiyum u'ndaagumpadi iyeasuki’risthuvinaal varugi’ra neethiyin kaniga'laal ni’rainthavarga'laagi,

Php1.11 neengga'l ki’risthuvin naa'lukken’ru thuppuravaanavarga'lum ida’ralat’ravarga'lumaayirukkavum vea'nduthal seygi’rean.

Php1.12 sagoathararea, enakkuch sambaviththavaiga'l suviseasham pirabalamaagumpadikku eathuvaayit’ren’ru neengga'l a’riya manathaayirukki’rean.

Php1.13 aramanai yenggumu'l'lavarga'lukkum mat’ra yaavarukkum en kattuga'l ki’risthuvukku'l'laana kattuga'len’ru ve'liyaranggamaagi,

Php1.14 sagoathararil aneagar en kattuga'laalea karththarukku'l thidanko'ndu bayamillaamal thiruvasanaththaich sollumpadi athigamaayth thu'ninthirukki’raarga'l.

Php1.15 silar po’raamaiyinaalum viroathaththinaalum, silar nalmanathinaalum ki’risthuvaip pirasanggikki’raarga'l.

Php1.16 silar en kattuga'loadea ubaththiravaththaiyungkootta ninaiththu, suththamanathoadea ki’risthuvai a’riviyaamal, viroathaththinaalea a’rivikki’raarga'l.

Php1.17 suviseashaththi’rkaaga naan uththaravu solla ea’rpaduththappattavanen’ru a’rinthu, silar anbinaalea a’rivikki’raarga'l.

Php1.18 ithanaalenna? vagnchagaththinaalaavathu, u'nmaiyinaalaavathu, eppadiyaavathu, ki’risthu a’rivikkappadugi’raar; athanaal santhoashappadugi’rean, innamum santhoashappaduvean.

Php1.19 athu ungga'l vea'nduthalinaalum iyeasuki’risthuvinudaiya aaviyin uthaviyinaalum enakku iradchippaaga mudiyumen’ru a’rivean.

Php1.20 naan on’rilum vedkappattuppoagaamal, eppozhuthumpoala ippozhuthum, miguntha thairiyaththoadea jeevanaalaagilum, saavinaalaagilum, ki’risthu en sareeraththinaalea magimaippaduvaaren’ru enakku u'ndaayirukki’ra vaagnchaikkum nambikkaikkum thakkathaay, appadi mudiyum.

Php1.21 ki’risthu enakku jeevan, saavu enakku aathaayam.

Php1.22 aagilum sareeraththil pizhaiththirukki’rathinaalea en kiriyaikkup palanu'ndaayiruppathaal, naan therinthuko'l'lavea'ndiyathu innathen’ru a’riyean.

Php1.23 eanenil ivvira'ndinaalum naan nerukkappadugi’rean; theagaththaivittup pirinthu, ki’risthuvudaneakooda irukka enakku aasaiyu'ndu, athu athiga nanmaiyaayirukkum;

Php1.24 appadiyirunthum, naan sareeraththil thariththiruppathu ungga'lukku athiga avasiyam.

Php1.25 intha nichchayaththaikko'ndirunthu, naan ma’rupadiyum ungga'lidaththil varugi’rathinaal ennaikku’riththu ungga'ludaiya magizhchchi ki’risthu iyeasuvukku'l perugumpadikku,

Php1.26 ungga'l visuvaasaththin varththanaikkaagavum santhoashaththukkaagavum naan pizhaiththu, ungga'l anaivaroadungkooda iruppeanen’ru a’rinthirukki’rean.

Php1.27 naan vanthu ungga'laik ka'ndaalum, naan varaamalirunthaalum, neengga'l orea aaviyilea u’ruthiyaay nin’ru, orea aaththumaavinaalea suviseashaththin visuvaasaththi’rkaagak koodappoaraadi, ethirkki’ravarga'laal on’rilum maru'laathirukki’reerga'len’ru ungga'laikku’riththu naan kea'lvippadumpadi, evvithaththilum neengga'l ki’risthuvin suviseashaththi’rkup paaththiraraaga maaththiram nadanthuko'l'lungga'l.

Php1.28 neengga'l maru'laathirukki’rathu avarga'l kettuppoagi’ratha’rkum, neengga'l iradchikkappadugi’ratha’rkum aththaadchiyaayirukki’rathu; ithuvum theavanudaiya seyalea.

Php1.29 eanenil ki’risthuvinidaththil visuvaasikki’ratha’rkumaaththiramalla, avar nimiththamaagap paadupadugi’ratha’rkum ungga'lukku aru'lappattirukki’rathu.

Php1.30 neengga'l ennidaththilea ka'ndathum enakku u'nden’ru ippozhuthu kea'lvippadugi’rathumaana poaraattamea ungga'lukkum u'ndu.

Php2.1 aathalaal ki’risthuvukku'l yaathoru aa’ruthalum, anbinaalea yaathoru thea’ruthalum, aaviyin yaathoru aikkiyamum, yaathoru urukkamaana padchamum irakkangga'lum u'ndaanaal,

Php2.2 neengga'l eaga sinthaiyum eaga anbumu'l'lavarga'laayirunthu, isaintha aaththumaakka'laay on’raiyea sinthiththu, en santhoashaththai ni’raivaakkungga'l.

Php2.3 on’raiyum vaathinaalaavathu vee'n perumaiyinaalaavathu seyyaamal, manaththaazhmaiyinaalea oruvaraiyoruvar thangga'lilum meanmaiyaanavarga'laaga e'n'nakkadaveerga'l.

Php2.4 avanavan thanakkaanavaiga'laiyalla, pi’rarukkaanavaiga'laiyum noakkuvaanaaga.

Php2.5 ki’risthu iyeasuviliruntha sinthaiyea ungga'lilum irukkakkadavathu;

Php2.6 avar theavanudaiya roobamaayirunthum, theavanukkuch samamaayiruppathaik ko'l'laiyaadina poru'laaga e'n'naamal,

Php2.7 thammaiththaamea ve’rumaiyaakki, adimaiyin roobameduththu, manushar saayalaanaar.

Php2.8 avar manusharoobamaayk kaa'nappattu, mara'napariyantham, athaavathu siluvaiyin mara'napariyanthamum keezhppadinthavaraagi, thammaiththaamea thaazhththinaar.

Php2.9 aathalaal theavan ellaavat’ri’rkum mealaaga avarai uyarththi,

Php2.10 iyeasuvin naamaththil vaanoar poothalaththoar boomiyin keezhaanoarudaiya muzhangkaal yaavum mudanggumpadikkum,

Php2.11 pithaavaagiya theavanukku magimaiyaaga iyeasuki’risthu karththaren’ru naavuga'l yaavum a’rikkaipa'n'numpadikkum, ellaa naamaththi’rkum mealaana naamaththai avarukkuth thantharu'linaar.

Php2.12 aathalaal, enakkup piriyamaanavarga'lea, neengga'l eppozhuthum keezhppadigi’rapadiyea, naan ungga'lukkuch sameebamaayirukkumpozhuthu maaththiramalla, naan thooramaayirukki’ra ippozhuthum, athiga bayaththoadum nadukkaththoadum ungga'l iradchippu ni’raivea’rap pirayaasappadungga'l.

Php2.13 eanenil theavanea thammudaiya thayavu'l'la siththaththinpadi viruppaththaiyum seygaiyaiyum ungga'lil u'ndaakkugi’ravaraayirukki’raar.

Php2.14 naan vee'naaga oadinathum vee'naagap pirayaasappattathumillaiyengi’ra magizhchchi ki’risthuvin naa'lil enakku u'ndaayiruppatha’rku, jeevavasanaththaip pidiththukko'ndu, ulagaththilea sudarga'laippoalap piragaasikki’ra neengga'l,

Php2.15 koa'nalum maa’rupaadumaana santhathiyin naduvilea kut’ramat’ravarga'lum kabadat’ravarga'lum, theavanudaiya maasat’ra pi'l'laiga'lumaayirukkumpadikku,

Php2.16 ellaavat’raiyum mu’rumu’ruppillaamalum tharkkippillaamalum seyyungga'l.

Php2.17 mealum, ungga'l visuvaasamaagiya baliyinmealum oozhiyaththinmealum naan vaarkkappattuppoanaalum, naan magizhnthu, ungga'lanaivaroadungkoodach santhoashappaduvean.

Php2.18 ithinimiththam neengga'lum magizhnthu, ennoadeakoodach santhoashappadungga'l.

Php2.19 an’riyum, naanum ungga'l seythiga'lai a’rinthu manaththea’ruthal adaiyumpadikkuch seekkiramaayth theemoaththeayuvai ungga'lidaththil anuppalaamen’ru karththaraagiya iyeasuvukku'l nambiyirukki’rean.

Php2.20 atheanen’raal, ungga'l kaariyangga'lai u'nmaiyaay visaarikki’ratha’rku ennaippoala manathu'l'lavan avanaiyan’ri vea’roruvanum ennidaththilillai.

Php2.21 mat’ravarga'lellaarum ki’risthu iyeasuvukkuriyavaiga'laith theadaamal, thangga'lukkuriyavaiga'laiyea theadugi’raarga'l.

Php2.22 thagappanukkup pi'l'lai oozhiyagnseyvathupoala, avan ennudaneakooda suviseashaththinimiththam oozhiyagnseythaanen’ru avanudaiya uththamaku'naththai a’rinthirukki’reerga'l.

Php2.23 aagaiyaal en kaariyangga'l innapadi nadakkum en’ru naan a’rinthavudanea avanai anuppalaamen’ru ninaiththirukki’rean.

Php2.24 an’riyum naanea seekkiraththil varuveanen’ru karththarukku'l nambikkaiyaayirukki’rean.

Php2.25 mealum, en sagoatharanum, udanvealaiyaa'lum, udanseavaganum, ungga'l sthaanaabathiyum, en ku’raichchalukku uthaviseythavanumaana eppaappiroatheeththuvai ungga'lidaththil anuppavea'ndumen’ru e'n'ninean.

Php2.26 avan ungga'l ellaarmealum vaagnchaiyu'l'lavanum, thaan viyaathippattathai neengga'l kea'lvippattathinaalea migavum viyaagulappadugi’ravanumaayirunthaan.

Php2.27 avan viyaathippattu mara'naththi’rkuch sameebamaayirunthathu meythaan. aagilum, theavan avanukku irangginaar; avanukku irangginathumallaamal, thukkaththinmeal thukkam enakku u'ndaagaathapadikku, enakkum irangginaar.

Php2.28 aagaiyaal neengga'l avanai ma’rupadiyum ka'ndu santhoashappadavum, en thukkang ku’raiyavum, avanai athiseekkiramaay anuppinean.

Php2.29 aanapadiyinaalea neengga'l karththarukku'l miguntha santhoashaththoadea avanai eat’rukko'ndu, ippadippattavarga'laik kanamaay e'n'nungga'l.

Php2.30 eanenil neengga'l enakkuch seyyavea'ndiya oozhiyaththilea ungga'l ku’raivai ni’raivaakkumpadikku, avan than piraa'nanaiyum e'n'naamal, ki’risthuvin oozhiyaththinimiththam mara'naththi’rkuch sameebamaayirunthaan.

Php3.1 mealum, en sagoathararea, karththarukku'l santhoashappadungga'l. ezhuthinavaiga'laiyea ezhuthuvathu enakku varuththamalla, athu ungga'lukku nalamaayirukkum.

Php3.2 naayga'lukku echcharikkaiyaayirungga'l, pollaatha vealaiyaadka'lukku echcharikkaiyaayirungga'l, sunnaththukkaararukku echcharikkaiyaayirungga'l.

Php3.3 eanenil, maamsaththinmeal nambikkaiyaayiraamal, aaviyinaalea theavanukku aaraathanaiseythu, ki’risthu iyeasuvukku'l meanmaipaaraattugi’ra naamea viruththaseathanamu'l'lavarga'l.

Php3.4 maamsaththinmeal nambikkai vaikkavea'ndumaanaal naanum vaikkalaam; vea’roruvan maamsaththinmeal nambikkaiyaayirukka ninaiththaal naan athigamaay appadich seyyalaam.

Php3.5 naan ettaam naa'lil viruththaseathanamadainthavan, israveal vamsaththaan, benyameen koaththiraththaan, ebireyaril pi’rantha ebireyan, niyaayappiramaa'naththinpadi pariseayan;

Php3.6 bakthivairaakkiyaththinpadi sabaiyaith thunbappaduththinavan, niyaayappiramaa'naththi’rkuriya neethiyinpadi kut’ragnsaattappadaathavan.

Php3.7 aagilum, enakku laabamaayirunthavaiga'levaiga'loa avaiga'laik ki’risthuvukkaaga nashdamen’ru e'n'ninean.

Php3.8 athumaaththiramalla, en karththaraagiya ki’risthu iyeasuvai a’rigi’ra a’rivin meanmaikkaaga ellaavat’raiyum nashdamen’ru e'n'nikko'ndirukki’rean.

Php3.9 naan ki’risthuvai aathaayappaduththikko'l'lumpadikkum, niyaayappiramaa'naththinaal varugi’ra suyaneethiyai udaiyavanaayiraamal, ki’risthuvaip pat’rum visuvaasaththinaal varugi’rathum visuvaasamoolamaayth theavanaal u'ndaayirukki’rathumaana neethiyai udaiyavanaayirunthu, ki’risthuvukku'l irukki’ravanen’ru kaa'nappadumpadikkum,

Php3.10 ippadi naan avaraiyum avarudaiya uyirththezhuthalin vallamaiyaiyum, avarudaiya paaduga'lin aikkiyaththaiyum a’rigi’ratha’rkum, avarudaiya mara'naththi’rkoppaana mara'naththi’rku'l'laagi, eppadiyaayinum naan mariththoarilirunthu uyiroadezhunthiruppatha’rkuth thaguthiyaagumpadikkum,

Php3.11 avarukkaaga ellaavat’raiyum nashdamen’ru vittean; kuppaiyumaaga e'n'nugi’rean.

Php3.12 naan adainthaayit’ru, allathu mut’rum thea’rinavanaanean en’ru e'n'naamal, ki’risthu iyeasuvinaal naan etha’rkaagap pidikkappatteanoa athai naan pidiththukko'l'lumpadi aasaiyaayth thodarugi’rean.

Php3.13 sagoathararea, athaip pidiththukko'ndeanen’ru naan e'n'nugi’rathillai; on’ru seygi’rean, pinnaanavaiga'lai ma’ranthu, munnaanavaiga'lai naadi,

Php3.14 ki’risthu iyeasuvukku'l theavan azhaiththa parama azhaippin panthayapporu'lukkaaga ilakkai noakkith thodarugi’rean.

Php3.15 aagaiyaal, nammil thea’rinavarga'l yaavarum inthach sinthaiyaayirukkakkadavoam; enthak kaariyaththilaavathu neengga'l vea’rea sinthaiyaayirunthaal, athaiyum theavan ungga'lukku ve'lippaduththuvaar.

Php3.16 aagilum naam ethuvaraiyil thea’riyirukki’roamoa, athumuthal orea ozhunggaay nadanthuko'ndu, orea sinthaiyaayiruppoamaaga.

Php3.17 sagoathararea, neengga'l ennoadeakoodap pinpat’rugi’ravarga'laagi, naangga'l ungga'lukku vazhikaattugi’rapadi nadakki’ravarga'lai maathiriyaaga noakkungga'l.

Php3.18 eanenil, aneagar vea’ruvithamaay nadakki’raarga'l; avarga'l ki’risthuvin siluvaikkup pagaignaren’ru ungga'lukku aneagantharam sonnean, ippozhuthu ka'n'neeroadum sollugi’rean.

Php3.19 avarga'ludaiya mudivu azhivu, avarga'ludaiya theavan vayi’ru, avarga'ludaiya magimai avarga'ludaiya ilachchaiyea, avarga'l boomikkaduththavaiga'laich sinthikki’raarga'l.

Php3.20 nammudaiya kudiyiruppoa paraloagaththilirukki’rathu, anggeayirunthu karththaraayirukki’ra iyeasuki’risthu ennum iradchagar vara ethirpaarththukko'ndirukki’roam.

Php3.21 avar ellaavat’raiyum thamakkuk keezhppaduththikko'l'laththakka thammudaiya vallamaiyaana seyalinpadiyea, nammudaiya a’rpamaana sareeraththaith thammudaiya magimaiyaana sareeraththi’rku oppaaga ma’ruroobappaduththuvaar.

Php4.1 aathalaal, enakkup piriyamum vaagnchaiyumaana sagoathararea, enakkuch santhoashamum kireedamumaanavarga'lea, piriyamaanavarga'lea, inthappadiyea karththarukku'l nilaiththirungga'l.

Php4.2 karththarukku'l orea sinthaiyaayirukka eyoathiyaa'lukkum sinthikeayaa'lukkum buththisollugi’rean.

Php4.3 an’riyum, en uththama koottaa'liyea, avarga'lukku uthaviyaayirukkumpadi unnaiyum vea'ndikko'l'lugi’rean; avarga'l kileamenthoadum mat’ra en udanvealaiyaadka'loadungkoodach suviseasha vishayaththil ennoadeakooda migavum pirayaasappattaarga'l, avarga'ludaiya naamangga'l jeevapusthagaththil irukki’rathu.

Php4.4 karththarukku'l eppozhuthum santhoashamaayirungga'l; santhoashamaayirungga'l en’ru ma’rupadiyum sollugi’rean.

Php4.5 ungga'l saanthaku'nam ellaa manusharukkum therinthiruppathaaga. karththar sameebamaayirukki’raar.

Php4.6 neengga'l on’rukkung kavalaippadaamal, ellaavat’raiyungku’riththu ungga'l vi'n'nappangga'lai sthoaththiraththoadea koodiya jebaththinaalum vea'nduthalinaalum theavanukkuth theriyappaduththungga'l.

Php4.7 appozhuthu, ellaap buththikkum mealaana theavasamaathaanam ungga'l iruthayangga'laiyum ungga'l sinthaiga'laiyum ki’risthu iyeasuvukku'l'laagak kaaththukko'l'lum.

Php4.8 kadaisiyaaga, sagoathararea, u'nmaiyu'l'lavaiga'levaiga'loa, ozhukkamu'l'lavaiga'levaiga'loa, neethiyu'l'lavaiga'levaiga'loa, ka’rpu'l'lavaiga'levaiga'loa, anbu'l'lavaiga'levaiga'loa, na’rkeerththiyu'l'lavaiga'levaiga'loa, pu'n'niyam ethuvoa, pugazh ethuvoa avaiga'laiyea sinthiththukko'ndirungga'l.

Php4.9 neengga'l ennidaththil kat’rum adainthum keattum ka'ndum irukki’ravaiga'levaiga'loa, avaiga'laiyea seyyungga'l; appozhuthu samaathaanaththin theavan ungga'loadiruppaar.

Php4.10 ennai visaarippatha’rku neengga'l ippozhuthu ma’rupadiyum manamalarnthapadiyinaalea karththarukku'l migavum santhoashappattean; ippadich seyya e'n'nangko'ndiruntheerga'l, samayammaaththiram ungga'lukku nearidavillai.

Php4.11 en ku’raichchalinaal naan ippadich sollugi’rathillai; eanenil naan entha nilaimaiyilirunthaalum manarammiyamaayirukkak kat’rukko'ndean.

Php4.12 thaazhnthirukkavum enakkuth theriyum, vaazhnthirukkavum enakkuth theriyum; evvidaththilum ellaavat’rilum thirupthiyaayirukkavum pattiniyaayirukkavum, paripoora'namadaiyavum ku’raivupadavum poathikkappattean.

Php4.13 ennaip belappaduththugi’ra ki’risthuvinaalea ellaavat’raiyugnseyya enakkup belanu'ndu.

Php4.14 aagilum neengga'l en ubaththiravaththil ennoadea udanpattathu nalamaayirukki’rathu.

Php4.15 mealum, pilippiyarea, suviseashaththin aarambaththilea naan makkethoaniyaavilirunthu pu’rappattapoathu, kodukkal vaanggal kaariyaththil neengga'lmaaththiram enakku udanpattatheayallaamal, vea’roru sabaiyum udanpadavillai en’ru neengga'l a’rinthirukki’reerga'l.

Php4.16 naan thesaloanikkeayilirunthapoathum, en ku’raichchalai neekkumpadi neengga'l ira'ndorutharam anuppineerga'l.

Php4.17 ubagaaraththai naan naadaamal, ungga'l ka'nakkukkup palan perugumpadiyea naadugi’rean.

Php4.18 ellaam enakkuk kidaiththathu, paripoora'namum u'ndaayirukki’rathu; ungga'laal anuppappattavaiga'laich sugantha vaasanaiyum theavanukkup piriyamaana ugantha baliyumaaga eppaappiroatheeththuvin kaiyil varappat’rikko'ndapadiyaal naan thirupthiyadainthirukki’rean.

Php4.19 en theavan thammudaiya aisuvariyaththinpadi ungga'l ku’raivaiyellaam ki’risthu iyeasuvukku'l magimaiyilea ni’raivaakkuvaar.

Php4.20 nammudaiya pithaavaagiya theavanaanavarukku en’ren’raikkum magimai u'ndaavathaaga. aamen.

Php4.21 ki’risthu iyeasuvukku'l'laana parisuththavaanga'l yaavarukkum vaazhththuthal sollungga'l. ennoadirukki’ra sagoathararga'l ungga'lukku vaazhththuthal sollugi’raarga'l.

Php4.22 parisuththavaanga'l anaivarum, viseashamaaga iraayanudaiya aramanaiyilu'l'lavarga'lum ungga'lukku vaazhththuthal sollugi’raarga'l.

Php4.23 namathu karththaraagiya iyeasuki’risthuvinudaiya kirubai ungga'lanaivaroadungkooda iruppathaaga. aamen.
