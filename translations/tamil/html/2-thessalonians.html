2Th1.1 pavulum, silvaanum, theemoaththeayum, nammudaiya pithaavaagiya theavanukku'l'lum, karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvukku'l'lum irukki'ra thesaloanikkeayar sabaikku ezhuthugi'rathaavathu:

2Th1.2 nammudaiya pithaavaagiya theavanaalum karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvinaalum ungga'lukkuk kirubaiyum samaathaanamum u'ndaavathaaga.

2Th1.3 sagoathararea, naangga'l eppozhuthum ungga'lukkaagath theavanai sthoaththirikkak kadanaa'liga'laayirukki'roam; ungga'l visuvaasam migavum perugugi'rapadiyinaalum, neengga'lellaarum oruvariloruvar vaiththirukki'ra anbu athigarikki'rapadiyinaalum, appadich seygi'rathu thaguthiyaayirukki'rathu.

2Th1.4 neengga'l sagikki'ra sagala thunbangga'lilum ubaththiravangga'lilum po'rumaiyaiyum visuvaasaththaiyum kaa'nbikki'rathinimiththam ungga'laikku'riththu naangga'l theavanudaiya sabaiga'lil meanmaipaaraattugi'roam.

2Th1.5 neengga'l theavanudaiya raajyaththinimiththam paadu anubavikki'ravarga'laayirukka, antha raajyaththi'rku neengga'l paaththiraren're'n'nappadumpadikku, theavan niyaayamaana theerppuchseygi'ravarenbatha'rku, athuvea aththaadchiyaayirukki'rathu.

2Th1.6 ungga'lai ubaththiravappaduththugi'ravarga'lukku ubaththiravaththaiyum, ubaththiravappadugi'ra ungga'lukku engga'loadeakooda i'laippaa'ruthalaiyum pirathipalanaagak koduppathu theavanukku neethiyaayirukki'rathea.

2Th1.7 theavanai a'riyaathavarga'lukkum, nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvin suviseashaththi'rkuk keezhppadiyaathavarga'lukkum neethiyu'l'la aakkinaiyaich seluththumpadikku,

2Th1.8 karththaraagiya iyeasu thamathu vallamaiyin thootharoadum, juvaaliththu erigi'ra akkiniyoadum, vaanaththilirunthu ve'lippadumpoathu appadiyaagum.

2Th1.9 annaa'lilea thammudaiya parisuththavaanga'lil magimaippadaththakkavaraayum, neengga'l engga'ludaiya saadchiyai visuvaasiththapadiyinaalea ungga'lidaththilum, visuvaasikki'ravarga'lellaaridaththilum aachchariyappadaththakkavaraayum, avar varumpoathu,

2Th1.10 avarga'l karththarudaiya sannithaanaththilirunthum, avarudaiya vallamai porunthiya magimaiyilirunthum neenggalaagi, niththiya azhivaagiya tha'ndanaiyai adaivaarga'l.

2Th1.11 aagaiyaal, nammudaiya theavanum karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvum a'likkum kirubaiyinpadiyea, nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvin naamam ungga'lidaththilum, neengga'l avaridaththilum magimaippadumporuttaaga;

2Th1.12 nammudaiya theavan ungga'laith thamathu azhaippukkup paaththiraraakkavum, thamathu thayaiyu'l'la siththam muzhuvathaiyum visuvaasaththin kiriyaiyaiyum balamaay ungga'lidaththil ni'raiveat'ravum vea'ndumen'ru, eppozhuthum ungga'lukkaaga vea'ndikko'l'lugi'roam.

2Th2.1 an'riyum, sagoathararea, nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvin varugaiyaiyum, naam avaridaththilea searkkappaduvathaiyungku'riththu, naangga'l ungga'lai vea'ndikko'l'lugi'rathu ennaven'raal,

2Th2.2 oru aaviyinaalaavathu, vaarththaiyinaalaavathu, engga'lidaththilirunthu vanthathaayth thoan'rugi'ra oru nirubaththinaalaavathu, ki'risthuvinudaiya naa'l sameebamaayirukki'rathaagach sollappattaal, udanea sagnchalappadaamalum kalanggaamalum irungga'l.

2Th2.3 evvithaththinaalum oruvanum ungga'lai moasampoakkaathapadikku echcharikkaiyaayirungga'l; eanenil visuvaasa thuroagam munthi nearittu, keattin maganaagiya paavamanushan ve'lippattaalozhiya, antha naa'l varaathu.

2Th2.4 avan ethirththuni'rki'ravanaayum, theavanennappaduvathethuvoa, aaraathikkappaduvathethuvoa, avaiyellaavat'ri'rkum mealaagath thannai uyarththugi'ravanaayum, theavanudaiya aalayaththil theavanpoala udkaarnthu, thannaiththaan theavanen'ru kaa'nbikki'ravanaayum iruppaan.

2Th2.5 naan ungga'lidaththilirunthapoathu ivaiga'laich sonnathu ungga'lukku gnaabagamillaiyaa?

2Th2.6 avan than kaalaththilea ve'lippadumpadikku, ippozhuthu avanaith thadaiseygi'rathu innathen'rum a'rinthirukki'reerga'lea.

2Th2.7 akkiramaththin iragasiyam ippozhuthea kiriyaiseygi'rathu; aanaalum thadaiseygi'ravan naduvilirunthu neekkappadumunnea athu ve'lippadaathu.

2Th2.8 neekkappadumpoathu, antha akkiramakkaaran ve'lippaduvaan; avanaik karththar thammudaiya vaayin suvaasaththinaalea azhiththu, thammudaiya varugaiyin pirasannaththinaalea naasampa'n'nuvaar.

2Th2.9 antha akkiramakkaaranudaiya varugai saaththaanudaiya seyalinpadi sagala vallamaiyoadum adaiyaa'langga'loadum poyyaana a'rputhangga'loadum,

2Th2.10 kettuppoagi'ravarga'lukku'l'lea aneethiyinaal u'ndaagum sagalavitha vagnchagaththoadum irukkum. iradchikkappadaththakkathaaych saththiyaththinmealu'l'la anbai avarga'l anggigariyaama'rpoanapadiyaal appadi nadakkum.

2Th2.11 aagaiyaal saththiyaththai visuvaasiyaamal aneethiyil piriyappadugi'ra yaavarum aakkinaikku'l'laakkappadumpadikku,

2Th2.12 avarga'l poyyai visuvaasikkaththakkathaagak kodiya vagnchagaththaith theavan avarga'lukku anuppuvaar.

2Th2.13 karththarukkup piriyamaana sagoathararea, neengga'l aaviyinaalea parisuththamaakkappadugi'rathinaalum, saththiyaththai visuvaasikki'rathinaalum iradchippadaiyumpadikku, aathimuthal theavan ungga'laith therinthuko'ndapadiyinaalea, naangga'l ungga'laikku'riththu eppozhuthum theavanai sthoaththirikkak kadanaa'liga'laayirukki'roam.

2Th2.14 neengga'l nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvin magimaiyai adaiyumporuttaaga engga'l suviseashaththinaalea antha iradchippukku avar ungga'lai azhaiththaar.

2Th2.15 aagaiyaal, sagoathararea, neengga'l nilaiko'ndu, vaarththaiyinaalaavathu nirubaththinaalaavathu naangga'l ungga'lukku ubatheasiththa mu'raimaiga'laik kaikko'l'lungga'l.

2Th2.16 nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvum, nammidaththil anbukoornthu niththiya aa'ruthalaiyum nalnambikkaiyaiyum kirubaiyaay namakkuk koduththirukki'ra nammudaiya pithaavaagiya theavanum,

2Th2.17 ungga'l iruthayangga'laith theat'ri, ellaa nalvasanaththilum na'rkiriyaiyilum ungga'lai sthirappaduththuvaaraaga.

2Th3.1 kadaisiyaaga, sagoathararea, ungga'lidaththil karththarudaiya vasanam parambi magimaippadugi'rathupoala, evvidaththilum parambi magimaippadumpadikkum,

2Th3.2 thurkku'naraagiya pollaatha manushar kaiyinin'ru naangga'l viduvikkappadumpadikkum, engga'lukkaaga vea'ndikko'l'lungga'l; visuvaasam ellaaridaththilumillaiyea.

2Th3.3 karththaroa u'nmaiyu'l'lavar, avar ungga'lai sthirappaduththi, theemaiyinin'ru vilakkik kaaththukko'l'luvaar.

2Th3.4 mealum, naangga'l katta'laiyidugi'ravaiga'lai neengga'l seythuvarugi'reerga'len'rum, inimealum seyveerga'len'rum, ungga'laikku'riththuk karththarukku'l nambikkaiyaayirukki'roam.

2Th3.5 karththar ungga'l iruthayangga'laith theavanaippat'rum anbukkum ki'risthuvin po'rumaikkum nearaay nadaththuvaaraaga.

2Th3.6 mealum, sagoathararea, engga'lidaththil eat'rukko'nda mu'raimaiyinpadi nadavaamal, ozhunggat'ru nadakki'ra enthach sagoatharanaiyum neengga'l vittu vilaga vea'ndumen'ru, nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvin naamaththinaalea, ungga'lukkuk katta'laiyidugi'roam.

2Th3.7 innavithamaay engga'laip pinpat'ra vea'ndumen'ru neengga'l a'rinthirukki'reerga'lea; naangga'l ungga'lukku'l'lea ozhunggat'ru nadavaamalum,

2Th3.8 oruvanidaththilum ilavasamaaych saappidaamalum, ungga'lil oruvanukkum baaramaayiraathapadikku iravum pagalum pirayaasaththoadum varuththaththoadum vealaiseythu saappittoam.

2Th3.9 ungga'lmeal baaraththai vaippatha'rkaana athigaaram engga'lukku illaiyenbathinaalea appadich seyyaamal, neengga'l engga'laip pinpat'rumpadikku naangga'l ungga'lukku maathiriyaayirukkavea'ndumen'rea appadich seythoam.

2Th3.10 oruvan vealaiseyya manathillaathirunthaal avan saappidavum koodaathen'ru naangga'l ungga'lidaththil irunthapoathu ungga'lukkuk katta'laiyittoamea.

2Th3.11 ungga'lil silar yaathoru vealaiyum seyyaamal, vee'n aluva'rkaararaay, ozhunggat'ruth thirigi'raarga'len'ru kea'lvippadugi'roam.

2Th3.12 ippadippattavarga'l amaithaloadea vealaiseythu, thangga'l sonthach saappaattaich saappidavea'ndumen'ru, nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvinaalea avarga'lukkuk katta'laiyittup buththisollugi'roam.

2Th3.13 sagoathararea, neengga'l nanmai seyvathilea soarnthupoagaamalirungga'l.

2Th3.14 mealum, intha nirubaththil solliya engga'l vasanaththukku oruvan keezhppadiyaama'rpoanaal, avanaik ku'riththukko'ndu, avan vedkappadumpadikku avanudanea kalavaathirungga'l.

2Th3.15 aanaalum avanaich saththuruvaaga e'n'naamal, sagoatharanaaga e'n'ni, avanukkup buththisollungga'l.

2Th3.16 samaathaanaththin karththarthaamea eppozhuthum sagalavithaththilum ungga'lukkuch samaathaanaththaith thantharu'luvaaraaga. karththar ungga'lanaivaroadungkooda iruppaaraaga.

2Th3.17 pavulaagiya naan en kaiyezhuththaalea ungga'lai vaazhththugi'rean, nirubangga'lthoa'rum ithuvea adaiyaa'lam; ippadiyea ezhuthugi'rean.

2Th3.18 nammudaiya karththaraagiya iyeasuki'risthuvin kirubai ungga'lanaivaroadungkooda iruppathaaga. aamen.
